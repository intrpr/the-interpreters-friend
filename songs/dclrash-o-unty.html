A declaration of unity - Alcoholic Anonymous

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 10 May 2006, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

WE OWE A-A HELP IMPROVE FUTURE. #ALL-OF-US HEALTHY MUST TOP-PRIORITY. OUR FELLOWSHIP UNITED MUST. WHY? LIFE FOR US AND FUTURE MEMBER DEPEND A-A UNITED.

Notes: #ALL-OF-US - [A], PO > NDS, FO up touches DS shoulder then [L^], PO > signer touches NDS shoulder.

TOP-PRIORITY - palm of DH [5] strikes index finger of [5], PO > signer, FO > DS.

FELLOWSHIP - (2h)[5], DH is held above NDH and and hands circle around each other.

UNITED - COOPERATE.

Lyrics:

This we owe to A.A's future: to place our common welfare first; to keep our fellowship united, for on A.A. unity depend our lives and the lives of those to come.
