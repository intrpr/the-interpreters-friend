Kah ribon

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

Refrain: GOD, YOU MASTER THIS WORLD AND EVERY POSSIBLE WORLD. YOURSELF KING CONTROL EVERY KING HERE EARTH. BEAUTIFUL ANNOUNCE YOUR ACTION POWERFUL, WONDERFUL, WHILE YOU WITNESS. [Repeat as first verse with refrain]

ALL-DAY~ALL-NIGHT ME PLAN BEST PRAISE. YOU GOD HOLY. YOURSELF MAKE (2h)#ALL LIVING THINGS: HOLY ANGEL, PEOPLE, ANIMAL DEPEND EARTH, BIRD FLY-AROUND SKY. Refrain

YOUR MIRACLE MIGHTY. PEOPLE ARROGANT WITNESS, GULP BECOME HUMBLE. PEOPLE OFF-POINT THINK STRAIGHT AGAIN. SUPPOSE MAN LIVE THOUSAND+ YEAR, FULL UNDERSTAND YOUR ACTION POWERFUL, CAN'T. Refrain

GOD WORTH HONOR RECOGNIZE GREAT. ME ASK YOU SAVE YOUR SHEEP, OUR ENEMY IDEA~LIKE LION. LONG-AGO ENEMY FORCE YOUR PEOPLE LEAVE ISRAEL. PLEASE BRING-#BACK-ctr YOUR FAVORITE NATION. Refrain

COME-#BACK-ctr YOUR HOLY PLACE. ENTER PLACE TOP HOLY IN TEMPLE. MEAN (<) SPIRIT, (>) SOUL, CELEBRATE CAN. SONG alt.EXPRESS, PRAISE YOU WILL. WHERE? JERUSALEM, CITY BEAUTIFUL. Refrain

Notes: YOU - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

WITNESS - [1] touches under eye while head moves down.

HOLY - H-CLEAN. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Holy - 1.

(2h)#ALL - (2h)[A], POs ><, FOs up, are held at shoulder height and move away while opening to (2h)[L].

FLY-AROUND - (2h)[1] cross fingers as in CONFLICT and move up and away while repeatedly curling fingers like flapping wings.

SKY - using the FT of the upturned palm, point > sky and sweep the hand > DS. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - sky.

MIRACLE - WONDERFUL(1x) WORK. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Miracle.

MIGHTY - (2h)[S], POs > back of signer, FOs up, are held at NDS and fists descend, then repeat at DS.

ARROGANT - (2h)[L:], POs ><, FOs away, are held at forehead and suddenly expand. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - brat.

GULP - [5:], PO > signer, FO up, closes at throat as if taking a big swallow in anticipatiohn of something bad.

OFF-POINT - DH [S], PO > NDS, flicks finger off to NDS.

IDEA~LIKE - a blending where IDEA has a [Y] instead of an [I] in anticipation of LIKE which is signed smoothly right afterwards.

BRING-#BACK-ctr - Both upturned palms are brought to singer and handshapes change to (2h)[C] and (2h)[K].

COME-#BACK-ctr - (2h)[B], POs > signer, FOs up, move > signer while changing to (2h)[C] and (2h)[K].

JERUSALEM (1) - [B with thumbs flush with other fingers but not folded into palm], touches mouth and then pronates.

Lyrics: (trans., The Complete Artscroll Siddur)

Refrain: O Creator, master of this world and all worlds, You are the King who reigns over kings - Your powerful and wondrous deeds it is beautiful to declare before you.

Praises shall I prepare morning and evening, to You, O Holy God, Who created all life: holy angels and sons of man, beasts of the field and birds of the sky.

Great are Your deeds and mighty, humbling the haughty and straightening the bent; even if man live thousands of years, he could not fathom the extent of Your powerful deeds.

God to Whom belongs honor and greatness, save Your sheep from the mouth of lions, and bring Your people out of its exile, the people that You chose from all the nations.

To Your Sanctuary return and to the Holy of Holies, the place where spirit and soul will rejoice and utter songs and praises - In Jerusalem, city of beauty.
