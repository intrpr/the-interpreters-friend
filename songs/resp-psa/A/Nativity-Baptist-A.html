Responsorial Psalm - The Nativity of Saint John the Baptist (A)

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 30 July 2008, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

LORD, YOU-rt ANALYZE ME. YOU-rt KNOW* ME.
ME SIT, ME STAND, YOU-rt KNOW*.
YOU-rt FAR-AWAY BUT STILL READ-MIND CAN.
ME TRAVEL, ME REST, YOU-rt ANALYZE.
EVERYTHING INSIDE-SELF ME, YOU-rt KNOW*. Response

TRUE-BIZ MY INSIDE-SELF, YOU-rt MAKE YOURSELF-rt.
WHILE STILL INSIDE-SELF MY MOTHER, YOU-rt WEAVE MY BODY.
ME THANK+-rt WHY? YOU-rt CREATE ME AWESOME.
YOUR-rt MIRACLE, ME CHERISH. Response

MY SOUL YOU-rt KNOW DEEP.
MY BODY HIDE INSIDE-SELF MOTHER,
BUT EVERY BONE MY SKELETON YOU-rt VISUALIZE.
E-V-E-N LONG-AGO BEFORE CREATE EARTH. Response

Notes: YOU

READ-MIND - (2h)[S], DHPO away, NDHPO > signer, FOs up. Hands are held at forehead level and fingers flick index fingers several times.s

INSIDE-SELF - The FT of [C] are held against the chest and stuff the FT of [O^] into the [C] twice.

TRUE-BIZ - TRUE + (2h)[S], POs down, FOs away, DH brushes from side to side across NDH wrist.

WEAVE - (2h)[5], POs down, NDH FO > DS, DH FO away, DH weaves as it moves forward, starting closer to signer's body and next to NDH.

CREATE - C-MAKE.

MIRACLE - GREAT WORK. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Miracle.

BONE - [V:], PO > NDS, FO away, strikes dorsal side (back hand) of [S], PO down, FO > DS.

SKELETON - (2h)[V:], POs > signer, cross at wrist held at upper chest and descend.

Lyrics (Psalm 139:1b-3, 13-14ab, 14c-15):

Response: I praise you, for I am wonderfully made.

O Lord, you have probed me, you know me:
you know when I sit and when I stand;
you understand my thoughts from afar.
My journeys and my rest you scrutinize,
with all my ways you are familiar. Response

Truly you have formed my inmost being;
you knit me in my mother’s womb.
I give you thanks that I am fearfully, wonderfully made;
wonderful are your works. Response

My soul also you knew full well;
nor was my frame unknown to you
When I was made in secret,
when I was fashioned in the depths of the earth. Response
