Responsorial Psalm - 12th Sunday in ordinary time (A)

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 6 May 2008, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

Response: LORD, YOU-rt LOVE ME SO-MUCH, PLEASE ANSWER.

ME PUT-UP-WITH INSULT FOR YOUR-rt S-A-K-E.
SHAME CAUSE MY FACE BLUSH.
MY BROTHERS REJECT ME.
MY CHILDREN REFUSE RECOGNIZE ME.
WHY? ME TOO-MUCH ENTHUSIASM FOR YOUR-rt HOUSE.
THOSE-lf CURSE YOU, ALSO CURSE ME. Response

LORD, ME PRAY TO YOU-rt.
PLEASE FAVOR ME, GOD.
KNOW-THAT YOU-rt KIND, WILLING ANSWER ME.
YOU-rt HELP-me"over time".
ANSWER ME, LORD. YOUR-rt KINDNESS OVERFLOW.
YOU-rt LOOK-DOWN-ON-me HAVE-MERCY-ON-me ME. Response

(2h)YOU PEOPLE FEEL LOWLY, LOOK-UP-TO GOD AND FEEL HAPPY.
IF (2h)YOU SEARCH-FOR GOD, PRAY YOUR HEART INSPIRED WILL.
WHY? THE-rt LORD PAY-ATTENTION PEOPLE POOR.
HIS-rt O-W-N PLACED-IN PRISON, HE-rt IGNORE NOT.
HEAVEN AND EARTH PRAISE HIM-rt SHOULD.
ALSO OCEAN AND EVERYTHING LIVE THEREABOUTS. Response

Notes: YOU - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

SO-MUCH - (2h)[5:], POs ><, FOs away, separate while "so much" is mouthed.

PUT-UP-WITH - PATIENT.

BLUSH - RED (2h)[O^] touch cheeks at bottom and move up face while opeing to (2H)[5].

REJECT - Hands start like the final position of STOP and DH slides > DS while pronating.

TOO-MUCH - (2h)[B^], touch FTs, then DH moves up in an arc towards the signer and away. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - more than.

CURSE - [5:], PO > singer, - held at mouth and moves > what - cursed while closing to [S], then fist is shaken at what is cursed.

FAVOR - middle finger of [open 8] taps chin.

KNOW-THAT - Starts like KNOW but twists and descends, looking almost like DON'T-KNOW except the hand twists down at the end.

KIND / KINDNESS - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs away, NDH is held close to heart and DH circles around it.

OVERFLOW - [5], PO down, FO away, covers top of [S], PO > DS, FO away, and FT wiggle as the hand moves away, like a cup overflowing. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - overflow.

HAVE-MERCY-ON-me - (2h)[open 8], POs > signer, hands move in vertical circles normal (perpendicular) to the chest. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Mercy - 2.

LOWLY - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, move downwards arcing towards the signer. This is the opposite of the sign ADVANCED. For an animated gif of ADVANCED, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Exalted.

PLACED-IN - [S], PO > NDS, FO away, places something under [B], PO down, FO > NDS.

Lyrics (Psalm 69:8-10, 14, 17, 33-35):

Response: Lord, in your great love, answer me.

For your sake I bear insult,
and shame covers my face.
I have become an outcast to my brothers,
a stranger to my children,
Because zeal for your house consumes me,
and the insults of those who blaspheme you fall upon me. Response

I pray to you, O LORD,
for the time of your favor, O God!
In your great kindness answer me
with your constant help.
Answer me, O LORD, for bounteous is your kindness;
in your great mercy turn toward me. Response

"See, you lowly ones, and be glad;
you who seek God, may your hearts revive!
For the LORD hears the poor,
and his own who are in bonds he spurns not.
Let the heavens and the earth praise him,
the seas and whatever moves in them!" Response
