Responsorial Psalm - 9th Sunday in ordinary time (A)

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 6 May 2008, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

Response: LORD, BECOME MY STONE OFFER SAFETY.

IN YOU-rt, LORD, ME HIDE CAN.
NOT FORCE ME EXPERIENCE SHAME.
YOUR-rt INNER-SENSE JUSTICE SAVE ME.
LOOK-DOWN-ON HAVE-MERCY-ON-me,
FORCE MY ENEMY ESCAPE"each" SOON. Response

BECOME IDEA-LIKE C-A-V-E ESCAPE, HIDE CAN.
BECOME FORTRESS PROTECT AND SAVE ME.
YOU-rt TRUE-BIZ MY FOUNDATION AND FORTRESS.
MEAN YOU-rt LEAD ME, SHOW WAY FOR S-A-K-E YOUR-rt NAME. Response

PLEASE LOOK-DOWN-ON-me, YOUR-rt FACE SHINE-DOWN-ON YOUR-rt SERVANT.
YOU-rt KIND, WILLING SAVE ME.
(2h)YOU-rt LOOK-FORWARD LORD HELP,
GO-AHEAD BRAVE, ACCEPT PEOPLE AGAINST (2h)YOU. Response

Notes: YOU - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

INNER-SENSE - [B^], PO > signer, taps above hip.

JUSTICE - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, tap FT while moving in a horizontal circle.

HAVE-MERCY-ON-me - (2h)[open 8], POs > signer, hands move in vertical circles normal (perpendicular) to the chest. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Mercy - 2.

IDEA-LIKE - A blending where IDEA has a [Y] instead of an [I] in anticipation of LIKE which is signed smoothly right afterwards.

FORTRESS - (2h)[S], POs down, FOs ><, strike the whole length of the forearms together like TABLE then DH is held under NDH and the arms strike again.

TRUE-BIZ - TRUE + (2h)[S], POs down, FOs away, DH brushes from side to side across NDH wrist.

FOUNDATION - NDH [S], PO > signer, FO > DS, is held out with arm parallel to the chest and DH [S], PO > signer, strikes hand from below and then strikes elbow from below.

FORTRESS - (2h)[S], POs down, FOs ><, strike the whole length of the forearms together like TABLE then DH is held under NDH and the arms strike again.

SHINE-DOWN-ON - [O^] is held high PO > signer; hand opens to [5^] while moving > signer.

KIND - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs away, NDH is held close to heart and DH circles around it.

GO-AHEAD - (2h)[B^ dot], POs ><, FOs away, move forward in a jerk.

Lyrics (Psalm 31:2-3, 3-4, 17, 25):

Response: Lord, be my rock of safety.

In you, O LORD, I take refuge;
let me never be put to shame.
In your justice rescue me,
incline your ear to me,
make haste to deliver me! Response

Be my rock of refuge,
a stronghold to give me safety.
You are my rock and my fortress;
for your name's sake you will lead and guide me. Response

Let your face shine upon your servant;
save me in your kindness.
Take courage and be stouthearted,
all you who hope in the LORD. Response
