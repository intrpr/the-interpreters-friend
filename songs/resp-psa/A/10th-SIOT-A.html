Responsorial Psalm - 10th Sunday in ordinary time (A)

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 6 May 2008, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

Response: ME SHOW"all" PEOPLE UPRIGHT, GOD HAVE POWER SAVE.

GOD, THE-rt LORD SPEAK AND SUMMON (2h)#ALL LIVE EARTH.
SUNRRISE-TO-SUNSET, HE-rt SAY (role play God)
ME REBUKE (2h)YOU NOT ENOUGH SACRIFICE? NOT-THAT.
ME SEE (2h)YOU ALWAYS BURN-TO-ASH SACRIFICE. Response

SUPPOSE ME HUNGRY, NOT-NEED INFORM"all".
EVERYTHING IN WORLD MINE, ME USE ANY TIME CAN.
THINK ME NEED BULL STRONG FOR FOOD?
THINK ME NEED GOAT BLOOD FOR DRINK? Response

BEST SACRIFICE "WHAT"? PRAISE GOD.
(2h)YOU SWEAR-OATH GOD SUPREME, DO-IT.
MEAN TIME HAPPEN AWFUL, GO-AHEAD SUMMON,
ME WILLING SAVE (2h)YOU AND ACCEPT GLORY. Response

Notes: ME - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

UPRIGHT - Elbow of [S], PO > NDS, FO up, is struck against the fingers of NDH [B] held like part of TABLE.

(2h)#ALL - (2h)[A], POs ><, FOs up, are held at shoulder height and move away while opening to (2h)[L].

SUNRRISE-TO-SUNSET - Sign SUNRISE while facing > rt (East) at a 45 degree angle, then twist > lf (West) at a 45 degree angle and sing SUNSET.

REBUKE - BAWL-OUT.

SACRIFICE - (2h)[B], POs up, lift up hands and move them towards God.

NOT-THAT - (2h)[5], POs away, FOs up, wave from side to side symmetrically.

BURN-TO-ASH - (2h)[5], POs > signer, FOs up, travel upwards with wg like flames + DISSOLVE + (2h)[5], POs down, FOs away, touch thumbs, follow a semi-cricular path and meet again at thumbs or index fingers.

BULL - (2h)[Y] hold thumbs to forehead and head dips down and then up like a bull threatening to charge.

SWEAR-OATH - NDH [B] is held up as if swearing and DH [B] is held palm down as if on Bible.

SUPREME - (2h)[A], FOs away, DH starts below NDH and arcs around it until it is on top.

GO-AHEAD - (2h)[B^ dot], POs ><, FOs away, move forward in a jerk.

GLORY - (2h)[5], POs ><, FOs away from signer, DH which is on top wiggles fingers while ascending. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Glory.

Lyrics (Psalm 50:1, 8, 12-13, 14-15):

God the LORD has spoken and summoned the earth,
from the rising of the sun to its setting.
"Not for your sacrifices do I rebuke you,
for your holocausts are before me always." Response

"If I were hungry, I would not tell you,
for mine are the world and its fullness.
Do I eat the flesh of strong bulls,
or is the blood of goats my drink?" Response

"Offer to God praise as your sacrifice
and fulfill your vows to the Most High;
Then call upon me in time of distress;
I will rescue you, and you shall glorify me." Response
