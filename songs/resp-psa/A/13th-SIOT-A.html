Responsorial Psalm - 13th Sunday in ordinary time (A)

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 15 July 2008, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

Response: LORD HIS-rt GOOD CHARACTER, ME SING ABOUT FOREVER.

LORD HIS-rt PROMISE, ME SING ABOUT FOREVER.
ME INFORM (2h)#ALL GENERATION WE TRUST YOU-rt CAN.
KNOW-THAT HE-rt FINISH SAY, MY KINDNESS SET-UP FOREVER.
HEAVEN PROOF WE TRUST YOU-rt CAN. Response

GOD BLESS THEM-ctr SO-MUCH THEY-ctr HAPPY SHOUT.
THEY-ctr ENJOY YOUR-rt FACE face-SHINY SHINE-DOWN, LORD.
YOUR NAME CAUSE THEM-ctr CELEBRATE ALL-DAY.
YOUR-rt JUSTICE MEAN THEY-ctr ADVANCED. Response

YOU-rt SPLENDOR THEIR-rt STRENGTH,
AND YOUR-rt FAVOR LIFT-UP OUR POWER.
KNOW-THAT LORD HIMSELF-rt OUR SHIELD.
OUR KING, JEWISH PEOPLE DECLARE HE-rt HOLY. Response

Notes: CHARACTER - C-PERSONALITY.

(2h)#ALL - (2h)[A], POs ><, FOs up, are held at shoulder height and move away while opening to (2h)[L].

KNOW-THAT - Starts like KNOW but twists and descends, looking almost like DON'T-KNOW except the hand twists down at the end.

KINDNESS - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs away, NDH is held close to heart and DH circles around it.

BLESS - (2h)[O^], POs ><, held at mouth. Hands are brought downwards while opening to (2h)[5]. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Bless.

SO-MUCH - (2h)[5:], POs ><, FOs away, separate while "so much" is mouthed.

SHOUT - (2h)[5:], POs > signer, FOs up, rise from mouth while twisting at wrists.

face-SHINY - (2h)[open 8], POs > face, hands twist as wrists while moving away from face.

SHINE-DOWN - [O^] is held high PO > signer; hands open to [5^] while moving > signer.

CAUSE - (2h)[S] are held at the chest as if holding a rope and move forward while opening to (2h)[5]. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - cause.

JUSTICE - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, tap FT while moving in a horizontal circle.

ADVANCED - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, move upwards arcing towards the signer. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Exalted.

SPLENDOR - Starts like the sign NAME, the NDH is left in place while the DH touches it with the [open 8] (as in the sign FEEL), then moves upwards while twisting the wrist.

FAVOR - PREFER.

LIFT-UP - (2h)[B], PO down are held high and swoop down while supinating and then rise up again as if helping something mature and the hands continue up and FTs are directed > Heaven.

SHIELD - [S] is held as if holding shield and FT of [1] traces a circle where the shield would be.

HOLY - H-CLEAN. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Holy - 1.

Lyrics (Psalm 89:2-3, 16-17, 18-19):

Response: For ever I will sing the goodness of the Lord.

The promises of the LORD I will sing forever,
through all generations my mouth shall proclaim your faithfulness.
For you have said, "My kindness is established forever;"
in heaven you have confirmed your faithfulness. Response

Blessed the people who know the joyful shout;
in the light of your countenance, O LORD, they walk.
At your name they rejoice all the day,
and through your justice they are exalted. Response

You are the splendor of their strength,
and by your favor our horn is exalted.
For to the LORD belongs our shield,
and the Holy One of Israel, our king. Response
