Notes: BOW-DOWN - (2h)[B], POs away, FOs up, are held like the final position of GIVE-UP, then bend these arms twice while bowing at waist once.

HE - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

BLESS - (2h)[O^], POs ><, held at mouth. Hands are brought downwards while opening to (2h)[5]. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Bless.

FAVOR - PREFER.

(2h)alt.GIFT - (2h)[X], POs ><, FOs up, alt. nod downwards at wrist to show that something is given as a gift.

IDEA-LIKE - A blending where IDEA has a [Y] instead of an [I] in anticipation of LIKE which is signed smoothly right afterwards.

VINE - Start like GROW and hands pronate (twist to POs away) and become (2h)[5:]. For a movei fo this sign, go to Dictionary of Sign (ASL) - vine.

SCADS-OF - (2h)[5:], POs down, FOs away, move forward to show many things.

KNOW-THAT - Starts like KNOW but twists and descends, looking almost like DON'T-KNOW except the hand twists down at the end.

TEMPLE - T-CHURCH.

REALLY-WANT - WANT but shaken back and forth to show intensity.

JERUSALEM - (1) [B with thumbs flush with other fingers but not folded into palm], touches mouth and then pronates or (2) - "J" CITY. For an animated gif of this sign, see Valley Bible Church - ASL dictionary: Jerusalem.

TAKE-OFF - Like DEVELOP but moves quickly and abruptly with a "po".

COVENANT - AGREE RELATIONSHIP-WITH-God.

(2h)#ALL - (2h)[A], POs ><, FOs up, are held at shoulder height and move away while opening to (2h)[L].

GLORY - (2h)[5], POs ><, FOs away from signer, DH which is on top wiggles fingers while ascending. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Glory.

HOLY - H-CLEAN. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Holy - 1.

ABRAHAM - [A] strikes between the thumb and forefinger of [B], like BOTHER, but a single movement. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Abraham (2).

JACOB - Could be fingerspelled or signed as a [J] across the chin.

ISAAC - [I] taps fingertip against throat.

Lyrics (Psalm 128:1-2, 3, 4-5):

Response: Blessed are those who fear the Lord and walk in his ways.

Blessed is
everyone who fears the LORD, who walks in his ways!
For you shall eat the fruit of your handiwork;
blessed shall you be, and favored. Response

Your wife shall be like a fruitful vine in the recesses of your home;
your children like olive plants around your table. Response

Behold, thus is the man blessed who fears the LORD.
The LORD bless you from Zion:
may you see the prosperity of Jerusalem
all the days of your life. Response

or

Lyrics (Psalm 105:1-2, 3-4, 5-6, 8-9):

Response: The Lord remembers his covenant for ever.

Give thanks to the LORD, invoke his name;
make known among the nations his deeds.
Sing to him, sing his praise,
proclaim all his wondrous deeds. Response

Glory in his holy name;
rejoice, O hearts that seek the LORD!
Look to the LORD in his strength;
constantly seek his face. Response

You descendants of Abraham, his servants,
sons of Jacob, his chosen ones!
He, the LORD, is our God;
throughout the earth his judgments prevail. Response

He remembers forever his covenant
which he made binding for a thousand generations
which he entered into with Abraham
and by his oath to Isaac. Response
