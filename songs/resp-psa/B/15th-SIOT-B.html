Responsorial Psalm - 15th Sunday in ordinary time (B)

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 28 June 2006, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

Response: LORD, SHOW-us US YOUR-rt KINDNESS. GIFT-TO-us US YOUR-rt SALVATION.

ME alt.HEAR WHAT GOD ANNOUNCE.
LORD HIMSELF-rt ANNOUNCE PEACE.
IF (2h)YOU BOW-DOWN-TO-rt, HE-rt SAVE WILL.
OUR LAND HAVE GLORY. Response

(<) KINDNESS, (>) TRUTH CAN MEET.
(<)JUSTICE, (>) PEACE WILL KISS.
EARTH CRACK OPEN-UP, TRUTH GROW*.
MANY-LOOK-TO HEAVEN, JUSTICE BECOME CLEAR. Response

BENEFIT WHERE FROM? LORD HIMSELF-rt alt.GIFT-TO"each".
FOOD OUR LAND INCREASE+ WILL.
JUSTICE ARRIVE BEFORE LORD.
FOR-FOR? PREPARE WORLD FOR HIM-rt. Response

Notes: KINDNESS - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs away, NDH is held close to heart and DH circles around it.

GIFT-TO-us - (2h)[X], POs ><, FOs up, hands tip back towards signer to show that something is given as a gift.

SALVATION - SAVE.

ME - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

GLORY - (2h)[5], POs><, FOs away from signer, DH which is on top wiggles fingers while ascending. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Glory.

JUSTICE - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, tap FT while moving in a horizontal circle.

CRACK - (2h)[B], DH, PO > NDS, FO away, moves in a wavy pattern to show a "crack" in NDH [B], PO up, FO away.

MANY-LOOK-TO - (2h)[4] move FT towards Heaven as if many eyes were looking to God.

alt.GIFT-TO"each" - (2h)[X], POs ><, FOs > the recipents, hands move together from NDS to DS.

PREPARE - PLAN.

Lyrics (Psalm 85:9-10, 11-12, 13-14):

Response: Lord, let us see your kindness, and grant us your salvation.

I will hear what God proclaims;
the LORD - for he proclaims peace.
Near indeed is his salvation to those who fear him,
glory dwelling in our land. Response

Kindness and truth shall meet;
justice and peace shall kiss.
Truth shall spring out of the earth,
and justice shall look down from heaven. Response

The LORD himself will give his benefits;
our land shall yield its increase.
Justice shall walk before him,
and prepare the way of his steps. Response
