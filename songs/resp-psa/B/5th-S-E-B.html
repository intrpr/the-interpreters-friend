Responsorial Psalm - 5th Sunday of Easter (B)

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 23 August 2006, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

Response: ME PRAISE YOU-rt LORD, WHILE PEOPLE SCADS-OF MANY-WATCH-me. THEY-rt YOUR PEOPLE.

or

ALLELUIA!

ME PAY-OFF WHILE PEOPLE BOWN-DOWN-TO HIM-rt WATCH-me.
PEOPLE HUMBLE WILL EAT AND SATISFIED.
THEY-rt SEEK THE-rt LORD, PRAISE HIM-rt.
YOUR(plural) HEART LIVE FOREVER WILL. Response

NORTH, SOUTH, EAST, WEST
REMEMBER THE-rt LORD WILL.
EVERY NATION COOPERATE APPROACH HIM-rt BOW-DOWN. Response

(2h)#ALL PEOPLE SLEEP IN EARTH
BOW-DOWN-TO HIM-rt WILL.
BEFORE BURIED DUST,
NOW ALL WORSHIP HIM-rt. Response

ME DEPEND THE-rt LORD KEEP SOUL ALIVE.
MY CHILDREN PASS-DOWN SERVE HIM-rt.
HIS-rt RIGHTEOUSNESS, THEY-rt COME ANNOUNCE
TO GENERATION NEW, DESCRIBE HIS-rt JUSTICE. Response

Notes: ME - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

SCADS-OF - (2h)[5:], POs down, FOs away, move forward to show many things.

MANY-WATCH-me - (2h)[4], FTs point to singers faces and hands circle orthogonal (perpendicular) to the signer's chest.

ALLELUIA! - CLAP-HANDS~CELEBRATE + open hands into (2h)[5] while lifting.

PAY-OFF - [open 8] runs middle finger up palm (> wrist), changes to a [B], PO down, and then moves back across palm.

APPROACH - (2h)[B], FOs up, DH PO away, ND is held in front of NDH and palm faces it, then both hands approach one another slightly.

(2h)#ALL - (2h)[A], POs ><, FOs up, are held at shoulder height and move away while opening to (2h)[L].

DUST - [C], PO away, FO up, moves from NDS to DS along dorsal (back hand) side of [B], PO down, FO > DS.

ALL - (2h)[B^], POs up, FOs ><, DH circles around NDH and lands on its palm. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Whole.

WORSHIP - NDH is clasped over DH [S] and hands circle perpendicular to the chest. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Worship.

RIGHTEOUSNESS - Elbow of DH [S] is placed firmly against the backhand of NDH [B], PO down.

JUSTICE - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, tap FT while moving in a horizontal circle.

Lyrics (Psalm 22:26-27, 28, 30, 31-32):

Response: I will praise you, Lord, in the assembly of your people.

or

Alleluia!

I will fulfill my vows before those who fear the LORD.
The lowly shall eat their fill;
they who seek the LORD shall praise him:
"May your hearts live forever!" Response

All the ends of the earth
shall remember and turn to the LORD;
all the families of the nations shall bow down before him. Response

To him alone shall bow down
all who sleep in the earth;
before him shall bend
all who go down into the dust. Response

And to him my soul shall live;
my descendants shall serve him.
Let the coming generation be told of the LORD
that they may proclaim to a people yet to be born
the justice he has shown. Response
