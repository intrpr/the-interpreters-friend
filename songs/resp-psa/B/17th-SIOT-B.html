Responsorial Psalm - 17th Sunday in ordinary time (B)

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 28 June 2006, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

Response: LORD HIMSELF-rt FEED HIS-rt PEOPLE.
EVERYTHING THEY-rt ASK HE-rt alt.GIFT-TO"each".

EVERYTHING YOUR-rt HANDS MAKE SHOULD (2h)THANK++-rt, LORD.
PEOPLE THEMSELVES-rt TRUST BLESS YOU-rt SHOULD.
GLORY YOUR-rt KINGDOM, THEY-rt CONVERSE.
PLUS ANNOUNCE YOU-rt MIGHTY. Response

EACH PERSON NEED HOPE, LOOK-UP-TO-rt YOU-rt,
APPROPRIATE TIME-PERIOD, YOU-rt FEED"each";
YOUR-rt HANDS READY HELP"each",
EACH LIVING THING, SATISFIED WILL. Response

EVERYTHING LORD DO, JUSTICE.
ALL HIS-rt WORK HOLY.
SUMMON LORD, HE CLOSER-AND-CLOSER.
UNDERSTAND, SUMMON HIM THROUGH TRUTH MUST. Response

Notes: THEY - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

alt.GIFT-TO"each" - (2h)[X], POs ><, FOs > the recipents, hands move together from NDS to DS.

BLESS - (2h)[O^], POs ><, held at mouth. Hands are brought downwards while opening to (2h)[5]. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Bless.

GLORY - (2h)[5], POs><, FOs away from signer, DH which is on top wiggles fingers while ascending. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Glory.

KINGDOM - KING + [5], PO down, FO away, circles horizontally. For an animated gif of this sign, see Valley Bible Church - ASL dictionary: Kingdom.

CONVERSE - (2h)[1], POs ><, FOs up, hands move to and for striking chin.

MIGHTY - (2h)[S], POs > back of signer, FOs up, are held at NDS and fists descend, then repeat at DS.

LOOK-UP-TO-rt - (2h)[V], point FTs up towards God.

TIME-PERIOD - [T], PO > NDS, FO up, travels in a vertical plane parallel to the NDH and makes contact with NDH [B], PO > DS, FO away. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Period - Time.

LIVING - LIVE+.

JUSTICE - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, tap FT while moving in a horizontal circle.

HOLY - H-CLEAN. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Holy - 1.

CLOSER-AND-CLOSER - Like the sign ARRIVE, but the DH moves in stops and starts > NDH.

Lyrics (Psalm 145:10-11, 15-16, 17-18):

Response: The hand of the Lord feeds us; he answers all our needs.

Let all your works give you thanks, O LORD,
and let your faithful ones bless you.
Let them discourse of the glory of your kingdom
and speak of your might.

The eyes of all look hopefully to you,
and you give them their food in due season;
you open your hand
and satisfy the desire of every living thing.

The LORD is just in all his ways
and holy in all his works.
The LORD is near to all who call upon him,
to all who call upon him in truth.
