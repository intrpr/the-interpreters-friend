Responsorial Psalm - 28th Sunday in ordinary time (B)

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 4 October 2006, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

Response: YOUR-rt LOVE, WE WANT FULL, LORD. THEN WE SING HAPPY CAN.

TEACH-us US REMEMBER OUR LIFE HAVE LIMIT.
MEAN OUR HEART BECOME WISE WILL.
WE REALLY-WANT #BACK-TOGETHER LORD.
HAVE-MERCY-ON-us US, YOUR SERVANT. Response

EVERDAY SUNRISE WANT FEEL KIND.
HAPPEN? SHOUT HAPPY, THRILLED FROM-NOW-ON UNTIL DEATH.
MEAN BEFORE-NOW YOU-rt ALLOW US SUFFER,
TIME-PERIOD WE SEE BAD HAPPEN, WORTH HAPPY NOW. Response

HELP-us US, YOUR-rt SERVANT, SEE YOUR-rt WORK.
HELP-us YOUR-rt CHILDREN SEE YOUR-rt GLORY.
REALLY-WANT LORD OUR GOD SEND GRACE TAKE-CARE-OF US.
||: HELP-us OUR WORK SUCCEED TAKE-OFF. :|| Response

Notes: REALLY-WANT - WANT but shaken back and forth to show intensity.

#BACK-TOGETHER - (2h)[B], POs ><, FOs up, are held at respective sides. The hands transition to a C and then a K while rushing towards each other and contacting.

HAVE-MERCY-ON-us - (2h)[open 8], POs > signer, hands move in vertical circles normal (perpendicular) to the chest. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Mercy - 2.

KIND - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs away, NDH is held close to heart and DH circles around it.

SHOUT - (2h)[5:], POs > signer, FOs up, rise from mouth while twisting at wrists.

FROM-NOW-ON - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs away, DHFT touches knuckle side of NDHFT and moves away in a downward arc. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - from now on.

BEFORE-NOW - [B], PO over shoulder, FO up, hands nods over shoulder to show previous time.

YOU - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

TIME-PERIOD - [T], PO > NDS, FO up, travels in a vertical plane parallel to the NDH and makes contact with NDH [B], PO > DS, FO away. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Period - Time.

GLORY - (2h)[5], POs><, FOs away from signer, DH which is on top wiggles fingers while ascending. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Glory.

REALLY-WANT - WANT but shaken back and forth to show intensity.

GRACE - [O^] or [S], held high and PO > Heaven, supinates and opens to [5] while being brought towards head. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Grace.

The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

TAKE-OFF - Like DEVELOP but moves quickly and abruptly with a "po".

ALLELUIA!-lf ALLELUIA!-rt ALLELUIA!-rt - Twist > lf and sign Alleluia! (CLAP-HANDS~CELEBRATE + open hands into (2h)[5] while lifting), twist > rt and sign Alleluia!, finally come back to center and sign Alleluia!

BLESS - (2h)[O^], POs ><, held at mouth. Hands are brought downwards while opening to (2h)[5]. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Bless.

KINGDOM - KING + [5], PO down, FO away, circles horizontally. For an animated gif of this sign, see Valley Bible Church - ASL dictionary: Kingdom.

Lyrics (Psalm 90:12-13, 14-15, 16-17):

Response: Fill us with your love, O Lord, and we will sing for joy!

Teach us to number our days aright,
that we may gain wisdom of heart.
Return, O LORD! How long?
Have pity on your servants! Response

Fill us at daybreak with your kindness,
that we may shout for joy and gladness all our days.
Make us glad, for the days when you afflicted us,
for the years when we saw evil. Response

Let your work be seen by your servants
and your glory by their children;
and may the gracious care of the LORD our God be ours;
prosper the work of our hands for us!
Prosper the work of our hands! Response
