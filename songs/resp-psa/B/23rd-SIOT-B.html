Response: MY SOUL SHOULD PRAISE LORD. or ALLELUIA!

J-A-C-O-B HIS-rt GOD, WE TRUST FOREVER.
WHY? PEOPLE OPPRESSED, HE-rt OPPRESSION-OFF.
PEOPLE HUNGRY, HE-rt FEED"each".
PRISONER, THE-rt LORD HELP FREE. Response

PEOPLE BLIND, HE-rt CAUSE-lf SEE.
PEOPLE LOWLY, HE-rt RAISE-UP-rt.
PEOPLE JUST, LORD LOVE THEM-lf.
PEOPLE FOREIGN AWKWARD, LORD PROTECT-rt. Response

(<) FATHER DIE, (>) HUSBAND GONE, LORD SUPPORT+ (2h)YOU.
BUT THEY-lf WICKED, GOD OVERTURN-lf.
THE-rt LORD RULE FOREVER WILL.
TEMPLE MOUNTAIN, YOUR-rt GOD CONTINUE PASS-DOWN'bit by bit', ALLELUIA! Response

Notes: OPPRESSION-OFF - Starts like PRESSURE, DH [B], PO down, FO away, push down slightly on [S], PO > DS, FO away, then the DH is brought upwards suddenly and the signer mouths "off".

HE - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

CAUSE - (2h)[S] are held at the chest as if holding a rope and move forward while opening to (2h)[5]. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - cause.

LOWLY - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, move downwards arcing towards the signer. This is the opposite of the sign ADVANCED. For an animated gif of ADVANCED, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Exalted.

RAISE-UP - (2h)[B], POs down, FOs away, lower slightly, supinate, and rise up.

JUST - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, tap FT while moving in a horizontal circle.

GONE - [5^], PO > face, FO up, moves downwards and closes to a [O^], while the NDH is a [B], PO > singer, FO > DS held further away and in neutral space.

WICKED - (2h)[3], wg while moving repeatedly > forehead. For an animated gif of this sign, see Valley Bible Church - ASL dictionary: Satan.

OVERTURN - (2h)[5:], FOs away, DH is held high, PO down at its side and NDH is held low, PO up at its side. Both hands interchange PO and levels while moving slightly > DS.

PASS-DOWN'bit by bit' - (2h)[O^], circle around each other from the shoulder and moving forward, but the action is stop and go.

Lyrics (Psalm 146:7, 8-9, 9-10):

Response: Praise the Lord, my soul! or: Alleluia.

The God of Jacob keeps faith forever,
secures justice for the oppressed,
gives food to the hungry.
The LORD sets captives free. Response

The LORD gives sight to the blind;
the LORD raises up those who were bowed down.
The LORD loves the just;
the LORD protects strangers. Response

The fatherless and the widow the LORD sustains,
but the way of the wicked he thwarts.
The LORD shall reign forever;
your God, O Zion, through all generations. Alleluia. Response
