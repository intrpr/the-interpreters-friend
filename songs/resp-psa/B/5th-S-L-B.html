Responsorial Psalm - Fifth Sunday of Lent (B)

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

Response: CREATE HEART PURE FOR ME, GOD.

HAVE-MERCY-ON-me ME, GOD, BECAUSE YOU-rt KIND.
YOUR-rt COMPASSION GREAT, GO-AHEAD PARDON MY INSULT.
MY GUILT WASH AWAY COMPLETE.
CLEAN++ MY SIN. Response

CREATE HEART PURE FOR ME, GOD.
MAKE MY SPIRIT NEW, UPRIGHT.
PLEASE DON'T LEAVE-me ALONE.
DON'T REMOVE YOUR-rt HOLY SPIRIT FROM ME. Response

HELP ME AGAIN JUMP-FOR-JOY YOUR-rt SALVATION,
AND TEACH MY SPIRIT WILLING LIKE BEFORE.
PEOPLE OFF-PATH-lf&rt, ME TEACH YOUR-rt WAYS.
SINNER #BACK-TO-rt YOU-rt WILL. Response

Notes: CREATE - C-MAKE.

PURE - Like NICE but the DH moves backwards slightly on the palm, pauses, then moves forward rapidly to show intensity.

ME - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

HAVE-MERCY-ON-me, us - (2h)[open 8], POs > signer, hands move in vertical circles normal (perpendicular) to the chest. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Mercy - 2.

KIND - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs away, NDH is held close to heart and DH circles around it.

COMPASSION - HEART + (2h)[5^] descend slightly twice while closing to (2h)[O^] but hands close to heart.

GO-AHEAD - (2h)[B^ dot], POs ><, FOs away, move forward in a jerk.

PARDON - Sign EXCUSE, switch dominance and sign EXCUSE again.

AWAY - (2h)[B], POs down, FOs away, nod hands down, then up, with a movement of the arms to show that something is being swept away.

SIN - (2h)[X] touch their respective sides of the mouth, descend to neutral space and circle antisymmetrically parallel to the chest. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Sin.

UPRIGHT - Elbow of [S], PO > NDS, FO up, is struck against the fingers of NDH [B] held like part of TABLE.

DON'T - (2h)[B], POs down, cross at wrists, then uncross moving horizontally and emphatically. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - don't.

LEAVE-me - FTs of (2h)[5^], POs > signer, poke chest.

HOLY - H-CLEAN. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Holy - 1.

JUMP-FOR-JOY - Start with the sign STAND and repeatedly and rapidly raise and lower the DH while curling the FT to show a person jumping.

SALVATION - S-FREE.

OFF-PATH-lf&rt - DH [S], PO > NDS, flicks finger > NDS from off [1], PO > NDS, FO up. Then DH flicks > DS.

#BACK-TO-rt - (2h)[B], POs > rt, FOs up; the hands transition to a C and then a K while rushing towards rt.

Lyrics (Psalm 51:3-4, 12-13, 14-15):

Response: Create a clean heart in me, O God.

Have mercy on me, O God, in your goodness;
in the greatness of your compassion wipe out my offense.
Thoroughly wash me from my guilt
and of my sin cleanse me. Response

A clean heart create for me, O God,
and a steadfast spirit renew within me.
Cast me not out from your presence,
and your Holy Spirit take not from me. Response

Give me back the joy of your salvation,
and a willing spirit sustain in me.
I will teach transgressors your ways,
and sinners shall return to you. Response
