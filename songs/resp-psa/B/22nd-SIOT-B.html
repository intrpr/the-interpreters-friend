Response: IF PERSON HE-ctr DO JUSTICE, LORD LIVE WITH HIM-ctr.

MEAN HE-ctr LIVE WITHOUT REASON BLAME-ctr, TEND JUSTICE.
HIS-ctr HEART THINK-ABOUT TRUTH.
HE-ctr GOSSIP NEGATIVE NEVER. Response

HE-ctr CAREFUL HURT NONE,
PLUS NEVER SPEAK NEGATIVE ABOUT HIS-ctr NEIGHBOR;
IF PERSON EVIL, HE DISDAIN-lf.
PEOPLE THEY-rt BOW-DOWN LORD, HE-ctr alt.HONOR-rt. Response

HE-ctr LEND MONEY HIGH INTEREST NOT.

WORLD BOTHER++-ctr CAN'T. Response

Notes: HE-ctr - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

JUSTICE - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, tap FT while moving in a horizontal circle.

THINK-ABOUT - [1] circles temple.

EVIL - (2h)[3], wg while moving repeatedly > forehead. For an animated gif of this sign, see Valley Bible Church - ASL dictionary: Satan.

DISDAIN-lf - [S], PO > NDS, FO up, is held at nose, then moves slightly > DS, then is thrown forward while opening to a [5]. If the signer stands in for the person disregarded, the hand is thrown against the chest.

BOW-DOWN - (2h)[B], POs away, FOs up, are held like the final position of GIVE-UP, then bend these arms twice while bowing at waist once.

INTEREST - [I], PO > NDS, FO away, rubs in a circle on the upturned palm of the NDH.

ctr-SAY-#NO!-TO-me - [3], PO > face, FO up, closes rapidly and once in front of face.

Lyrics (Psalm 15:2-3, 3-4, 4-5):

Response: One who does justice will live in the presence of the Lord.

Whoever walks blamelessly and does justice;
who thinks the truth in his heart
and slanders not with his tongue. Response

Who harms not his fellow man,
nor takes up a reproach against his neighbor;
by whom the reprobate is despised,
while he honors those who fear the LORD. Response

Who lends not his money at usury
and accepts no bribe against the innocent.
Whoever does these things
shall never be disturbed. Response
