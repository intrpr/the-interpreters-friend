Responsorial Psalm - 14th Sunday in Ordinary Time (C)

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 19 June 2007, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

Response: (2h)#ALL LIVE EARTH SHOULD SHOUT JOYFUL TO GOD.

(2h)#ALL EARTH COME-ON, SHOUT JOY TO GOD.
GLORY HIS-rt NAME, SING PRAISE.
PRAISE HIS-rt GLORY ANNOUNCE.
TELL-rt GOD, (role play to rt) YOUR ACTION AWESOME. (end role play) Response

(back to role play) (2h)#ALL EARTH SHOULD WORSHIP AND SING PRAISE YOU-rt.
YOUR NAME SING PRAISE. (end role play)
COME-ON SEE GOD HIS-rt WORK.
HIS ACTION AWESOME WITH ADAM HIS-ctr CHILDREN. Response

SEA, HE-rt CHANGE BECOME LAND DRY.
JEWISH PEOPLE WALK THROUGH RIVER CAN.
THEREFORE WE SHOULD CELEBRATE HIM-rt.
HE-rt RULE MIGHTY FOREVER. Response

(2h)#ALL BOW-DOWN GOD ATTEND-TO-me WHILE ME ANNOUNCE
FAVOR HE-rt SHOW-me ME.
BLESS GOD, HIMSELF-rt REFUSE ANSWER
PRAYER OR SHOW-me KINDNESS NOT. Response

Notes: (2h)#ALL - (2h)[A], POs ><, FOs up, are held at shoulder height and move away while opening to (2h)[L].

JOYFUL - (1) alt.HAPPY. (2) EXCITED. (3) J-EXCITED.

COME-ON - (2h)[B^] move in circles pivoting at the wrists to beckon someone to do something.

GLORY - (2h)[5], POs><, FOs away from signer, DH which is on top wiggles fingers while ascending. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Glory.

ACTION - (2h)[5:], POs down, FOs away, circle symmetrically in a plane parallel to the chest.

AWESOME - (2h)[S], POs ><, FOs up are held at eyes with awed facial expression and the hands open and then close.

WORSHIP - NDH is clasped over DH [S] and hands circle perpendicular to the chest. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Worship.

ADAM - [A] taps DS temple with thumb tip 2x.

THEREFORE - [bO] pokes a triangle of dots in the air like the mathematical symbol for "therefore".

RULE - CONTROL.

MIGHTY - (2h)[S], POs > back of signer, FOs up, are held at NDS and fists descend, then repeat at DS.

BOW-DOWN - (2h)[B], POs away, FOs up, are held like the final position of GIVE-UP, then bend these arms twice while bowing at waist once.

ATTEND-TO-me - PAY-ATTENTION.

FAVOR - PREFER.

BLESS - (2h)[O^], POs ><, held at mouth. Hands are brought downwards while opening to (2h)[5]. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Bless.

KINDNESS - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs away, NDH is held close to heart and DH circles around it.

Lyrics (Psalm 66:1-3, 4-5, 6-7, 16, 20):

Response: Let all the earth cry out to God with joy.

Shout joyfully to God, all the earth,
sing praise to the glory of his name;
proclaim his glorious praise.
Say to God, “How tremendous are your deeds!” Response

"Let all on earth worship and sing praise to you,
sing praise to your name!"
Come and see the works of God,
his tremendous deeds among the children of Adam. Response

He has changed the sea into dry land;
through the river they passed on foot;
therefore let us rejoice in him.
He rules by his might forever. Response

Hear now, all you who fear God, while I declare
what he has done for me.
Blessed be God who refused me not
my prayer or his kindness! Response
