Responsorial Psalm - 23rd Sunday in Ordinary Time (C)

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 11 July 2007, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

Response: EACH GENERATION, LORD, YOU-rt OUR PLACE SAFE.

or

ALLELUIA!

YOU-rt CAUSE PEOPLE BECOME DUST.
YOU-rt SAY, (role play) TIME COME-#BACK-TO ME. (end role play)
YOU-rt SEE ONE THOUSAND YEAR STEP-BY-STEP,
NOTHING-TO-IT, LIKE ONE-DAY GONE.
LIKE SOLDIER WATCH OVER-NIGHT. Response

THEY-rt LIVE IDEA~LIKE SLEEP.
THEY-rt GROW-UP LIKE GRASS SOON GONE.
MORNING GRASS GROW FRESH, STRONG.
EVENING ARRIVE, CUT-A-SHEATH, DISSOLVE. Response

TEACH-us US REMEMBER OUR LIFE HAVE LIMIT.
MEAN OUR HEART BECOME WISE WILL.
WE REALLY-WANT #BACK-TOGETHER LORD.
HAVE-MERCY-ON-us US, YOUR SERVANT. Response

EVERDAY SUNRISE WANT FEEL KIND. HAPPEN?
SHOUT HAPPY, THRILLED FROM-NOW-ON UNTIL DEATH.
REALLY-WANT LORD OUR GOD SEND GRACE TAKE-CARE-OF US.
HELP-us OUR WORK SUCCEED TAKE-OFF.
YES, HELP-us OUR WORK SUCCEED TAKE-OFF. Response

Notes: YOU - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

ALLELUIA! - CLAP-HANDS~CELEBRATE + open hands into (2h)[5] while lifting.

CAUSE - (2h)[S] are held at the chest as if holding a rope and move forward while opening to (2h)[5]. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - cause.

DUST - [C], PO away, FO up, moves from NDS to DS along dorsal (back hand) side of [B], PO down, FO > DS.

COME-#BACK-TO - (2h)[B], POs > signer, FOs up, move > signer while changing to (2h)[C] and then (2h)[K].

STEP-BY-STEP - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs away, DH is held ahead of NDH, then NDH leapfrogs over DH, and DH over NDH. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - procedure.

NOTHING-TO-IT - (2h)[F], POs away, FOs up, hands move slightly and symetrically from side to side.

IDEA~LIKE - A blending where IDEA has a [Y] instead of an [I] in anticipation of LIKE which is signed smoothly right afterwards.

FRESH - (2h)[5], PO > face, hands move like FRIENDLY but fingers don't wg.

CUT-A-SHEATH - [S] holds an imaginary handful of grain, and [B], PO up, FO away, slices through the grain below the NDH.

REALLY-WANT - WANT but shaken back and forth to show intensity.

#BACK-TOGETHER - (2h)[B], POs ><, FOs up, are held at respective sides. The hands transition to a C and then a K while rushing towards each other and contacting.

HAVE-MERCY-ON-us - (2h)[open 8], POs > signer, hands move in vertical circles normal (perpendicular) to the chest. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Mercy - 2.

KIND - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs away, NDH is held close to heart and DH circles around it.

SHOUT - (2h)[5:], POs > signer, FOs up, rise from mouth while twisting at wrists.

FROM-NOW-ON - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs away, DHFT touches knuckle side of NDHFT and moves away in a downward arc. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - from now on.

UNTIL - TO.

GRACE - [O^] or [S], held high and PO > Heaven, supinates and opens to [5] while being brought towards head. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Grace.

TAKE-OFF - Like DEVELOP but moves quickly and abruptly with a "po".

Lyrics (Psalm 90:3-4, 5-6, 12-13, 14-17):

Response: In every age, O Lord, you have been our refuge.

or

Alleluia!

You turn man back to dust,
saying, "Return, O children of men."
For a thousand years in your sight
are as yesterday, now that it is past,
or as a watch of the night. Response

You make an end of them in their sleep;
the next morning they are like the changing grass,
Which at dawn springs up anew,
but by evening wilts and fades. Response

Teach us to number our days aright,
that we may gain wisdom of heart.
Return, O LORD! How long?
Have pity on your servants! Response

Fill us at daybreak with your kindness,
that we may shout for joy and gladness all our days.
And may the gracious care of the LORD our God be ours;
prosper the work of our hands for us!
Prosper the work of our hands! Response
