Responsorial Psalm - Holy Thursday, Chrism Mass (C)

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 23 February 2008, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

Response: FOREVER ME SING ABOUT THE-rt LORD HIMSELF GOOD.

(role play God) ME FIND KING-DAVID, MY SERVANT.
USE MY HOLY O-I-L, ME ANOINT HIM-lf.
MEAN MY HAND ALWAYS WITH HIM-lf
AND MY ARM WILL MAKE HIM-lf STRONG. (end role play) Response

(role play God) ME WORTH TRUST HAVE-MERCY-ON HIM-lf.
MY NAME WILL MAKE HIS-lf POWER INCREASE LIKE CURLED-HORN CAN PIERCE.
KING-DAVID TELL-me (role play David) YOU-rt MY HEAVENLY-FATHER,
MY GOD, MY FOUNDATION, MY SAVIOR! (end role play) Response

Notes: ME - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

KING-DAVID - D-KING.

SERVANT - SERVE AGENT.

HOLY - H-CLEAN. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Holy - 1.

ANOINT - [A dot] circles head with DH thumb pointing down, then repeats at lower left to show that King David is the object of the anointing.

WORTH - IMPORTANT.

HAVE-MERCY-ON

CURLED-HORN - (2h)[S] touch thumbsides to temples and spiral upwards.

PIERCE - [1], PO up, FO away, pierces between index and middle fingers of [modified B], PO > signer, FO > DS. The middle and index fingers are separated before being pierced.

HEAVENLY-FATHER - (2h)[A], FOs up, DH is held against forehead, NDH close to but lower, both hands open to (2h)[5] while moving upwards. HEAVEN may be added to this. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Heavenly Father - 1.

FOUNDATION - NDH [S], PO > signer, FO > DS, is held out with arm parallel to the chest and DH [S], PO > signer, strikes hand from below and then strikes elbow from below.

SAVIOR - S-FREE AGENT.
