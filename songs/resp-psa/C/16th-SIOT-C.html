(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

Response: PERSON DO JUSTICE MEAN LIVE WITH THE-rt LORD WILL.

HE-ctr LIVE WITHOUT REASON BLAME-ctr, TEND RIGHTEOUS.
HIS-ctr HEART THINK-ABOUT TRUTH.
HE-ctr GOSSIP NEGATIVE NEVER. Response

HE-ctr CAREFUL HURT NONE,
PLUS NEVER SPEAK NEGATIVE ABOUT HIS-ctr NEIGHBOR;
IF PERSON EVIL, HE-ctr DISDAIN.
PEOPLE THEY-rt BOW-DOWN LORD, HE-ctr alt.HONOR-rt. Response

HE-ctr LEND MONEY HIGH INTEREST NOT.
PEOPLE TRY BRIBE-ctr LIE, ctr-SAY-#NO!-TO-me.
ANYONE LIKE THAT, LORD PROTECT.
WORLD BOTHER++-ctr CAN'T. Response

Notes: THE - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

RIGHTEOUS - Elbow of DH [S] is placed firmly against the backhand of NDH [B], PO down.

EVIL - (2h)[3], wg while moving repeatedly > forehead. For an animated gif of this sign, see Valley Bible Church - ASL dictionary: Satan.

DISDAIN - [S], PO > NDS, FO up, is held at nose, then moves slightly > DS, then is thrown forward while opening to a [5].

BOW-DOWN - (2h)[B], POs away, FOs up, are held like the final position of GIVE-UP, then bend these arms twice while bowing at waist once.

INTEREST - [I], PO > NDS, FO away, circles on palm of NDH.

ANYONE - [A dot], PO > NDS, FO up, twists rapidly at the wrist while moving in a horizontal circle.

Lyrics (Psalm 15:2-3, 3-4, 5):

Response: He who does justice will live in the presence of the Lord.

One who walks blamelessly and does justice;
who thinks the truth in his heart
and slanders not with his tongue. Response

Who harms not his fellow man,
nor takes up a reproach against his neighbor;
by whom the reprobate is despised,
while he honors those who fear the LORD. Response

Who lends not his money at usury
and accepts no bribe against the innocent.
One who does these things
shall never be disturbed. Response
