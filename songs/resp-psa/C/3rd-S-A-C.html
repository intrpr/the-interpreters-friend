Responsorial Psalm - 3rd Sunday of Advent (C)

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 29 November 2006, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

Response: GO-AHEAD SHOUT HAPPY, THRILLED. WHY? MESSIAH GREAT, HOLY ARRIVE FINISH.

GOD TRUE-BIZ MY SAVIOR.
ME CONFIDENT, NOT NEED AFRAID.
WHY FEEL STRONG, HAVE COURAGE?
LORD HE-rt MY SAVIOR.
SALVATION IDEA~LIKE PLACE WATER FLOW.
BRING HAPPINESS TO US LIKE WATER. Response

YOU"all" SHOULD THANK THE-rt LORD. HIS NAME INFORM"all".
HIS-rt MIRACLE TELL-STORY (2h)#ALL NATION.
ANNOUNCE HIS-rt NAME ADVANCED. Response

SING PRAISE THE-rt LORD SUCCEED GLORY.
ALL WORLD GENERAL SHOULD KNOW.
CITY HAVE TEMPLE MOUNTAIN SHOULD SHOUT THRILLED.
GOD RULE JEWISH PEOPLE HOLY.
HE-rt GREAT WITH US! Response

Notes: GO-AHEAD - (2h)[B^ dot], POs ><, FOs away, move forward in a jerk.

SHOUT - (2h)[5:], POs > signer, FOs up, rise from mouth while twisting at wrists.

MESSIAH - M-KING.

GREAT - Like WONDERFUL but a single movement.

HOLY - H-CLEAN. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Holy - 1.

TRUE-BIZ - TRUE + (2h)[S], POs down, FOs away, DH brushes from side to side across NDH wrist.

SAVIOR - S-FREE AGENT.

ME - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

SALVATION - S-FREE.

IDEA~LIKE - A blending where IDEA has a [Y] instead of an [I] in anticipation of LIKE which is signed smoothly right afterwards.

FLOW - (2h)[4], DHPO down, NDHPO up, FOs away, both hands move towards right side.

MIRACLE - WONDERFUL(1x) WORK. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Miracle.

(2h)#ALL - (2h)[A], POs ><, FOs up, are held at shoulder height and move away while opening to (2h)[L].

ADVANCED - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, move upwards arcing towards the signer. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Exalted.

GLORY - (2h)[5], POs><, FOs away from signer, DH which is on top wiggles fingers while ascending. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Glory.

ALL - (2h)[B^], POs up, FOs ><, DH circles around NDH and lands on its palm. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Whole.

GENERAL - (2h)[B], FO away, palms are "held heel to heel" with FTs pointing > respective sides slightly and move away on a diagonal.

TEMPLE - T-CHURCH.

RULE - CONTROL.

Lyrics (Is 12:2-3, 4, 5-6):

Response: Cry out with joy and gladness: for among you is the great and Holy One of Israel.

God indeed is my savior;
I am confident and unafraid.
My strength and my courage is the LORD,
and he has been my savior.
With joy you will draw water
at the fountain of salvation. Response

Give thanks to the LORD, acclaim his name;
among the nations make known his deeds,
proclaim how exalted is his name. Response

Sing praise to the LORD for his glorious achievement;
let this be known throughout all the earth.
Shout with exultation, O city of Zion,
for great in your midst
is the Holy One of Israel! Response
