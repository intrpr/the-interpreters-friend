Responsorial Psalm - 2nd Sunday of Lent (C)

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 12 February 2007, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

Response: LORD HE-rt (<) MY LIGHT, (>) MY SALVATION.

LORD HE-rt (<) MY LIGHT, (>) MY SALVATION.
ME FEAR NONE.
THE-rt LORD MY PLACE ESCAPE.
NONE MAKE ME AFRAID. Response

PAY-ATTENTION LORD WHILE ME SUMMON YOU-rt.
HAVE-MERCY-ON-me AND ANSWER ME.
MY HEART REALLY-WANT YOU-rt, ME THIRST SEE YOU-rt. Response

ME SEARCH PLACE TOGETHER YOU-rt, LORD.
PLEASE NOT TURN-AWAY.
HAPPEN YOUR SERVANT REBEL, PLEASE NOT ANGRY.
YOU-rt MY HELPER. PLEASE NOT DISREGARD ME. Response

ME BELIEVE ME SEE LORD alt.GIFT-TO-us OVERFLOW
WHILE ME STILL LIVE.
PATIENT, WAIT FOR THE-rt LORD HELP-us.
PATIENT, BRAVE, WAIT FOR THE-rt HELP-us. Response

Notes: HE - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

LIGHT - Same sign as BRIGHT and CLEAR, that is, (2h)[O^], POs away, FOs up, open to (2h)[5], while separating. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Light - 1.

SALVATION - S-FREE.

FEAR - (2h)[5], POs away, FOs up, hands are held far from body and move back to body in a wavy pattern.

SUMMON - DH [B] taps NDH [B], PO down, FO > signer, then moves > signer while closing to an [A], PO away from signer, FO up, and moving up.

HAVE-MERCY-ON-me - (2h)[open 8], POs > signer, hands move in vertical circles normal (perpendicular) to the chest. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Mercy - 2.

REALLY-WANT - WANT but shaken back and forth to show intensity.

TURN-AWAY (God) - Look up towards God and use your [V] to have God look into your face and then "look away".

DISREGARD - [S], PO > NDS, FO up, is held at nose, then moves slightly > DS, then is thrown forward while opening to a [5].

alt.GIFT-TO-us - (2h)[X], POs ><, FOs up, hands alt. flip thumb sides > signer.

OVERFLOW - [5], PO down, FO away, covers top of [S], PO > DS, FO away, and FT wiggle as the hand moves away, like a cup overflowing. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - overflow.

Lyrics (Psalm Ps 27:1, 7-8, 8-9, 13-14):

Response: The Lord is my light and my salvation.

The LORD is my light and my salvation;
whom should I fear?
The LORD is my life's refuge;
of whom should I be afraid? Response

Hear, O LORD, the sound of my call;
have pity on me, and answer me.
Of you my heart speaks; you my glance seeks. Response

Your presence, O LORD, I seek.
Hide not your face from me;
do not in anger repel your servant.
You are my helper: cast me not off. Response

I believe that I shall see the bounty of the LORD
in the land of the living.
Wait for the LORD with courage;
be stouthearted, and wait for the LORD. Response
