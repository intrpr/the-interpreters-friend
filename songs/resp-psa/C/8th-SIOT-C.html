Responsorial Psalm - 8th Sunday in Ordinary Time (C)

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 19 July 2008, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

Response: LORD, APPROPRIATE THANK+ YOU-rt.

APPROPRIATE THANK+ THE-rt LORD.
SING PRAISE YOUR-rt NAME SUPREME.
EVERY-MORNING ANNOUNCE YOU-rt KIND,
AND EVERY-NIGHT GO-AHEAD TRUST YOU-rt. Response

PEOPLE UPRIGHT THEY-ctr TAKE-OFF IDEA~LIKE PALM-TREE.
LIKE LEBANON TREE (2h)C-CL'thick trunk' GROW TALL*.
LORD HIS-rt HOUSE alt.PUT-IN-PLACE.
DEVELOP* CLOSE-TO TEMPLE AREA. Response

BECOME OLD, STILL PRODUCE FRUIT CAN.
ENTHUSIASTIC, PERKY THEY-ctr.
THEY-ctr ANNOUNCE LORD HIMSELF-rt JUST.
MY FOUNDATION, SUDDEN-WRONG CRASH NEVER. Response

Notes: YOU - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

SUPREME - (2h)[A], FOs away, DH starts below NDH and arcs around it until it is on top.

KIND - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs away, NDH is held close to heart and DH circles around it.

GO-AHEAD - (2h)[B^ dot], POs ><, FOs away, move forward in a jerk.

UPRIGHT - Elbow of [S], PO > NDS, FO up, is struck against the fingers of NDH [B] held like part of TABLE.

TAKE-OFF - Like DEVELOP but moves quickly and abruptly with a "po".

IDEA-LIKE - A blending where IDEA has a [Y] instead of an [I] in anticipation of LIKE which is signed smoothly right afterwards.

PALM-TREE - NDH [5^] grabs the elbow of [3^] and the DH which is held straight up but bent at wrist, twists at the wrist.

LEBANON - (2h)[index, middle finger and thumb], POs away, FOs up, close repeatedly, like the ASL sign for NO while descending and separating. For a film of this sign, click on Lebanon - لبنان. (< cedar tree on flag.)

alt.PUT-IN-PLACE - (2h)[S], POs ><, FOs away, put things in random places.

TEMPLE - T-CHURCH.

PERKY - Fists are held close to chest and elbows swing out slightly twice.

JUST - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, tap FT while moving in a horizontal circle.

FOUNDATION - NDH [S], PO > signer, FO > DS, is held out with arm parallel to the chest and DH [S], PO > signer, strikes hand from below and then strikes elbow from below.

SUDDEN-WRONG

COLLAPSE - (2h)[5:], POs ><, interlace fingers and then bring knuckles downwards.

Lyrics (Psalm 92:2-3, 13-14, 15-16):

Response: Lord, it is good to give thanks to you.

It is good to give thanks to the Lord,
to sing praise to your name, Most High,
to proclaim your kindness at dawn
and your faithfulness throughout the night. Response

The just one shall flourish like the palm tree,
like a cedar of Lebanon shall he grow.
They that are planted in the house of the Lord
shall flourish in the courts of our God. Response

They shall bear fruit even in old age;
vigorous and sturdy shall they be,
declaring how just is the Lord,
my rock, in whom there is no wrong. Response
