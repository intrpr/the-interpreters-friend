2. The prophecy

MARY SIT. JESUS (2h)C-CL'set on knee from rt'. [Role play to rt] COME-ON SWEET SON, TELL-STORY. EXPLAIN FUTURE "WHAT"? [Role play to lf] KNOW-THAT ME DIE WILL. (1h)DEAD LIKE STONE IN WALL. LIKE STONE IN STREET, MOTHER. (2h)#ALL MOURN FOR ME WILL. UNDERSTAND++, SOME DAY EASTER, MOTHER. ME RESURRECT WILL. (<) RESURRECT LIKE SUN SUNRISE. (>) RESURRECT LIKE MOON MOONRISE. RISE'in spiral' HOME.

3. The birthday

||: (<) TODAY CHRIST BORN. (>) TODAY SAVIOR SHOW-UP. (<) TODAY ANGEL SING. (>) TODAY ADVANCED CELEBRATE. (|) TODAY JUSTICE SAY, GIVE-TO GOD GLORY. HALLELUJAH. :|| (Although this continues with many repeated Alleluia's, repeat TODAY CHRIST... If there is still time, repeat "Alleluia", to the end.)

Notes: The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

PLAY-PIPES'sway' - Mime playing a flute with the instrument swaying.

SCADS-OF - (2h)[5:], POs down, FOs away, move forward to show many beings.

(2h)C-CL'set on knee from rt' - mime Mary picking up the Christchild and putting him on her lap.

MOONRISE - Like SUNRISE but with bC.

RISE'in spiral' - [V:], PO down, FO away, spiraling upwards.

ADVANCED - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, move upwards arcing towards the signer. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Exalted.
