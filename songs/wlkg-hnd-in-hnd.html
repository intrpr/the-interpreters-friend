Walking hand in hand - Kathy Sherman

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 20 October 2006, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

WE WILL WALK HAND-IN-HAND,
GO-TOWARDS-GOAL NEW* DAY.
GOD HIS-rt L-O-V-E ROOTED, GOSPEL VISUALIZE HELP-us LEAD US.
WE WILL HELP HOPE SPREAD. OUR LIFE SHOW"all" JUSTICE.
WE SOFT-HEARTED (2h)#ALL EARTH.
WE WILL WALK TOGETHER. WORLD CHANGE FOREVER WILL.
WE WALK HAND-IN-HAND.

Notes: HAND-IN-HAND - Hold hands as in WEDDING but the hands swing > and away from signer.

GO-TOWARDS-GOAL - like the sign GOAL but the DH moves in a vertical circle while going towards the goal to show a constant process.

ROOTED - (2h)[5], DH PO > signer, FO down, pushes FT between the fingers of NDH PO down, FO away.

GOSPEL - G-NEW. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Gospel - 2.

VISUALIZE - (2h)[S] are held at forehead with DH closer to head, and hands open to (2h)[5] while separating while FT wg. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Vision - 1.

JUSTICE - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, tap FT while moving in a horizontal circle.

SOFT-HEARTED - HEART + (2h)[5^] descend slightly twice while closing to (2h)[O^] but hands close to heart.

(2h)#ALL - (2h)[A], POs ><, FOs up, are held at shoulder height and move away while opening to (2h)[L].

Lyrics:

We will walk hand in hand,
Onward toward a brand new day.
Rooted in God's love, with a gospel vision to lead us.
We will be the voice of hope, living signs of justice,
tender compassion for all the earth.
We will walk together, change the world forever,
Walking hand in hand.
