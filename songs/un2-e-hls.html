EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

HILLS THEREABOUTS@rt ME LIFT-UP-EYES-TO-rt LOOK-AT'over time'. WHY? HE SEND HELP WILL. HE WHO? GOD. HIMSELF ALWAYS WILLING HELP-me. GOD HIMSELF LORD THAT (<) MAKE HEAVEN, (>) MAKE EARTH.

ENEMY WANT (2h)YOU FAIL, GOD SAVE WILL. GOD SLEEP NEVER, ALWAYS TAKE-CARE-OF "each". KNOW-THAT HE SUPERVISE WORLD EVERY MINUTE EVERY-DAY SAME-OLD-THING. HIS PEOPLE, GOD CHERISH, KEEP HOLY.

LORD, (2h)YOU TRUST CAN. HE CHANGE NEVER. LORD ALWAYS READY HELP. WHY? HE MAKE (2h)YOU, MEAN CHERISH MUST. (<) ALL-DAY GOD PROTECT AGAINST SUN. (>) ALL-NIGHT GOD PROTECT AGAINST MOON.

(<) EACH BAD THING, GOD REJECT. (>) EACH SIN, GOD THROW-OUT. (<) (2h)YOU ENTER, GOD GO-TOGETHER. (>) (2h)YOU LEAVE, GOD GO-TOGETHER. (<) HE LOOK-DOWN, SUPERVISE. (>) WE LOOK-UP, WORSHIP. GOD TAKE-CARE-OF (2h)YOU, FROM-NOW-ON FOREVER FOREVER. AMEN.

Notes:

LIFT-UP-EYES-TO-rt - (2h)[V], POs down, FOs > right, move FTs upwards as if looking up.

KNOW-THAT - Starts like KNOW but twists and descends, looking almost like DON'T-KNOW except the hand twists down at the end.

SAME-OLD-THING - (2h)[Y], POs down, FOs away, circle vertically and symmetrically in a plane parallel to the chest.
