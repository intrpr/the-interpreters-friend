EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

UP-TO-NOW NOT THINK MY LIFE CAN SOMETHING NOT SCREWED-UP. BUT HIT* ME START NOW SEE, LITTLE-BIT GOOD LUCK FOR ME. WHY? GOLDEN TICKET HAVE*. MEAN GOLDEN eyes-SHINY, LOOK-AT-eyes MY EYES.

UP-TO-NOW NO CHANCE SUCCESS FOR ME. NO HAPPY SONG. BUT HIT* NOW HALF EVERYTHING IN WORLD MINE. WHY? GOLDEN TICKET HAVE*. ME HAVE GOLD SUN UP-THERE MY SKY.

UP-TO-NOW NOT THINK SEE DAY ARRIVE, APPROACH WORLD CAN, GOOD MORNING. LOOK-UP-rt SEE SUN HON.-IX. UP-TO-NOW NOT THINK MY LIFE CAN FANCY LONG-LIST HAVE++. UP-TO-NOW ME SAY (2h)FORGET~I-T, IMPOSSIBLE. BUT HAPPEN CAN. UP-TO-NOW NOT DREAM MOON'hold until "jump over"' ME JUMP-OVER-moon. THRILLED. BUT ANYWAY SOON ME ARRIVE THERE MOON. WHY? GOLDEN TICKET HAVE*. GOLDEN CHANCE HAVE*. PAH TAKE-OFF. WITH GOLDEN TICKET, ME HAVE DAY SHINY IDEA~LIKE GOLD'stretch out'.

Notes: SCREWED-UP - (2h)[5:], FOs away, DH is held high, PO down at its side and NDH is held low, PO up at its side. Both hands interchange PO and levels while moving slightly > DS.

HIT* - An emphatic form of HIT to show a sudden unexpected result.

eyes-SHINY - (2h)[open 8], POs > eyes, twist at wrists while moving away

LOOK-AT-eyes - [V], FOs switch from away to > eyes.

UP-THERE - [B], PO up, FO > sun.

APPROACH - (2h)[B], FOs up, DH PO away, ND is held in front of NDH and palm faces it, then both hands approach one another slightly.

HON.-IX - [B], PO up, FO > sun.

LONG-LIST - (2h)[O^], POs > signer, NDH - above and touches DH; both hands open to (2h)[5] while DH descends.

(2h)FORGET~I-T - (2h)[open 8] touch middle fingers to forehead, separate while maintaining contact, then close to (2h)[T], PO > forehead.

MOON'hold until "jump over"' ME JUMP-OVER-moon - Sign MOON with NDH, that is, [bC], PO > DS, FO up, dips down then moves up and stays in place while signer signs ME and then JUMP-OVER in such a way that is does jumpr over the [bC] (moon).

THERE - [B], PO up, FO > sun.

TAKE-OFF - Like DEVELOP but moves quickly and abruptly with a "po".

SHINY - (2h)[open 8], POs down, FOs away, twist rapidly at wrists while rising. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Shine.

IDEA~LIKE - a blending where IDEA has a [Y] instead of an [I] in anticipation of LIKE which - signed smoothly right afterwards.

Lyrics:

I never thought my life could be anything but catastrophe! But suddenly I begin to see a bit of good luck for me! 'Cos I've got a golden ticket! I've got a golden twinkle in my eye! I never had a chance to shine. Never a happy song to sing. But suddenly half the world is mine! What an amazing thing! 'Cos I've got a golden ticket! I've got a golden sun up in my sky! I never thought I'd see the day when I would face the world and say, "Good morning. Look at the sun!" I never thought that I would be slap in the lap of luxury! 'Cause I'd have said it couldn't be done! But it can be done! I never dreamed that I would climb over the moon in ecstasy; But nevertheless it's there that I'm shortly about to be! 'Cos I've got a golden ticket! I've got a golden chance to make my way! And with a golden ticket it's a golden day!
