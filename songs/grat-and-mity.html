EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

||: WONDERFUL STRONG HE. :||
HE SHINY, BRIGHT MIME'put up hands and look away'.
WONDERFUL STRONG HE.
||: WONDERFUL STRONG HE :||
HE SHINY, BRIGHT MIME'put up hands and look away'.
WONDERFUL STRONG HE.

COME-ON ANNOUNCE HIS NAME ADVANCED. CELEBRATE HIS GRACE
OUR LIFE HE SAVE FINISH. HE CONTROL WORLD FAR-AWAY HEAVEN.

||: WONDERFUL STRONG HE. :||
HE SHINY, BRIGHT MIME'put up hands and look away'.
WONDERFUL STRONG HE.
||: WONDERFUL STRONG HE :||
HE SHINY, BRIGHT MIME'put up hands and look away'.
WONDERFUL STRONG HE.

Notes: The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

SHINY - (2h)[open 8], POs down, FOs away, twist rapidly at wrists while rising. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Shine.

COME-ON - (2h)[B] move in circles pivoting at the wrists to beckon someone to do something.

ADVANCED - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, move upwards arcing towards the signer. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Exalted.

FAR-AWAY - [1], twists while moving away with tongue shuttling from side to side. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Far.

Lyrics:

Great and mighty is He.
Great and mighty is He.
Clothed in glory, arrayed in splendor.
Great and mighty is He.

Let us lift His name up high.
Celebrate His grace,
For He has redeemed our lives
and He reigns on high.
