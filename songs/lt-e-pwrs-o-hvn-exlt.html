Footnotes

1. The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

2. THOSE - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

3. POWERFUL - [C] or [5:] brings FTs rapidly to the ND biceps.

4. ANGEL - (2h)[B^] touch FTs to shoulders, then arms extended and bent move slightly as if flying gently.

5. SCADS-OF - (2h)[5:], POs down, FOs away, move forward to show many beings.

6. (2h)#ALL - (2h)[A], POs ><, FOs up, are held at shoulder height and move away while opening to (2h)[L].

7. LIVING - LIVE+.

8. RESURRECT/ION - [V], PO up, FO away, pronates and hops onto the palm of [B], PO up, FO away. For an animated gif of this sign, see Valley Bible Church - ASL dictionary: Resurrection.

9. COME-ON - (2h)[B^] move in circles pivoting at the wrists to beckon someone to do something.

10. SOUND-TRUMPET - (2h)[S] are held to the mouth as if holding an ancient (valveless) trumpet to the mouth, and the cheeks puff out as if blowing as the head moves forward.

11. LOUD - Touch ear with index finger then (2h)[S], PO down, FO away, move antisymmetrically from side to side while mouthing "pow". For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - thunder.

12. (2h)[S], POs down, FOs away, are held with DH on top of NDH and DH bends back fist and strikes NDH at wrist while fist continues to move down.

13. earth-SHINY - (2h)[open 8], POs down, FOs away, twist rapidly at wrists while rising. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Shine. Note that the word before "SHINY" will determine where the FTs point to as the sign begins.

14. FROM-NOW-ON - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs away, DHFT touches knuckle side of NDHFT and moves away in a downward arc. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - from now on.

15. DRESS-UP - (2h)[5], POs down, FOs away, alt. circle perpendicular to the chest with thumbs brushing against chest when they come hear the body.

16. HOLY - H-CLEAN. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Holy - 1.

17. SUNSHINE - Index finger traces a circle in the sky + [O^] is held high PO > circle drawn but turns to signer; hands open to [5^] while moving > signer.

18. CRY-OUT - [5:] holds palm to mouth and then hand moves up and away as if the sound is leaving the mouth.

19. REFLECT - [O^], PO away, FOs up, is brought to [B], PO away, FO up, and reflects off it while opening to [5].

20. MIGHTY - (2h)[S], POs > back of signer, FOs up, are held at NDS and fists descend, then repeat at DS.

21. THEREFORE - [bO] pokes a triangle of dots in the air like the mathematical symbol for "therefore".

22. GATHER-TOGETHER - (2h)[S], POs down, FOs ><, move > each other while opening to (2h)[5wg].

23. BRIGHT - (2h)[O^], POs away, FOs up, open to (2h)[5], while separating. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Light - 1.

24. SUMMON - DH [B] taps NDH [B], PO down, FO > signer, then moves > signer while closing to an [A], PO away from signer, FO up, and moving up.

25. ADVANCED - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, move upwards arcing towards the signer. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Exalted.

26. HAVE-MERCY-ON-us - (2h)[open 8], POs > signer, hands move in vertical circles normal (perpendicular) to the chest. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Mercy - 2.

27. PRIEST - [G], PO > signer, FO up, moves across neck from NDS to DS to show clerical collar.

28. SHINE-DOWN-ON - [O^] is held high PO > signer; hands open to [5^] while moving > signer.

29. AMEN - (2h)[B], FOs up, are pressed together and brought down in a long, deliberate motion. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Amen.

30. LIFT-UP - (2h)[B], PO down are held high and swoop down while supinating and then rise up again as if helping something mature and the hands continue up and FTs are directed > Heaven.

31. LIFT-HEART-UP -(2h)[C], POs ><, FOs up, curl fingers slightly so that nails are pressed against nails and the hands make a traditional heart shape. Hold hands against heart and then lift them up towards Heaven.

32. GO-AHEAD - (2h)[B^ dot], POs ><, FOs away, move forward in a jerk.

33. TRUE-BIZ - TRUE + (2h)[S], POs down, FOs away, DH brushes from side to side across NDH wrist.

34. OVERFLOW - [5], PO down, FO away, covers top of [S], PO > DS, FO away, and FT wiggle as the hand moves away, like a cup overflowing. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - overflow.

35. BLESS - (2h)[O^], POs ><, held at mouth. Hands are brought downwards while opening to (2h)[5]. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Bless.

36. ADAM - [A] taps DS temple with thumb tip 2x.

37. SACRED - S-NICE.

38. FLOW - (2h)[4], POs > signer, FOs ><, both hands move down toegther twice to show flow from the body.

39. PAY-OFF - [open 8] runs middle finger up palm (> wrist), changes to a [B], PO down, and then moves back across palm.

40. SIN - (2h)[X] touch their respective sides of the mouth, descend to neutral space and circle antisymmetrically parallel to the chest. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Sin.

41. DEBT - [1^] taps FT against palm of [B], PO up, FO away. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Debt.

42. PARDON - Sign EXCUSE, switch dominance and sign EXCUSE again.

43. KNOW-THAT - Starts like KNOW but twists and descends, looking almost like DON'T-KNOW except the hand twists down at the end.

44. PASSOVER - [S] taps thumbside against elbow (like CRACKER). For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - cracker.

45. FEAST - (2h)alt.EAT. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Feast.

46. SLAUGHTER - Thumb of [A dot] is drawn against throat, as if slitting it.

47. DOORPOST - DOOR + [G], PO > NDS, FO away, runs FT down [B], PO > DS, FO up, to the elbow.

48. GIFT-TO-rt~#BACK - (2h)[X], POs ><, FOs up, nod downwards at wrist to show that something is given as a gift + [B], PO away, FO up, fingers curl, then staighten to a [K], while moving > rt.

49. PILLAR - (2h)[C], POs ><, FOs away, hands ascend from below to outline a pillar.

50. GLOOM - (2h)[5], POs > signer, FOs up, descend while fingers curl.

51. CRAZY-FOR - [5:], PO > singer, FO up, is shaken slightly from side to side.

52. GLORY - (2h)[5], POs><, FOs away from signer, DH which is on top wiggles fingers while ascending. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Glory.

53. LEAVE-AT-lf - (2h)[5], POs ><, FOs away, descend at left to show where sin is left.

54. BECKON - [1], PO up, FO up, gestures by bending to beckon someone.

55. OFF-POINT-lf&rt - DH [S], PO > NDS, flicks finger off to the left and then to the right.

56. GRACE - [O^] or [S], held high and PO > Heaven, supinates and opens to [5] while being brought towards head. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Grace.

57. IDEA~LIKE - a blending where IDEA has a [Y] instead of an [I] in anticipation of LIKE which is signed smoothly right afterwards.

58. BRIDE - (2h)[S], POs ><, FOs away, DH is held on top of the NDH as if holding a bouquet and the hands move forward in two downward arcs. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - bride.

59. TENDER - HEART + (2h)[5^] descend slightly twice while closing to (2h)[O^] but hands close to heart.

60. MORE-THAN - (2h)[B^], touch FTs, then DH moves up in an arc towards the signer and away. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - more than.

61. SLOUGH-OFF - The sign LOSE(-AN-OBJECT) is made several times close to the body and moves towards the DS, rather than straight down.

62. RELAPSE - [V] touches FT to [B], PO up, and "slips and falls".

63. VANISH - (2h)[5^], DH PO down, NDH PO up, DH is held above NDH. Hands separate and close to (2h)[O^].s

64. ARROGANCE - (2h)[L:], POs ><, FOs away, are held at forehead and suddenly expand. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - brat.

65. TYRANNY - (2h)[X], POs away, FOs > respective sides, alt. move > signer and away, like CONTROL but with a smug facial expression.

66. many-CONNECT-UP - COOPERATE but with the interlinked fingers opening and closing.

67. FLAME - FIRE but a smaller movement.

68. EASTER - (2h)[E], POs away, FOs up, separate in upward arcs. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Easter.

69. TINY-LITTLE - [G] is held close to head and twisted with hu.

70. DH [F] touches the cheek with its joined fingers to represent an insect biting; then changes to a [Bb] and brushes it aside. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - bee.

71. TEMPT - (2h)[A^] hold imaginary pitchfork and poke at the person being tempted. Don't confuse this with TEMPTED, which is FT of [X] taps elbow.

72. DRAWN-TO - [5] points FT to eyes, moves away with wg while closing to an [S].

73. CRUSH - [5:] is bent up at wrist and plows across the NDH palm.

74. STEADFAST - STUBBORN, but with a noble facial expression.

75. REALLY-WANT - WANT but shaken back and forth to show intensity.

76. star-SET - Like SUNSET but here it refers to a star dipping below the horizon.

For this is the Passover feast in which the true Lamb was killed, whose blood made holy the doorposts of the faithful.

O night of glory! :|| Therefore this blessed night scatters evil, washes sins away, restores the integrity to the fallen, and gives back joy to those in sorrow. Let it put hatred to flight! This is the night the Light broke the chains of death. O holy night! O night of glory!
