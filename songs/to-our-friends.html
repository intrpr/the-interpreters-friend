Notes: GOOD-FRIEND - [R], PO > signer, FO up, moves forward slightly.

LOOK-BACK - (2h)[N], PO > signer, FO up, start in neutral space and move in an outward arc to the DS shoulder.

YOU-TWO - TWO-OF-YOU.

BELLY-LAUGH - (2h)[5:], POs ><, FOs away. DH is held above NDH and moves backwards and forwards,

UPS-AND-DOWNS - (2h)[Bb], POs down, FOs away, are held side-by-side. DH tilts and moves up, then tilts and moves down.

INDEX-LIST - index finger of DH points to thumb, then index finger, then middle finger of NDH [5], PO > signer, FO > DS.

ASSEMBLE - (2h)[S], FO > signer, move > signer while opening to (2h)[5wg].

#ALL-OF-US - [A], PO > NDS, FO up touches DS shoulder then [L^], PO > signer touches NDS shoulder.

The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

IMPACT-us - HIT but the NDH is held close to the chest and the DH strikes it while moving > chest.
