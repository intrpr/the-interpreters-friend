Lord, by your cross and resurrection - John Foley

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 3 September 2004, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

Refrain: LORD, YOU (<) CRUCIFIED, (>) RESURRECTED, MEAN WE FREE. YOU SAVE WHOLE WORLD YOU.

1. DOESN'T-MATTER YOURSELF GOD, STILL WILLING BECOME LOWLY MAN. WE PRAISE YOU, WE THANK YOU, WE ADVANCE YOUR NAME HIGH. Refrain

2. YOU CHRIST JESUS, WILLING HUMBLE, GIVE-UP PLACE ADVANCE. WE PRAISE YOU, WE THANK YOU, WE ADVANCE YOUR NAME HIGH. Refrain

3. CHRIST OBEY, DEATH ACCEPT. NOT EMBARASSED DIE CRUCIFIED. WE PRAISE YOU, WE THANK YOU, WE ADVANCE YOUR NAME HIGH. Refrain

4. YOU LORD JESUS, GOD HELP ADVANCE. YOUR NAME ANNOUNCE, WE KNEEL BOW-DOWN. WE PRAISE YOU, WE THANK YOU, WE ADVANCE YOUR NAME HIGH. Refrain

Notes: WHOLE - (2h)[B^], POs up, FOs ><, DH circles around NDH and lands on its palm.

LOWLY - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, move downwards arcing towards the signer. Is the opposite of ADVANCED. See below.

ADVANCED - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, move upwards arcing towards the signer. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Exalted.

Lyrics:

Refrain: Lord, by your cross and resurrection, you have set us free. You are the Savior of the world.

1. You, Lord Jesus, though in the form of God, you assumed a servant form. We praise you, we thank you, we raise your name on high.

2. You, Christ Jesus, emptied yourself for all, never grasping Godliness. We praise you, we thank you, we raise your name on high.

3. Christ, obedient, even to death itself, even death upon a tree. We praise you, we thank you, we raise your name on high.

4. You, Lord Jesus, God has exalted you. At your name eacn knee will bow. We praise you, we thank you, we raise your name on high.
