Notes: GO-AWAY - (2h)[B], arms parallel to the floor, FOs away to one side, hands start at right angles to the wrists like the end of the sign LATE, and move up to their normal position while arms move away, as if shooing someone away.

SHH - [1] is tapped against pouted lips like the hearing gesture one uses with children.

SAY-#NO - #NO with a single, emphatic movement.

THUNDER - touch ear with index finger then (2h)[S], PO down, FO away, move antisymmetrically from side to side while mouthing "pow". For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - thunder.

INSIDE-SELF - Hold the FT of [C] against the chest and stuff the FT of [O^] into the [C] twice.

GREEN* - Like GREEN but with one broad sweep of the arm.

(2h)5-CL'trees bend down and return' is (2h)[5], POs away, FOs up, bow down once and then return to their place.

HAVE-MERCY-ON-us - (2h)[open 8], POs > signer, hands move in vertical circles normal (perpendicular) to the chest. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Mercy - 2.

LEGS-TREMBLE is (2h)[1^], POs down, FOs away, tremple symmetrically.

LIGHTNING is [1] makes a zig zag in the air. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - thunderbolt.
