SUPPOSE WATER DEEP, HE WILL PROTECT. SUPPOSE WIND STRONG*, HE WILL PROTECT.

FEEL OVERWHELMED, HE WILL PROTECT. SCARED NIGHT? THAT ALRIGHT.

KNOW-THAT (2h)YOU CAN'T ESCAPE GOD HIS KINGDOM. NO*, CAN'T ESCAPE.

NO*, CAN'T ESCAPE. HE TAKE-CARE (1h)YOU.

KNOW-THAT (2h)YOU CAN'T ESCAPE GOD HIS KINGDOM. NO*, CAN'T ESCAPE. HE NURTURE~(2h)YOU.

KNOW-THAT (2h)YOU CAN'T ESCAPE GOD HIS KINGDOM. NO*, CAN'T ESCAPE.

HE YOUR A-TO-Z. CAN'T ESCAPE. HAPPEN FEEL SAD, CAN'T ESCAPE.

HE COMFORT YOU, CAN'T ESCAPE. SILLY TRY HIDE, CAN'T ESCAPE.

HE ONLY WANT HELP-'you all', CAN'T ESCAPE.

||: KNOW-THAT (2h)YOU CAN'T ESCAPE GOD HIS KINGDOM. (3 times) :||

CAN'T ESCAPE.

Notes: The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

KNOW-THAT - Starts like KNOW but twists and descends, looking almost like DON'T-KNOW except the hand twists down at the end.

NURTURE~(2h)YOU - (2h)[B], PO down are held high and swoop down while supinating and then rise up again as if helping something mature, followed immediately by spreading the hands to the side.

A-TO-Z - [A], PO away, FO up, is held on NDS of NDH [5], PO > signer, FO up, and then the DH moves over NDH FT and traces a "Z" on the DS of the NDH.

HELP-'you all' - HELP swept from NDS to DS to indicate helping everyone.
