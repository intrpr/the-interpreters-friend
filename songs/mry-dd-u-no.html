Notes: MARY - [M] touches NDS temple, is drawn across forehead, and descends. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Mary - 1.

SURFACE~WALK-ON - SURFACE - Palm of [B], rubs in circles against back hand of [B], PO down (for a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - surface) + with NDH left in place, DH [V], PO > signer, moves away while moving fingers as if walking.

QM - [1], PO away, FO up, traces the shape of a question mark curling and uncurling the fingers once. This will clarify that the sentences are a series of questions. This sign need not be used again.

YOU - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

RELEASE - (2h)[F], interlink fingers, let go, and separate.

CAUSE - (2h)[S] are held at the chest as if holding a rope and move forward while opening to (2h)[5]. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - cause.

STORM CALM-DOWN - (2h)[5], POs ><, FOs up, antisymmetrically make figure-eights, then POs change to down and descend slowly.

WOW - Shake out [5].

LAME - (2h)[1^], PO down, FO away, hands circle normal (perpendicular) to the chest with the DH making bigger circles as if the person is limping.

MUTE - Palm of [A] strikes the mouth.

GO-AHEAD - (2h)[B^ dot], POs ><, FOs away, move forward in a jerk.

RULE - CONTROL.

(2h)#ALL - (2h)[A], POs ><, FOs up, are held at shoulder height and move away while opening to (2h)[L].

FALL-ASLEEP - (2h)[5:], DH PO > face, FO up, NDH PO up, FO away. Both hands close to [A], with twisting around and striking NDH from above.

INDEX-SELF - [A], PO > NDS, FO away, knocks on thumb side of [1], PO > DS, FO up.

Lyrics:

1. Mary did you know that your baby boy would one day walk on water?
Mary, did you know that your baby boy would save our sons and daughters?
Did you know that your baby boy has come to make you new?
This child that you delivered will soon deliver you.

2. Mary did you know that your baby boy will give sight to the blind man?
Mary did you know that your baby boy would calm a storm with his hand?
Did you know that your baby boy has walked where angels trod,
and when you kiss your little baby, you've kissed the face of God?

Coda: Mary did you know? Mary did you know?
The blind will see and the deaf will hear.
The dead will live again. The lame will leap,
the dumb will speak the praises of the Lamb.

3. Mary did you know that your baby boy is Lord of all creation?
Mary did you know that your baby boy will one day rule the nations?
Did you know that your baby boy was heaven's perfect Lamb,
and the sleeping Child you're holding is the great I am. Oh, Mary. Oh.
