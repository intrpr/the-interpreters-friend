Up from the waters (Sprinkling song) - Marty Haugen

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 18 April 2006, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

Refrain: RISE-UP, WATER LEAVE. GOD NOW ADOPT (2h)YOU.
RISE-UP, WATER LEAVE, (2h)YOU CHILDREN HAVE LIGHT.
PRAISE HIM-lf,hi HIMSELF-lf,hi SUMMON AND NAME (2h)YOU.
RISE-UP, WATER LEAVE. ENTER LIFE.

1. ALWAYS ANNOUNCE WONDERFUL STORY:
RISE-UP, WATER LEAVE. (2h)YOU CHILDREN HAVE LIGHT.
GOD RAISE-UP (2h)YOU, GLORY REPLACE DEATH:
RISE-UP, WATER LEAVE. ENTER LIFE. Refrain

2. RIVER JORDAN FINISH CROSS:
RISE-UP, WATER LEAVE. (2h)YOU CHILDREN HAVE LIGHT.
NOW (2h)YOU WITNESS GOD HAVE-MERCY-ON-us, WILLING PARDON:
RISE-UP, WATER LEAVE. ENTER LIFE. Refrain

3. SIN LOSE POWER TEMPT AND ENTHRALL (2h)YOU:
RISE-UP, WATER LEAVE. (2h)YOU CHILDREN HAVE LIGHT.
JESUS SEND GRACE SPRINKLE-DOWN, EMBRACE (2h)YOU:
RISE-UP, WATER LEAVE. ENTER LIFE. Refrain

4. COME-ON PRAISE GOD, HIMSELF RULE (2h)#ALL LIVE:
RISE-UP, WATER LEAVE. (2h)YOU CHILDREN HAVE LIGHT.
GO-AHEAD SING LOUD THANK YOUR"all" GOD:
RISE-UP, WATER LEAVE. ENTER LIFE. Refrain

5. WATER HAVE LIFE, OFFER GRACE AND SALVATION:
RISE-UP, WATER LEAVE. (2h)YOU CHILDREN HAVE LIGHT.
WATER CAUSE HEART (2h)#ALL LIVING HEAL:
RISE-UP, WATER LEAVE. ENTER LIFE. Refrain

6. NOW (2h)YOU HAVE LIFE LIKE RAIN FROM HEAVEN:
RISE-UP, WATER LEAVE. (2h)YOU CHILDREN HAVE LIGHT.
YOUR"all" NEW LIFE, JESUS alt.GIFT-TO"each":
RISE-UP, WATER LEAVE. ENTER LIFE. Refrain

Notes: RISE-UP - (2h)[B], POs down, FOs away, lower slightly, supinate, and rise up.

LEAVE - As in go to another place, rather than leave something in the same place.

(2h)YOU - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

LIGHT - Same sign as BRIGHT and CLEAR, that is, (2h)[O^], POs away, FOs up, open to (2h)[5], while separating. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Light - 1.

SUMMON - DH [B] taps NDH [B], PO down, FO > signer, then moves > signer while closing to an [A], PO away from signer, FO up, and moving up.

RAISE-UP - Same as RISE-UP, but labelled differently to show it function in the sentence.

GLORY - (2h)[5], POs><, FOs away from signer, DH which is on top wiggles fingers while ascending. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Glory.

JORDAN - [L], FO up, touches thumb to forehead. For a film of this sign, click on Jordan - المملكة الأردنية الهاشمية.

CROSS - Same as ACROSS.

WITNESS - [1] touches under eye while head moves down.

HAVE-MERCY-ON-us - (2h)[open 8], POs > signer, hands move in vertical circles normal (perpendicular) to the chest. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Mercy - 2.

SIN - (2h)[X] touch their respective sides of the mouth, descend to neutral space and circle antisymmetrically parallel to the chest. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Sin.

TEMPT - (2h)[A^] hold imaginary pitchfork and poke at the person being tempted. Don't confuse this with TEMPTED, which is FT of [X] taps elbow.

ENTHRALL - Point FT of [5], PO down, > face, move hand away with wg and close to a [5] with the head following after it.

GRACE - [O^] or [S], held high and PO > Heaven, supinates and opens to [5] while being brought towards head. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Grace.

SPRINKLE-DOWN - (2h)[5], PO down, FO away, hands descend with wg.

EMBRACE - Mime hugging.

COME-ON - (2h)[B^] move in circles pivoting at the wrists to beckon someone to do something.

RULE - CONTROL.

(2h)#ALL - (2h)[A], POs ><, FOs up, are held at shoulder height and move away while opening to (2h)[L].

GO-AHEAD - (2h)[B^ dot], POs ><, FOs away, move forward in a jerk.

LOUD - Touch ear with index finger then (2h)[S], PO down, FO away, move antisymmetrically from side to side while mouthing "pow". For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - thunder.

SALVATION - SAVE.

CAUSE - (2h)[S] are held at the chest as if holding a rope and move forward while opening to (2h)[5]. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - cause.

alt.GIFT-TO"each" - (2h)[X], POs ><, FOs > the recipents, hands move together from NDS to DS.

Lyrics:

Refrain: Up from the water, God has claimed you,
Up from the waters, O child of light.
Praise to the One who called and named you,
Up from the waters to life.

1. Always proclaim the wonderful story:
Up from the waters, child of light.
How you were raised from death into glory:
Up from the water into life.

2. Now you have crossed the River of Jordan:
Up from the waters, child of light.
Now you behold God's mercy and pardon:
Up from the water into life.

3. No more shall sin have power to hold you:
Up from the waters, child of light.
Now let the grace of Jesus enfold you:
Up from the water into life.

4. Come now and praise the God of the living:
Up from the waters, child of light.
Now let your song ring out with thanksgiving:
Up from the water into life.

5. Water of life and grace and salvation:
Up from the waters, child of light.
Water that heals the heart of creation:
Up from the water into life.

6. This is the life that rains down from heaven:
Up from the waters, child of light.
This is the new life Jesus has given:
Up from the water into life.
