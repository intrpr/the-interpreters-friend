EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

HERE ME WAIT, PRAY YOU LIVE INSIDE-SELF WILL.
HERE ME, (2h)alt.LONG FOR YOU
YOUR , ME REALLY-WANT DEEPLY-INVOLVED.
MAKE ME HUMBLE DURING PRAY.
ME WANT KNOW JESUS MORE-AND-MORE.

Refrain: ||: COME-ON LIVE INSIDE-SELF.
MY LIFE TAKE-OVER COMPLETE.
BREATHE INSIDE-SELF.
ME TAKE-OFF IDEA~LIKE EAGLE + (2h)B-CL'fly away'. :||

Notes: INSIDE-SELF - Holds the FT of [C] against the chest and stuff the FT of [O^] into the [C] twice.

(2h)alt.LONG - Hands alt. execute WISH.

REALLY-WANT - The sign WANT but shaken back and forth to show intensity.

DEEPLY-INVOLVED - DH [V:] circles slightly and dives into [C], PO > DS, FO away.

MORE-AND-MORE - The NDH stays in place while the DH strikes the NDH while moving in a circle parallel to the chest.

The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

COME-ON - (2h)[B] move in circles pivoting at the wrists to beckon someone to do something.

TAKE-OVER - (2h)[5:], POs down, FOs away, close while rapidly rising.

COMPLETE - (2h)[B^], POs up, FOs ><, DH circles around NDH and lands on its palm. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Whole.

TAKE-OFF - Like DEVELOP but with a rapid movement.

IDEA~LIKE - A blending where IDEA has a [Y] instead of an [I] in anticipation of LIKE which is signed smoothly right afterwards.

(2h)B-CL'fly away' - (2h)[B], POs down, cross at wrists and flap while moving upwards and away.
