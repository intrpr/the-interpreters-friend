SOON SEE YOUR KINGDOM COME WILL.

Notes: COVER-UP is (2h)[B], DHPO down, NDHPO up, DH moves from DS to NDS, brushes its palm over the palm of the NDH and then passing by.

BURDEN-OFF starts like BURDEN but the hands flick off the burden.
