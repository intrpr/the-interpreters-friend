Notes: LOOK'looklooklook@lf, low' - [V] points FT to low, left where Baby Jesus is spatialized and the signer mouths "looklooklook".

IDEA~LIKE - A blending where IDEA has a [Y] instead of an [I] in anticipation of LIKE which is signed smoothly right afterwards.

BLOOM - (2h)[A] are held together but the hands are twisted like the beginning of CHANGE. Hands twist back while opening to (2h)[5:] with the hands still held together.

KING-DAVID - D-KING.

BUD - (2h)[O^], held together with FOs up.

PAH - LIKE SUCCEED but "pah" is mouthed and has the meaning of "at long last". For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - finally.

ISAIAH - I-PREDICT.

SELF-ON-INDEX - [A], PO > signer, FO away, taps against [1], PO > DS, FO up.

ME - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

MARY - [M] touches NDS temple, is drawn across forehead, and descends. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Mary - 1.

VIRGIN - V-GIRL.

KIND - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs away, NDH is held close to heart and DH circles around it.

GIVE-BIRTH - S-ENTER.

SCADS-OF - (2h)[5:], POs down, FOs away, move forward to show many beings.

DURING-NIGHT - (2H)[B], DH, FO away, rests wrist on the back of NDH, PO down, FO > DS, and DH curls around little finger edge of NDH until its PO is up and touching NDH palm.

GLORY - (2h)[5], POs ><, FOs away from signer, DH which is on top wiggles fingers while ascending. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Glory.

BETHLEHEM - "B" CITY. For an animated gif of this sign, see Valley Bible Church - ASL dictionary: Bethlehem.

REALLY-WANT - the sign WANT but shaken back and forth to show intensity.

MANGER - (2h)[5], FOs down interlace fingers and then separate while ascending in outward arcs.

FRAGRANCE - [5], PO down, FO > NDS, starts with wrist touching nose and the hand is moved > DS while FT wg.

(2h)5wg-CL'waft upwards' - (2h)[5], POs down, FOs away, rise while wg to show the fragrance ascending.

SHINY - (2h)[open 8], POs down, FOs away, twist rapidly at wrists while rising. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Shine.

SIN - (2h)[X] touch their respective sides of the mouth, descend to neutral space and circle antisymmetrically parallel to the chest. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Sin.

CRAZY-FOR - [5:], PO > singer, FO up, - shaken slightly from side to side.

Lyrics:

1. Lo, how a Rose e'er blooming from tender stem hath sprung!
Of Jesse's lineage coming, as those of old have sung.
It came, a floweret bright, amid the cold of winter,
When half spent was the night.

2. Isaiah 'twas foretold it, the Rose I have in mind;
With Mary we behold it, the virgin mother kind.
To show God's love aright, she bore to us a Savior,
When half spent was the night.

3. The shepherds heard the story proclaimed by angels bright,
How Christ, the Lord of glory was born on earth this night.
To Bethlehem they sped and in the manger they found Him,
As angel heralds said.

4. This Flower, whose fragrance tender with sweetness fills the air,
Dispels with glorious splendor the darkness everywhere;
True Man, yet very God, from sin and death He saves us,
And lightens every load.

5. O Savior, Child of Mary, Who felt our human woe,
O Savior, King of glory, Who dost our weakness know;
Bring us at length we pray, to the bright courts of heaven,
And to the endless day!
