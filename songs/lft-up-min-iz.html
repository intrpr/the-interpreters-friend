EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

HILLS@rt, ME LOOK-UP-TO-rt. WHY? HELP ARRIVE WILL. LORD HE HELP-me (2h)ME. HIMSELF MAKE (<) HEAVEN, (>) EARTH. PEOPLE TRY FORCE (2h)YOU, HE PREVENT. YOUR PROTECTOR WATCH"each"'vigilantly'. SLEEP NEVER. GOD SUPERVISE JEWISH PEOPLE, SLEEPY-EYED, FALL-ASLEEP NEVER. HILLS@rt, ME LOOK-UP-TO-rt. WHY? HELP ARRIVE WILL. LORD HE HELP-me (2h)ME. HIMSELF MAKE (<) HEAVEN, (>) EARTH. LORD SUPERVISE (2h)YOU, SUNSHINE HOT 5^-CL'sun beat down + sta++', HE PROTECT (2h)YOU. ALL-DAY SUN, ALL-NIGHT MOON, BE-PATIENT. BAD INFLUENCE"all" CAN'T. YOUR"all" SOUL SAVE. ANY* PLACE (2h)YOU TRAVEL, LORD PROTECT FROM-NOW-ON FOREVER. AMEN.

Notes: HELP-me - HELP but moving > signer.

WATCH"each"'vigilantly' - (2h)[V], POs down, FOs away, with hands moving away with a deliberate movement > NDS, > ctr, > DS with lips pursed.

SLEEPY-EYED - (2h)[Bb] held close to eyes, POs alt. away and > signer.

FALL-ASLEEP - (2h)[5:], NDH PO up, FO away, DH PO away, FO up, both hands close to [A], with NDH keeping its PO and FO.

5^-CL'sun beat down + sta++' - [5^], PO > signer from DS, moving in a vertical circle and with the signer mouthing "stastasta".
