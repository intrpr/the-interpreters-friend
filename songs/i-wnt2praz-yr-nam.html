I want to praise your name - Bob Hurd

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 13 June 2007, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

1. PRAISE GOD HOW? SOUND-TRUMPET, PRAISE PLAY-HARP.
PRAISE PLAY-TAMBOURINE. DANCE AND PLAY-LYRE.
EVERYTHING BREATH LIVE, GO-AHEAD PRAISE GOD.

Refrain: ME WANT PRAISE YOUR-rt NAME.
ME WANT SING ABOUT YOU-rt KIND.
GLORY, MY GOD HAVE GLORY.

2. MOUNTAIN AND VALLEY, RIVER AND SEA,
STAR, HEAVEN SCADS-OF, FISH DEEP WATER,
(2h)#ALL GOD CREATE PRAISE HIM-rt ADVANCED.

3. (<) MOTHER, (>) FATHER, (<) DAUGHTER, (>) SON.
(2h)#ALL GOD HIS-rt PEOPLE, (<) OLD, (>) YOUNG.
(2h)#ALL WISH DO GOD HIS-rt WILL. GO-AHEAD PRAISE.

Notes: SOUND-TRUMPET - (2h)[S] grasp a valveless trumpet at the mouht.

PLAY-LYRE - A lyre looks like a small harp and is played by plucking with the hands staying in place.

GO-AHEAD - (2h)[B^ dot], POs ><, FOs away, move forward in a jerk.

KIND - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs away, NDH is held close to heart and DH circles around it.

GLORY - (2h)[5], POs><, FOs away from signer, DH which is on top wiggles fingers while ascending. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Glory.

SCADS-OF - (2h)[5:], POs down, FOs away, move forward to show many things.

(2h)#ALL - (2h)[A], POs ><, FOs up, are held at shoulder height and move away while opening to (2h)[L].

CREATE - C-MAKE.

ADVANCED - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, move upwards arcing towards the signer. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Exalted.

WILL - WANT but shaken back and forth to show intensity.

Lyrics:

1. Praise with the trumpet, praise with the harp,
Praise with the timbrel, the dance and the lyre;
Let everything that has breath give praise to God.

Refrain: I want to praise your name.
I want to sing your goodness.
Glory, O God; Glory.

2. Mountains and valleys, rivers and seas,
Stars in the heavens and fish in the deep;
Let all creation give praise to God on high.

3. Mothers and fathers, daughters and sons,
All of God's people, the old and the young;
Let all who hunger to do God's will give praise.
