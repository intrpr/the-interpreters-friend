Song of the risen one - David Haas

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 19 March 2008, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

Refrain: (role play angel from rt) WHY YOU(plural) SEARCH-FOR ALIVE WITH DEAD?
HE-lf NOT HERE. GOD RAISE-UP HIM-lf HAVE LIFE NEW.
ALLELUIA-lf! ALLELUIA-rt!

1. COME-ON, WITNESS. YOU(plural) SEARCH-FOR HIM-lf, BUT GONE.
HE-lf RESURRECT AND LIVE WITH YOU(plural).
ALLELUIA-lf! ALLELUIA-rt!

2. TEARS, HE-lf SHOO-AWAY. PAIN NO-MORE.
CRYING AND MOURNING CAN END. KNOW-THAT HE-lf MAKE EVERYTHING NEW!
ALLELUIA-lf! ALLELUIA-rt!

3. WEAK BECOME STRONG, BLIND SEE NEW WAY.
GO-AHEAD ANNOUNCE (2h)#ALL NATION, MIRACLE FROM GOD!
ALLELUIA-lf! ALLELUIA-rt!

Notes: YOU(plural) - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

RAISE-UP - (2h)[B], POs down, FOs away, lower slightly, supinate, and rise up.

ALLELUIA!-lf ALLELUIA!-rt - Twist to lf and sign Alleluia! (CLAP-HANDS~CELEBRATE + open hands into (2h)[5] while lifting) and then twist to the rt and sign Alleluia! again.

COME-ON - (2h)[B^] move in circles pivoting at the wrists to beckon someone to do something.

RESURRECT - [V], PO up, FO away, pronates and hops onto the palm of [B], PO up, FO away. For an animated gif of this sign, see Valley Bible Church - ASL dictionary: Resurrection.

SHOO-AWAY - (2h)[B^], POs down, FOs > thing being shooed away, hands bend at wrist and then straighten to (2h)[B] as if shooing something away.

NO-MORE - (2h)[B], POs down, cross at wrists, then uncross moving horizontally and emphatically. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - don't.

KNOW-THAT - Starts like KNOW but twists and descends, looking almost like DON'T-KNOW except the hand twists down at the end.

GO-AHEAD - (2h)[B^ dot], POs ><, FOs away, move forward in a jerk.

(2h)#ALL - (2h)[A], POs ><, FOs up, are held at shoulder height and move away while opening to (2h)[L].

MIRACLE - GREAT WORK. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Miracle.

Lyrics:

Refrain: Why do you look for the living among the dead?
He is not here: he has been raised to new life!
Alelluia! Alleluia!

1. Come amd see! The one who you seek is no longer here:
Who is risen among you, who goes now before you alive!
Alleluia! Alleluia!

2. Every tear will be driven away; no more pain:
No more weeping and mourning, "Behold I make all things new!"
Alleluia! Alleluia!

3. Strength to the weak! New sight for the blind:
Go and tell all the nations the wondrous deeds of our God!
Alleluia! Alleluia!
