Notes:

KNOW-THAT - Starts like KNOW but twists and descends, looking almost like DON'T-KNOW except the hand twists down at the end.

COOL - [5], PO > NDS, holds thumb at midchest and wg.

REALLY-WANT - the sign WANT but shaken back and forth to show intensity.

BOW-DOWN - (2h)[B], POs away, FOs up, are held like the final position of GIVE-UP, then bend these arms twice while bowing at waist once.

THINK-DIFFERENTLY - (2h)[5:] grasp head at front and back and move as if twisting top of skull around 180 degrees.

MACHO - [S], PO up, rubs little fin

CONFRONT - (2h)[B], POs ><, FOs away, DH above NDH, hands moves away, then tilt until FOs are up.

SHAME-ON-YOU - [B^], PO > back, FO > NDS, moves away while brushing against cheek and ends with [B], PO up, FO away.

SICK-OF-IT - [open 8] touches forhead and then twists around to PO away, and moves away.

COME-ON - (2h)[B^] move in circles pivoting at the wrists to beckon someone to do something.

PITY-ON-YOU - [open 8], PO away, hand moves in vertical circles normal (perpendicular) to the chest.

NOTHING-TO-IT - (2h)[F], POs away, FOs up, hands move slightly and symetrically from side to side.

HEAD-BANG - (2h)[horns], POs away, FOs up, hands are held high and head nods violently a few times.
