EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

1. ||: (3x) INSIDE-SELF HAVE LIGHT. ME SHOW"all" BRIGHT*. :||
||: (3x) SHOW"all" BRIGHT*. :||

2. ||: (3x) EVERY PLACE ME GO"each", ME SHOW"all" BRIGHT*. :||
||: (3x) SHOW"all" BRIGHT*. :||

3. ||: (3x) ALL IN MY HOUSE. ME SHOW"all" BRIGHT*. :||
||: (3x) SHOW"all" BRIGHT*. :||

4. ||: (3x) ME NOT FORCE BRIGHT*, ITSELF NATURAL. :||
||: (3x) SHOW"all" BRIGHT*. :||

5. ||: (3x) OUTSIDE HAPPEN DARK, ME SHOW"all" BRIGHT*. :||
||: (3x) SHOW"all" BRIGHT*. :||

Notes: The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

INSIDE-SELF - The FT of [C] are held against the chest and stuff the FT of [O^] into the [C] twice.

LIGHT and BRIGHT are actually the same sign, so use the emphatic form (*).

ME - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

To make the song more visually interesting, make sure you understand what "all" and "each" mean. See Explanation of the glosssing system.
