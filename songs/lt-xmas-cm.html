EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

1. COME-ON-right CHRISTMAS, TELL-STORY. (<) EVERYDAY SHORT*, (>) WIND COLD*. COME-ON-right CHRISTMAS, SING BEAUTIFUL. (<) EVENING ARRIVE SOON, (>) ALL-NIGHT LONG.

2. COME-ON-right CHRISTMAS, YOUR-rt SPECIAL STAR (1h)GLOW-DOWN-ON-me. MAKE QUIET CITY SNOW BRIGHT. COME-ON-right CHRISTMAS, SET-UP"each" FOOD PLENTY. WE FEEL LOVE AGAIN, DREAM BECOME TRUE.

Notes: COME-ON-right - (2h)[B] move in circles pivoting at the wrists to beckon Christmas which is spatilized at the right.

(1h)GLOW-DOWN-ON-me - [5^], helf very high and palm points ti signer while fingers flutter.
