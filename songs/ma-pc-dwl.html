May peace dwell within our hearts - Rev. Arthur Foote II

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 10 January 2006, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

REALLY-WANT PEACE LIVE INSIDE-SELF,
PLUS UNDERSTANDING KEEP-IN-MIND.
REALLY-WANT COURAGE HELP-us US DETERMINED,
AND KISS-FIST TRUTH FOREVER GUIDE US.

Notes: REALLY-WANT - WANT but shaken back and forth to show intensity.

INSIDE-SELF - The FT of [C] are held against the chest and stuff the FT of [O^] into the [C] twice.

UNDERSTANDING - alt.UNDERSTAND.

KEEP-IN-MIND - [A dot], touches thumb to temple and twists slightly.

DETERMINED - Like STUBBORN, but with a more positive, but determined facial expression.

KISS-FIST - Kiss the dorsal side (back) of hand to show you loves something other than a person.

Lyrics:

May peace dwell within our hearts,
And understanding in our minds;
May courage steel our wills,
And the love of truth forever guide us.
