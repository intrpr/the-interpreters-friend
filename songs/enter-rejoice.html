EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

||: ENTER, CELELBRATE, COME-ON. :||

Refrain: TODAY WE HAPPY WILL. ENTER, CELELBRATE, COME-ON.

||: OPEN-MIND, ACCEPT SONG. :|| Refrain

||: OPEN-HEART (2h)#ALL HERE. :|| Refrain

||: DON'T SCARED WORLD CHANGE. :|| Refrain

||: ENTER, CELELBRATE, COME-ON. :|| Refrain

Notes: The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

COME-ON - (2h)[B^] move in circles pivoting at the wrists to beckon someone to do something.

(2h)#ALL - (2h)[A], POs ><, FOs up, move away while opening to (2h)[L].

DON'T - (2h)[B], POs down, cross at wrists, then uncross moving horizontally and emphatically. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - don't.

Lyrics:

Enter, rejoice and come in. (2x)

Refrain: Today wil be a joyful day. Enter, rejoice and come in.

Open your ears to the song. (2x) Refrain

Open your hearts, everyone. (2x) Refrain

Don't be afraid of some change. (2x) Refrain
