EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

ME CAN'T FORGET YOU. SWEET LONG-LIST.
ME CAN'T FORGET YOU.
(<) TOGETHER, (>) TWO-APART, DOESN'T-MATTER, STILL.
IDEA-LIKE SONG ABOUT LOVE FOREVER,
ME STOP VISUALIZE YOU, NEVER.
KNOW-THAT UP-TO-NOW NONE MEAN MORE. INSPIRED.

||: ME CAN'T FORGET YOU. SWEET LONG-LIST.
FROM-NOW-ON FOREVER, TWO-OF-US STAY me-CONNECTED-TO-you.
THAT MY REASON, HARD BELIEVE
SOMEONE LIKE YOU, ME CAN'T FORGET,
FEEL YOU CAN'T FORGET ME you-SAME-AS-me. :||

Notes: YOU - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

LONG-LIST - (2h)[O^], POs > signer, NDH - above and touches DH; both hands open to (2h)[5] while DH descends.

TWO-APART - (2h)[1], FOs up, touch at palms and then separate in an upward arc.

IDEA-LIKE - A blending where IDEA has a [Y] instead of an [I] in anticipation of LIKE which is signed smoothly right afterwards.

KNOW-THAT - Starts like KNOW but twists and descends, looking almost like DON'T-KNOW except the hand twists down at the end.

The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

(2h)#NO - Both hands sign NO but forcefully and close only once.

you-SAME-AS-me - (2h)[Y], POs ><, FOs up, move to and fro.
