Notes: TIME-PERIOD - [T], PO > NDS, FO up, travels in a vertical plane parallel to the NDH and makes contact with NDH [B], PO > DS, FO away. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Period - Time.

DRINK - Use the [bC] variant that implies alcohol.

PUNCH - [8] flicks middle finger into center of [C], PO > DS, FO away.

alt.HELLO"lololo" - (2h)[Bb] salutes alt. from respective temples with mouthing of "low low low" while face sweeps from NDS to DS as if greeting a number of people.

FRIENDS - (2h)[X] interlaces fingers and moves in a horizontal circle.

MEET++++ - (2h)[1], POs ><, brush against each other while moving from side to side and both hands slowly sweep from NDS to DS brushing a total of five times.

"WOW" - [5] is held very limply and shakes from side to side.

BERRY - [O^], twists at FT of [1], PO > singer, FO > DS.

HANG-IN-CLUSTER - [5^], PO up, FO away, suddenly ascends and stops abruptly.

OVERLOOK - [B], PO > signer, FO up, pass faces while descending from elbow and ending with FO > NDS.

Lyrics:

Have a holly, jolly Christmas;
It's the best time of the year
I don't know if there'll be snow,
but have a cup of cheer.
Have a holly, jolly Christmas;
And when you walk down the street
Say Hello to friends you know
and everyone you meet.

Oh, ho, the mistletoe
hung where you can see;
Somebody waits for you;
Kiss her once for me.
Have a holly jolly Christmas,
and in case you didn't hear,
Oh by golly, have a holly,
jolly Christmas this year.
