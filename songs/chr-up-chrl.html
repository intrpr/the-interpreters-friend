Notes: SAME'circle' - (2h)[Y], POs down, FOs away, circle horizontally. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - standard.

BLOW-ON-HAND - Bring head forward, close to palm and blow on it.

(2h)5wg-CL'float away' - Downturned hands move upwards.

COME-ON - (2h)[B^] move in circles pivoting at the wrists to beckon someone to do something.

CHARLEY'C taps chin' - I use this name sign because the musical is all about eating, but obviously you can make up your own name sign.

Q-M - Trace a question mark with your index finger. Index curls and uncurls during the trace.

GRIN - Trace a grin on your face with the index finger side of [Bb], PO down, FO > NDS.

IDEA~LIKE - a blending where IDEA has a [Y] instead of an [I] in anticipation of LIKE which - signed smoothly right afterwards.

INSIDE-SELF - The FT of [C] are held against the chest and stuff the FT of [O^] into the [C] twice.

KNOW-THAT - Starts like KNOW but twists and descends, looking almost like DON'T-KNOW except the hand twists down at the end.

TOY - T-PLAY. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - toy.

THUMBS-DOWN++, THUMBS-UP++ - (1h)[A dot] or (2h) are held with thumbs down and descend in steps, or thumbs up and ascend in steps.

SUPREME - (2h)[A], FOs away, DH starts below NDH and arcs around it until it is on top.

GO-TOWARDS-GOAL - like the sign GOAL but the DH moves in a vertical circle while going towards the goal to show a constant process.

HON.-IX - Use the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

PRETTY [SOON] - The sign for PRETTY is used here, even though it is not used in its literal sense.

SKY - using the FT of the upturned palm, point > sky and sweep the hand > DS. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - sky.

(2h)YOU'sweep down slowly' - Using the FT of the upturned palm, point > Charlie and sweep the hands downwards.
