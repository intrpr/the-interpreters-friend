Refrain: I SHOULD HAVE RIGHT LOVE ANY ONE I WANT.
I SHOULD ABLE GIRL-FRIEND HUG ANY TIME I WANT.
BUT I HAVE-NONE RIGHT. SUPPOSE WE-TWO REVERSE?
YOU WANT RIGHT LOVE SOMEONE YOU PICK, TOO.

REALLY-WANT HOLD-HANDS, DANCE, BUT YOU TOO-MUCH AFRAID.
IN-THE-CLOSET CONTINUE+, LIVE LIKE MASQUERADE.
NEVER INTEND TWO-OF-US GET-ALONG LIKE THAT.
WHY CAN'T YOU UNDERSTAND HOW THAT MAKE ME FEEL? Refrain

PEOPLE SEE WE-TWO LOVEY-DOVEY, SOME ANGERED, SOME DISGUSTED.
INSULT alt.FLY-AT-me, FRIENDS ANNOYED, BUT LIVE, LOVE WE MUST.
WHY WORLD PREJUDICED, WANT LABEL STEREOTYPE?
WHAT HAPPEN IF SOMEONE TEACH NEW-PARADIGM, THINK RIGHT? Refrain

I SHOULD HAVE RIGHT, SHOULD HAVE RIGHT,
SHOULD HAVE RIGHT. SUPPOSE WE-TWO REVERSE?
YOU WANT RIGHT LOVE SOMEONE YOU PICK, TOO.
I SHOULD HAVE RIGHT LOVE ANY ONE I WANT.

Notes: HAVE-NONE - (2h)[B], POs > signer move upwards past mouth and swing upwards and apart while mouth blows on hands as they pass.

REVERSE - (2h)[V], NDH PO up, DH down, FOs ><, hands reverse POs.

REALLY-WANT - WANT but shaken back and forth to show intensity.

HOLD-HANDS - Hold hands together as in WEDDING and swing them to and fro.

IN-THE-CLOSET - [X], PO down, FO away, taps on thumb of [C], PO > DS, FO away, with midsection and body crouches as if hiding.

MASQUERADE - Both hands move like SEEM in front of face.

INTEND - THINK MEAN.

GET-ALONG - (2h)[B^ dot], POs ><, FOs away, move forward in a jerk.

LOVEY-DOVEY - (2h)[ILY] rub against one another in circles, DHPO down, NDHPO up.

alt.FLY-AT-me - (2h)[1] alt. "fly" at face.

ANNOYED - [5], PO > face, FO up, curls fingers several times.

PREJUDICED - AGAINST.

LABEL - [H] moves palmar side of FTs across palm of NDH [B], PO away, FO up, as if applying a label.

STEREOTYPE - Index finger touches temple, then [open 8] touches FT to [S], PO down and the two hands circle together horizontally with th. For a QuickTime movie of the last sign, see ASL browser - standard.

NEW-PARADIGM - (2h)[C] grasp at top of head and move as if twist the top around 180 degrees.
