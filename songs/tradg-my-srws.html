EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

ME SAD #DO-DO? OFFER-TO GOD.
ME ASHAMED #DO-DO? OFFER-TO GOD.
SET-ASIDE THROW-OUT, ACCEPT HAPPY FROM MY LORD

ME SICK #DO-DO? GIVE-TO GOD.
ME PAIN #DO-DO? GIVE-TO GOD.
SET-ASIDE THROW-OUT, ACCEPT HAPPY FROM MY LORD

WE SAY, YES, LORD; YES, LORD; YES, YES, LORD.
YES, LORD; YES, LORD; YES, YES, LORD.
YES, LORD; YES, LORD; YES, YES, LORD. AMEN

ME OPPRESSED, NOT-YET CRUSHED. PERSECUTED, GOD NOT IGNORE-me.
ME FAIL, BUT DEVIL NOT DESTROY ME.
BLESS BEAT CURSE, WHY? HIS-rt,hi PROMISE CONTINUE.

HIS-rt,hi JOY BECOME MY STRENGTH.
MAYBE ALL-NIGHT SAD
BUT SUNRISE BRING-me HAPPY FROM GOD.

Notes: SET-ASIDE - (2h)[B], POs > DS, with DH higher, palms are pressed against imaginary wall that is pushed to one side.

THROW-OUT - (2h)[S] grasp an imaginary object and move > NDS to get some momentum, then throw it away by moving > DS and releasing it.
