EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

HAPPEN NIGHT ARRIVE,
MY WORLD BECOME DARK.
ONLY MOON SHINE-DOWN HELP ME SEE.
ME NOT FEEL AFRAID.
NO, NOT FEEL AFRAID,
IF YOU WILLING TWO-OF-US TOGETHER
SUPPORT ME.

||: SWEETHEART, SWEETHEART NEED TWO-OF-US TOGETHER SUPPORT ME.
NEED TWO-OF-US TOGETHER SUPPORT ME.
SUPPOSE SKY TWO-OF-US WATCH"over time"
SUDDEN-WRONG BREAK-DOWN STAR HAIL-DOWN
O-R MOUNTAIN
SHAKE AVALANCHE-INTO SEA.
ME REFUSE CRY; YES, ME REFUSE CRY.
ME NOT NEED UPSET,
IF TWO-OF-US TOGETHER
YOU SUPPORT ME. :||

HAPPEN YOU HAVE TROUBLE,
IF TWO-OF-US TOGETHER
ME SUPPORT YOU.

Notes: SHINE-DOWN - [O^] is held high PO > signer; hands open to [5^] while moving > signer.

The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

SWEETHEART - SWEET.

SUDDEN-WRONG - [Y], PO > NDS, FO up, twists until PO is > signer and strikes chin to show an unexpected (negative) result.

BREAK-DOWN - (2h)[5:] interlaced, POs ><, FTs move down while remaining interlaced so that the PO becomes down. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - break-down.

HAIL-DOWN - (2h)[F], POs ><, FOs away, hands alt. descend.

SHAKE - (2h)[5], POs down, FOs away, shake hands left to right antisymmetrically.

AVALANCHE-INTO - (2h)[S], change to [5] with wg to show avalanche going down the mountain.
