One name - Anonymous

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 23 October 2008, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

||: ONLY ONE* NAME UNDER HEAVEN
ABLE SAVE YOUR SOUL. :||

||: GOD FORGIVE OUR SIN. WE EXPERIENCE BAPTISM FINISH.
HOLY SPIRIT SAVE US. LAMB HIS-lf BLOOD WASH-BODY OUR SINS AWAY. :||

||: FREE, TRUE-BIZ FREE, MY FRIENDS.
LAMB HIS-lf BLOOD MAKE US FREE. :||

||: GOD MOVE THROUGH HERE PLACE.
GOD MOVE THROUGH HERE PLACE.
THIS WORLD, GOD CHANGE. EVIL, THROW-OUT. :||

Notes: The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

ONE* - PO > signer.

BAPTISM - (2h)[A dot], POs ><, FOs away, both hands are tipped to one side so that the thumbs point > NDS, then > upwards. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Baptize.

HOLY - H-CLEAN. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Holy - 1.

WASH-BODY - (2h)[A], rub palm sides in symmetric circles on chest.

SIN - (2h)[X] touch their respective sides of the mouth, descend to neutral space and circle antisymmetrically parallel to the chest. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Sin.

AWAY - (2h)[B], POs down, FOs away, nod hands down, then up, with a movement of the arms to show that something is being swept away.

TRUE-BIZ - TRUE + (2h)[S], POs down, FOs away, DH brushes from side to side across NDH wrist.

FRIENDS - Interlocked (2h)[X] move in a horizontal circle.

EVIL - (2h)[3], wg while moving repeatedly > forehead. For an animated gif of this sign, see Valley Bible Church - ASL dictionary: Satan.
