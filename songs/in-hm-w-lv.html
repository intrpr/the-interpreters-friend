Refrain: ||: WE LIVE, MOVE, DEPEND-God HIM. :||

GO-AHEAD CELEBRATE SOUND"pow". SHOW-God HAPPY. Refrain

EXPRESS (2h)HE LORD. Refrain

INFORM-God (2h)I-LOVE-YOU YOU. Refrain

GO-AHEAD DANCE. GOD LOOK-DOWN OH-I-SEE. Refrain

GO-AHEAD CELEBRATE SOUND"pow". SHOW-God HAPPY. Refrain

EXPRESS (2h)HE LORD. Refrain

INFORM-God (2h)I-LOVE-YOU YOU. Refrain

HALLELUJAH! Refrain

Notes: The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

DEPEND-God - Hands are held high and toward God.

GO-AHEAD - (2h)[B^ dot], POs ><, FOs away, move forward in a jerk.

SOUND"pow" - Touch ear with index finger then (2h)[S], PO down, FO away, move antisymmetrically from side to side while mouthing "pow". For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - thunder.
