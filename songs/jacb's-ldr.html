EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

||: WE CLIMB-LADDER J-A-C-O-B DREAM (or WE CLIMB-LADDER LEAD-TO HEAVEN) :||
#ALL-OF-US (<) BROTHER, (>) SISTER.

||: EVERY-DAY WE ADVANCED+. :||
#ALL-OF-US (<) BROTHER, (>) SISTER.

||: WE DANCE LIKE S-A-R-A-H TEACH-us US. :||
#ALL-OF-US (<) SISTER, (>) BROTHER.

||: GENERATION TEACH EACH-OTHER. :||
#ALL-OF-US (<) SISTER, (>) BROTHER.

||: WE CLIMB-LADDER J-A-C-O-B DREAM (or WE CLIMB-LADDER LEAD-TO HEAVEN) :||
#ALL-OF-US (<) BROTHER, (>) SISTER.

Notes: The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

CLIMB-LADDER - Hands mime grabbing onto successive rungs of a ladder.

#ALL-OF-US - [A], PO > NDS, FO up touches DS shoulder then [L^], PO > signer touches NDS shoulder.

LEAD-TO - LEAD moves up to "Heaven" not straight forward as usual.

ADVANCED+ - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, move upwards arcing towards the signer, and then arc up again even higher. For an animated gif of the sign this is based on, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Exalted.
