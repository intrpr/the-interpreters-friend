Shout to the Lord - Darlene Zschech

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

||: MY JESUS, MY SAVIOR,
LORD, HAVE-NONE EQUAL-TO-lf YOU-lf.
EVERYDAY MY LIFE, ME REALLY-WANT PRAISE YOU-lf.
YOUR-lf L-O-V-E AWESOME LIKE MIRACLE.
YOU-lf COMFORT, PROTECT ME.
YOU-lf FORTRESS MIGHTY.
ME REALLY-WANT EVERYTHING INSIDE-SELF
WORSHIP YOU-lf WITHOUT END.

PRAISE LORD LOUD, SING TO (2h)#ALL LIVE EARTH.
POWER ADVANCED, PRAISE OUR KING HON.-INDEX-lf.
MOUNTAIN BOW-DOWN, OCEAN CRASH-SHORE
WHEN YOU-lf ANNOUNCE YOUR-lf NAME.
ME SING JOY WHAT YOUR-lf HANDS CAN DO.
FOREVER ME LOVE YOU-lf, FOREVER ME ANNOUNCE
NOTHING EQUAL-TO-lf YOUR-lf PROMISE TO ME. :||

Notes: The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

SAVIOR - S-FREE AGENT.

HAVE-NONE - (2h)[B], POs > signer move upwards past mouth and swing upwards and apart while mouth blows on hands as they pass.

YOU - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

REALLY-WANT - WANT but shaken back and forth to show intensity.

AWESOME - (2h)[S], POs ><, FOs up are held at eyes with awed facial expression and the hands open and then close.

MIRACLE - WONDERFUL(1x) WORK. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Miracle.

COMFORT - Like COMFORTABLE but signed more slowly.

FORTRESS - (2h)[S], POs down, FOs ><, strike the whole length of the forearms together like TABLE then DH is held under NDH and the arms strike again.

MIGHTY - (2h)[S], POs > back of signer, FOs up, are held at NDS and fists descend, then repeat at DS.

INSIDE-SELF - The FT of [C] are held against the chest and stuff the FT of [O^] into the [C] twice.

WORSHIP - NDH is clasped over DH [S] and hands circle perpendicular to the chest. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Worship.

LOUD - Touch ear with index finger then (2h)[S], PO down, FO away, move antisymmetrically from side to side while mouthing "pow". For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - thunder.

(2h)#ALL - (2h)[A], POs ><, FOs up, are held at shoulder height and move away while opening to (2h)[L].

ADVANCED - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, move upwards arcing towards the signer. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Exalted.

BOW-DOWN - (2h)[B], POs away, FOs up, are held like the final position of GIVE-UP, then bend these arms twice while bowing at waist once.

CRASH-SHORE - (2h)[B], POs ><, FOs up, NDH is held still and DH smacks against it and moves up with wg to show waves crashing on the shore.

JOY - alt.HAPPY.

Lyrics:

||: My Jesus, My Savior
Lord there is none like You
All of my days, I want to praise
The wonders of Your mighty love
My Comfort, My Shelter
Tower of refuge and strength
Let every breath, all that I am
Never cease to worship You

Shout to the Lord, all the earth let us sing.
Power and majesty, praise to the King.
Mountains bow down and the seas will roar
At the sound of Your name.
I sing for joy at the work of Your hands
Forever I'll love you, forever I'll stand
Nothing compares to the promise I have in You! :||
