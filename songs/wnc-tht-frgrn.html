Whence that goodly fragrance flowing? - Traditional French carol, A. B. Ramsay (trans.)

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 28 December 2004, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

1. BEAUTIFUL SMELL, WAFT-UNDER-NOSE WHERE FROM?
MAKE OUR SENSES MESMERIZED DONE-FOR.
VERY-FIRST TIME SHEPHERD EXPERIENCE.
E-V-E-N SPRING NOT EQUAL INSPIRED.

2. LIGHT SHINE-DOWN STRONG, WHAT'S-UP?
NOT EXPECT SKY HAVE STAR SO-MUCH BRIGHT.
SOMETHING NEW NOW SEEM HAPPEN.
WHAT CAN CHANGE OUR SKY LIKE THAT?

3. BETHLEHEM! THERE-rt IN MANGER LIE"over time".
HE-rt PAY-OFF OUR DEBT. GO FIND HIM-rt.
HURRY! HURRY! GO FIND THERE-rt NOW.
WORSHIP YOUR SAVIOR BORN TODAY.

Notes: WAFT-UNDER-NOSE - [5], PO donw, FO > NDS, moves under nose > DS while wg and head travels a little when the smell reaches the nose.

SENSES MESMERIZED DONE-FOR - Middle FT of [open 8] travels up the chest from waist to top + [5] points its FT > the signer's face, FT wg and hand moves away from face with the face following after it and the hand closes to [S] + (2h)[5], POs down, FOs away, hands rise while swiveling rapidly at the wrists.

VERY-FIRST - [1], PO down, FO away, supinates like the sign FIRST, but strikes the thumb of NDH [A dot], PO > DS, FO away. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: First.

LIGHT SHINE-DOWN - (2h)[O^], POs away, FOs up, open to (2h)[5], while separating + [O^] is held high PO > signer; hands open to [5^] while moving > signer.

SO-MUCH - (2h)[5:], POs ><, FOs away, separate while "so much" is mouthed.

BETHLEHEM - "B" CITY. For an animated gif of this sign, see Valley Bible Church - ASL dictionary: Bethlehem.

THERE-rt - [B], PO up, FO > the thing to be honored.

MANGER - (2h)[5], FOs down interlace fingers and then separate while ascending in outward arcs.

PAY-OFF - [open 8] runs middle finger up palm (> wrist), changes to a [B], PO down, and then moves back across palm.

DEBT - [1^] taps FT against palm of [B], PO up, FO away. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Debt.

Lyrics:

1. Whence that goodly fragrance flowing,
Stealing our senses all away?
Never the like did come a blowing,
Shepherds, from flow'ry fields in May.

2. What is that light so brilliant,
Breaking here in the night across our eyes?
Never so bright the day-star waking
Started to climb the morning skies!

3. Bethlehem! There in manger lying
Find your redeemer, haste away!
Run ye with eager footsteps hieing!
Worship the Savior born today!
