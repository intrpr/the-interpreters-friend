EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

1. ||: HOLY SPIRIT, HOLY SPIRIT. COME-ON BRING YOUR-ctr FIRE. :||

Refrain: ||: COME-ON HOLY SPIRIT. ALLOW YOUR-ctr FIRE fire-FALL.
ALLOW YOUR-ctr FIRE fire-FALL. :||

2. ||: HOLY SPIRIT, HOLY SPIRIT, MAKE MY HEART PURE. :||

3. ||: HOLY SPIRIT, HOLY SPIRIT, CAUSE MY LIFE BURN-FORWARD. :||

Notes: The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

HOLY - H-CLEAN. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Holy - 1.

COME-ON - (2h)[B^] move in circles pivoting at the wrists to beckon someone to do something.

fire-FALL - (2h)[5], POs > signer, FOs up, hands descend from above.

PURE - like NICE but the DH moves backwards slightly on the palm, pauses, then moves forward rapidly to show intensity.

CAUSE - (2h)[S] are held at the chest as if holding a rope and move forward while opening to (2h)[5]. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - cause.

BURN-FORWARD - (2h)[5], POs > signer, FOs up, hands move forward with wg.

Lyrics:

1. ||: Holy Spirit, Holy Spirit come with your fire. :||

Let the fire fall. :||

2. ||: Holy Spirit, Holy Sprit purify my heart. :||

3. ||: Holy Spirit, Holy Spirit set my life on fire. :||
