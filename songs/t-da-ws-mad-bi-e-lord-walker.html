Notes: THE - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

COME-ON - (2h)[B^] move in circles pivoting at the wrists to beckon someone to do something.

REJOICE - CELEBBRATE.

GO-AHEAD - (2h)[B^ dot], POs ><, FOs away, move forward in a jerk.

SAVED - S-FREE.

ETERNAL - ALWAYS + DH produces STILL in the usual way, and the NDH produces STILL so that it moves back over the shoulder. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Eternal.

Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

GIFT-TO - (2h)[X], POs ><, FOs up, nod downwards at wrist to show that something is given as a gift.

MIRACLE - GREAT WORK. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Miracle.

RAISE-UP - (2h)[B], POs down, FOs away, lower slightly, supinate, and rise up.

TRIUMPH - WIN.

FOUNDATION - NDH [S], PO > signer, FO > DS, is held out with arm parallel to the chest and DH [S], PO > signer, strikes hand from below and then strikes elbow from below.

SAVIOR - S-FREE AGENT.

Refrain: This day was made by the Lord,
let us rejoice, let us be glad.
This day was made by the Lord,
let us rejoice in salvation.

1. I thank you, your love is eternal.
||: You have given me life. :||
I will proclaim the wonders you do!

2. Your hand raised me up in triumph.
||: You have given me life. :||
I will proclaim the wonders you do!

3. The stone which the builders rejected,
||: is the cornerstone. :||
I will proclaim the wonders you do!

4. You opened the gates of heaven.
||: You have given me life. :||
I will proclaim the wonders you do!

5. You are my strength and my Savior.
||: You have given me life. :||
I will proclaim the wonders you do!
