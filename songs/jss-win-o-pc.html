EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

Refrain: JESUS WINE BRING PEACE, BRING L-O-V-E. REALLY-WANT DRINK YOU-lf, TASTE YOU-lf WITH US. PROMISE FUTURE BRIGHT.

1. (role play) MY PATH CAN GUIDE (2h)YOU.
ME SAVE YOU WILL.
DRINK GOOD. DRINK AND LIVE.

2. (role play) (2h)YOU NOT NEED FEEL ALONE.
ME GO-WITH (2h)YOU.
DRINK GOOD. DRINK AND LIVE.

3. (role play) (2h)YOU WILL NEVER THIRST AGAIN.
ME FILL-UP (2h)YOU.
DRINK GOOD. DRINK AND LIVE.

4. (role play) WITH ME, (2h)YOU
LAUGH AND SING AGAIN WILL.
DRINK GOOD. DRINK AND LIVE.

5. (role play) (2h)YOU WILL LIVE WITHOUT FEAR FROM-NOW-ON.
PEACE GO-WITH (2h)YOU.
DRINK GOOD. DRINK AND LIVE.

6. (role play) ME COME EARTH HELP"all" LIVE FOREVER
ME GO-WITH (2h)YOU.
DRINK GOOD. DRINK AND LIVE.

7. (role play) (2h)YOU WILL RESSURECT.
LIVE NEW IN MY KINGDOM.
DRINK GOOD. DRINK AND LIVE.

8. (role play) ME YOUR ONLY TRUE SHEPHERD.
ME GUIDE (2h)YOU WILL.
DRINK GOOD. DRINK AND LIVE.

9. (role play) ME LIGHT DURING DARK.
ME SAVE (2h)YOU WILL.
DRINK GOOD. DRINK AND LIVE.

10. (role play) CRYING WILL END.
PAIN IN MY KINGDOM GONE.
DRINK GOOD. DRINK AND LIVE.

Notes: REALLY-WANT - WANT but shaken back and forth to show intensity.

YOU - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

PATH - Show curvy road.

FILL-UP - DH [B], PO down, rises until it slaps bottom of NDH, PO down. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - maximum.

FEAR - (2h)[5], POs away, FOs up, hands are held far from body and move back to body in a wavy pattern.

FROM-NOW-ON - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs away, DHFT touches knuckle side of NDHFT and moves away in a downward arc. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - from now on.

RESSURECT - [V], PO up, FO away, pronates and hops onto the palm of [B], PO up, FO away. For an animated gif of this sign, see Valley Bible Church - ASL dictionary: Resurrection.

KINGDOM - KING + [5], PO down, FO away, circles horizontally. For an animated gif of this sign, see Valley Bible Church - ASL dictionary: Kingdom.

SHEPHERD - SHEEP SUPERVISE AGENT.

Refrain: Jesus, wine of peace, wine of love, may we drink of you;
may we taste your presence, your promise, our future.

1. I will be the path that guides you, I will save you.
Drink well, drink and live.

2. You will never be alone, I am with you.
Drink well, drink and live.

3. You will never thirst again, I will fill you.
Drink well, drink and live.

4. You will laugh and sing again in my presence.
Drink well, drink and live.

5. You will live in fear no more, peace be with you.
Drink well, drink and live.

6. I have come that you may live, I am with you.
Drink well, drink and live.

7. You will rise and live anew in my kingdom.
Drink well, drink and live.

8. I will be your one true shepherd, I will guide you.
Drink well, drink and live.

9. I will be your light in darkness, I will save you.
Drink well, drink and live.

10. No more weeping, no more pain in my kingdom.
Drink well, drink and live.
