EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

WE BOW-DOWN-lf.
OUR CROWN~LAY-DOWN-lf
NEAR JESUS HIS-lf FEET.
GREAT MERCY AND LOVE, FIND WHERE?
NEAR JESUS HIS-lf FEET.

Refrain: ||: (3x) WE SHOUT HOLY++'ever higher levels'. :|| THAT THE-lf LAMB.

MY JESUS, ME LOVE YOU-lf.
ME KNOW YOU-lf NOW MINE.
TO YOU ME SURRENDER OFFER-UP.
MY FRIENDLY REDEEMER,
MY SAVIOR, THAT YOU-lf.
IF E-V-E-R ME LOVE YOU-lf,
MY JESUS, FEEL NOW.

Notes: BOW-DOWN - (2h)[B], POs away, FOs up, are held like the final position of GIVE-UP, then bend these arms twice while bowing at waist once.

CROWN~LAY-DOWN - (2h)[L:] (like BIG) place crown on head then move the hands as if to lay it as Jesus' feet.

The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

THE - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

REDEEMER - R-FREE AGENT.

SAVIOR - S-FREE AGENT.

My gracious redeemer,
