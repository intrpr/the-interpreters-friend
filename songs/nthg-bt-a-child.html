Notes: DESERT - DRY THEREABOUTS. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Desert.

REALLY-WANT - WANT but shaken back and forth to show intensity.

PRECIOUS - (2h)[5:], hold FTs by the sides of the chin and move down while maintaining contact and closing to (2h)[S].

THEY - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

MIRACLE - GREAT WORK. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Miracle.

WASH-tears - Palm sides of (2h)[A], wash under eyes.

TEARS - CRY.

WIPE-AWAY-tears - FTs of (2h)[B] curl as they wipe the tears > their respective sides.

EXHAUSTED - TIRED + [V], PO up, FO > NDS, is drawn with effort across the palm of [B], PO up, FO away.

LIGHT - (2h)[O^], POs away, FOs up, open to (2h)[5], while separating. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Light - 1.

ALL-AROUND-world - (2h)[A], POs down, FOs away, touch thumbs then open to (2h)[5:] while the outline travelling from the top of a sphere (the earth) to the bottom.

PRECIOUS - (2h)[5:], hold FTs by the sides of the chin and move down while maintaining contact and closing to (2h)[S].

SOUND - Touch ear with index finger then (2h)[S], PO down, FO away, move antisymmetrically from side to side while mouthing "pow". For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - thunder.

KIND - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs away, NDH is held close to heart and DH circles around it.

LOOK-AT-baby-in-arms - Hold NDH as in BABY and look at baby with DH [V].

AWED - (2h)[S], POs ><, FOs up are held at eyes with awed facial expression and the hands open and then close.
