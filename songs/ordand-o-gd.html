Ordained of God

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 4 September 2004, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

1. GOD ORDAIN PROPHET AND THEY-lf TAKE-OFF. THEY-lf REALLY-WANT (<) MONEY, (>) FAMOUS, NOT. CHRIST SUMMON, THEY-lf PAY-ATTENTION-above. ONLY WANT INSIDE-SELF OFFER-UP.

2. WE PREACH"each" GOSPEL, DON'T-WANT MISUNDERSTAND. WE HAPPY OUR SIN HE FORGIVE CAN. OUR HOME HEAVEN. IF SINNER HAVE WILLING SPIRIT ACCEPT CHRIST, LIVE NEW* CAN.

3. WE SUPPORT EVERY GROUP JUSTICE. WE USE OUR STRENGTH PROTECT THEIR-rt RIGHTS. GREEDY, DISCRIMINATE, GO-AHEAD WAR, GOD LOOK-DOWN-ON DISGUSTED, DAMN THEM-rt WILL.

4. TODAY WE GRADUATE, FINISH, PRAY #ALL-OF-US INTERACT CONNECT* FROM-NOW-ON. PROPHET THEIR-lf TRUTH AND JESUS HIS LIGHT SHOULD SHOW-TO-us PATH RIGHT, DIVERT-TO-lf~DIVERT-TO-rt (2h)SAY-#NO.

Notes: ORDAIN is [B] touches temple and then NDH [B], PO down. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - ordain.

TAKE-OFF - Intensive form of DEVELOP.

REALLY-WANT - WANT but shaken back and forth to show intensity.

INSIDE-SELF - Hold the FT of [C] against the chest and stuff the FT of [O^] into the [C] twice.

#ALL-OF-US - [A], PO > NDS, FO up touches DS shoulder then [L^], PO > signer touches NDS shoulder.

DIVERT-TO-lf~DIVERT-TO-rt - Like OFF-THE-POINT travelling first to one side, and then the other. It's a way of saying "to sin".
