EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

||: EVERY ONE LOOK-BACK REMEMBER. (4x) :||
||: LOOK-AT-me SEE MY LIFE IMPROVE (6x) :||
||: USED-TO-BE LOWLY, HE-ctr HELP ADVANCED. (4x) :||
||: LOOK-AT-me SEE MY LIFE IMPROVE (6x) :||
||: USED-TO-BE LOWLY, HE-ctr HELP ADVANCED. (4x) :||

USED-TO-BE THINGS ME DO, REFUSE AGAIN.
USED-TO-BE PLACE ME FREQUENT, REFUSE AGAIN.
USED-TO-BE THINGS ME SAY, REFUSE AGAIN.
USED-TO-BE WAY ME THINK, REFUSE AGAIN.

||: LOOK-AT-me SEE MY LIFE IMPROVE (4x) :||
||: USED-TO-BE LOWLY, HE-ctr HELP ADVANCED. (4x) :||

EVERYONE alt.SAY-#YES. alt.SAY-#YES.
||: LOOK-AT-me SEE MY LIFE IMPROVE (2x) :||

Notes: The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

LOOK-BACK - (2h)[H or V] are brought back over the shoulder, POs down, FOs back.

LOWLY - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, move downwards arcing towards the signer. This is the opposite of the sign ADVANCED. For an animated gif of ADVANCED, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Exalted.

HE - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

ADVANCED - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, move upwards arcing towards the signer. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Exalted.

FREQUENT - [Bb], PO > NDS, FO away, taps on [1], PO > DS, FO up, on thumb side.

alt.SAY-#YES - Both hands alt. perform the following, [Y], PO > face, FO up, approaches face and closes to an [S].
