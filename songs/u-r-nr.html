You are near - Reuben Morgan

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 4 April 2006, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

||: WE WORSHIP YOU-rt,hi AWED.
YOU-rt,hi LOVE US SO-MUCH, CRAZY-FOR.
WE CHANGE: OLD GLORY BECOME NEW GLORY,
OUR HEART GO-TOWARDS-GOAL YOU-rt,hi OUR GOD

NOW YOU-rt,hi TOGETHER US HERE PLACE.
WE PRAISE YOU-rt,hi HAPPEN?
NOW REALIZE YOU-rt,hi ADVANCED.
WHILE WE WORSHIP, ME BELIEVE,
YOU-rt,hi NEAR. YOU-rt,hi NEAR. :||

BLESS+, HONOR,
GLORY, POWER
||: (5 x) FOREVER, FOREVER. :||

WE WORSHIP YOU-rt,hi AWED.

Notes: The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

WORSHIP - NDH is clasped over DH [S] and hands circle perpendicular to the chest. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Worship.

YOU-rt,hi - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

AWED - (2h)[S], POs ><, FOs up are held at eyes with awed facial expression and the hands open and then close.

SO-MUCH - (2h)[5:], POs ><, FOs away, separate while "so much" is mouthed.

CRAZY-FOR - [5:], PO > singer, FO up, is shaken slightly from side to side.

GLORY - (2h)[5], POs><, FOs away from signer, DH which is on top wiggles fingers while ascending. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Glory.

GO-TOWARDS-GOAL - like the sign GOAL but the DH moves in a vertical circle while going towards the goal to show a constant process.

ADVANCED - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, move upwards arcing towards the signer. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Exalted.

BLESS+ - (2h)[O^], POs ><, held at mouth. Hands are brought downwards while opening to (2h)[5]. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Bless. This is repeated.

||: (5 x) FOREVER, FOREVER. :|| - The DH signs FOREVER, and with the final position held, the NDH signs the second half. This is repeated 5 times.
