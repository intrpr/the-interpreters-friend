EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

1. (<) SOMETIMES WE LAUGH,
(>) SOMETIMES WE CRY.
(<) SOMETIMES WE HOPE,
(>) SOMETIMES WE FEAR.
HAVE SO-MUCH WE SHARE,
TIME ARRIVE WE KNOW+
#ALL-OF-US ONE* WORLD.

2. MOON ONE*,
SUN SAME-EVERYWHERE,
EVERYONE KNOW SMILE
MEAN TWO-OF-US FRIEND.
(<) MAYBE MOUNTAIN HIGH,
(>) MAYBE OCEAN BIG, BUT
#ALL-OF-US ONE* WORLD.

Refrain: (<) #ALL-OF-US ONE* WORLD.
#ALL-OF-US ONE* WORLD.
(>) #ALL-OF-US ONE* WORLD.
OUR WORLD SAME-EVERYWHERE.

Notes: FEAR - (2h)[5], POs away, FOs up, hands are held far from body and move back to body in a wavy pattern.

SO-MUCH - (2h)[5:], POs ><, FOs away, separate while "so much" is mouthed.

#ALL-OF-US - [A], PO > NDS, FO up touches DS shoulder then [L^], PO > signer touches NDS shoulder.

Lyrics:

1. It's a world of laughter,
A world of tears.
It's a world of hopes,
And a world of fears.
There's so much that we share,
That it's time we're aware.
It's a small world after all.

2. There is just one moon,
And one golden sun,
And a smile means
Friendship to everyone.
Though the mountains divide,
And the oceans are wide,
It's a small world after all.

Refrain: It's a small world after all.
It's a small world after all.
It's a small world after all.
It's a small, small world.
