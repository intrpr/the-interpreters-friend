(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

GOD OFFER LIFE, SHOW-us HIS-ctr MIGHT.

Refrain: GIFT PROVE HE-ctr LOVE US.
GIFT PROVE HE-ctr LOVE US.

2. FAR-AWAY HEAVEN GOD SAY,
(role play God) PREFER LIVE, DIE WHICH?

LOVE HE-lf GIFT-TO-us HOW? HIS-lf BIRTH.

Notes: GIFT- (2h)[X], POs ><, FOs up, nod downwards at wrist to show that something is given as a gift.

IDEA~LIKE - A blending where IDEA has a [Y] instead of an [I] in anticipation of LIKE which is signed smoothly right afterwards.

CAUSE - (2h)[S] are held at the chest as if holding a rope and move forward while opening to (2h)[5]. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - cause.

MIGHT - (2h)[S], POs > back of signer, FOs up, are held at NDS and fists descend, then repeat at DS.

ALL - (2h)[B^], POs up, FOs ><, DH circles around NDH and lands on its palm. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Whole.

BRIGHT - (2h)[O^], POs away, FOs up, open to (2h)[5], while separating. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Light - 1.

GLORY - (2h)[5], POs><, FOs away from signer, DH which is on top wiggles fingers while ascending. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Glory.

HEAVEN - (2h)[B], POs down, FOs ><, hands are held high, circle around each other and then separate.

BLEND - (2h)[5:], POs > signer, are interlaced and FTs move down like a gear.

GIFT-TO-us - (2h)[X], POs ><, FOs up, hands tip back towards signer to show that something is given as a gift.

BIRTH - S-ENTER. Not to be confused with BORN. The child is BORN, the mother GIVE[s]-BIRTH.

PROPHET - PREDICT AGENT.

SHINE-DOWN - [O^] is held high PO > signer; hands open to [5^] while moving > signer.

LIFT-UP - (2h)[B], PO down are held high and swoop down while supinating and then rise up again as if helping something mature and the hands continue up and FTs are directed > Heaven.

Lyrics:

1. Gift of love, dawning bright,
Pierces through endless night:
God of life, sharing might,
All creation holding,
Earth and sky enfolding.

Refrain: Gift of love, love, love.
Gift of love, love, love.
Glory be, Lord, to thee,
God of salvation.

2. Thus he spoke from on high:
Choose to live or to die,
Love to give or deny --
Life and love now blending;
Love is life unending.

3. Then he came on the earth,
Gift of love from his birth;
Shared our life, gave us worth;
To himself inviting,
God with us uniting.

4. For our sake was he killed;
Once foretold, now fulfilled;
Love so great, be not stilled!
Beams of love he lifted,
And with life was gifted.
