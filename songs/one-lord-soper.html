EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

Refrain: HAVE ONE* LORD. HAVE ONE* FAITH.
HAVE ONE* HEAVENLY-FATHER RULE (2h)#ALL.
HIS-rt SON COME SAVE US. HAVE ONE* GOD INSIDE-SELF #ALL-OF-US.

1. THROUGH LORD WE BECOME ONE* BODY.
#ALL-OF-US, HIS-ctr PEOPLE HE-ctr LOVE.

2. WE ONE* SPIRIT HAVE LIFE NEW.
THROUGH LORD #ALL-OF-US UNITED.

3. HOPE LIVE INSIDE-SELF US HOMOGENEOUS.
HE-ctr SUMMON US LIVE FOREVER.

4. HAVE ONE* FOUNTAIN OFFER BORN NEW.
THAT BLESS US WATER LIVE NEW CAN.

5. WE SHOULD WORTHY HE-ctr SUMMON US.
WE SHOULD SEEK EACH-OTHER THROUGH OUR GOD.

Notes: FAITH - Like THINK TRUST but with elevated hands to show the trust is spiritual.

HEAVENLY-FATHER - (2h)[A], FOs up, DH - held against forehead, NDH close to but lower, both hands open to (2h)[5] while moving upwards. HEAVEN may be added to this. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Heavenly Father - 1.

(2h)#ALL - (2h)[A], POs ><, FOs up, are held at shoulder height and move away while opening to (2h)[L].

INSIDE-SELF - The FT of [C] are held against the chest and stuff the FT of [O^] into the [C] twice.

#ALL-OF-US - [A], PO > NDS, FO up touches DS shoulder then [L^], PO > signer touches NDS shoulder.

UNITED - COOPERATE.

HOMOGENEOUS - (2h)[Y], POs down, FOs away, circle horizontally. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - standard.

SUMMON - DH [B] taps NDH [B], PO down, FO > signer, then moves > signer while closing to an [A], PO away from signer, FO up, and moving up.

FOUNTAIN - WATER + (2h)[O^], press palms together, move upwards, open hands and move outwards like a spray.

BLESS - (2h)[O^], POs ><, held at mouth. Hands are brought downwards while opening to (2h)[5]. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Bless.

SUMMON - DH [B] taps NDH [B], PO down, FO > signer, then moves > signer while closing to an [A], PO away from signer, FO up, and moving up.

SEEK - SEARCH.

EACH-OTHER - (2h)[A dot], thumbs revolve around each other, DHFO > NDS, NDFO DS.

Lyrics:

Refrain: There is one Lord. There is one faith.
There is one Father of us all, and through his Son,
who came to save us, there is one God living in us all.

1. We are one body in the Lord;
we are all the people of his love.

2. We are one spirit of new life;
we are all united in the Lord.

3. There is one living hope in us;
we are called to everlasting life.

4. There is one fountain of rebirth;
blessing us with waters of new life.

5. Let us be worthy of his call;
let us seek each other in our God.
