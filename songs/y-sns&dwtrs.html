EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

Refrain: ALLELUIA-lf, ALLELUIA-rt, ALLELUIA-ctr.

1. (2h)YOU LORD HIS-lf,hi (<) SON, (>) DAUGHTER.
HE-lf,hi KING HAVE GLORY, KING WE WORSHIP.
TODAY HIMSELF-lf,hi CONQUER DEATH. Refrain

2. HAPPEN EARLY-MORNING SAD, GRAY.
THREE-OF-THEM-rt HOLY WOMAN GO-lf
SEE JESUS LAY INSIDE GRAVE. Refrain

3. CHEMICAL PURE THEY-rt BRING FROM HOME
TRADITION REQUIRE BODY PREPARE
THEY-rt READY POUR-ON HIS-lf SACRED BODY. Refrain

4. ANGEL WEAR WHITE THREE-OF-THEM-rt NOTICE.
ANNOUNCE, (role play to rt) HE-lf NOW RISEN GO-AWAY SEE.
HE-lf LEAVE, MEET (2h)YOU G-A-L-I-L-E-E. Refrain

5. THEY-rt INFORM-ctr PETER, INFORM-ctr J-O-H-N.
TWO-OF-THEM-ctr RUN WITNESS GRAVE.
BUT J-O-H-N FASTER, BEAT-ctr PETER. Refrain

6. SAME NIGHT HAPPEN WHILE APOSTLE FEAR.
IN G-A-L-I-L-E-E THEY-ctr STAY, DOOR-CLOSED LOCKED.
LORD THEY-ctr CHERISH VISIT THEM-ctr. Refrain

7. T-H-O-M-A-S HEAR WOMAN REPORT,
AND HE-ctr DOUBT STORY TRUE-BIZ.
BUT JESUS COME, MAKE HIM-ctr TRUST. Refrain

8. JESUS SAY (role play to rt) LOOK++-side MY SIDE.
MY HANDS, MY FEET, MY BODY SEE.
DON'T DOUBT, COME-ON TRUST ME. Refrain

9. HAPPEN T-H-O-M-A-S WITNESS HIS-lf SIDE
TRUTH HE-ctr CAN'T CONTINUE DENY.
(role play to lf) ME BELIEVE YOU-lf MY LORD, MY GOD. Refrain

10. GOD BLESS (2h)YOU BETTER. WHY? NOT-YET SEE,
YOUR LORD IN FLESH BUT STILL BELIEVE.
ETERNAL LIFE GOD alt.GIFT"each" (2h)YOU. Refrain

11. COME-ON NOW PRAISE LORD ADVANCED.
MAGNIFY HIS-lf,hi NAME HERE WORLD.
TODAY WE CELEBRATE HE-lf,hi RESURRECT. Refrain

12. HIS-lf,hi MERCY-ON-us US OVERFLOW.
PEOPLE AND ANGEL WORSHIP HIM-lf,hi.
WE RECOGNIZE HIS-lf,hi GLORY FROM-NOW-ON FOREVER. Refrain

Notes: ALLELUIA! - CLAP-HANDS~CELEBRATE + open hands into (2h)[5] while lifting.

(2h)YOU - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

GLORY - (2h)[5], POs><, FOs away from signer, DH which is on top wiggles fingers while ascending. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Glory.

WORSHIP - NDH is clasped over DH [S] and hands circle perpendicular to the chest. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Worship.

CONQUER - (2h)[S], DPO > NDS, NDPO down, FOs away, are held with DH on top of NDH and DH strikes NDH at wrist while index and middle fingers shoot out suddenly.

HOLY - H-CLEAN. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Holy - 1.

LAY - [V], PO up, FO away, is laid in the palm of [B], PO up, FO > DS, and both hands move in a circle normal (perpendicular) to the chest.

PURE - like NICE but the DH moves backwards slightly on the palm, pauses, then moves forward rapidly to show intensity.

PREPARE - PLAN.

POUR-ON - [A dot], holds thumb down and moves in a circle over what is being poured onto.

SACRED - S-NICE.

ANGEL - (2h)[B^] touch FTs to shoulders, then arms extended and bent move slightly as if flying gently.

GO-AWAY - [5^], PO > signer, FO up, moves away while closing to [O^].

WITNESS - [1] touches under eye while head moves down.

APOSTLE - FOLLOW AGENT. For an animated gif of this sign, see Valley Bible Church - ASL dictionary: Apostle.

FEAR - (2h)[5], POs away, FOs up, hands are held far from body and move back to body in a wavy pattern.

TRUE-BIZ - TRUE + (2h)[S], POs down, FOs away, DH brushes from side to side across NDH wrist.

LOOK++-side - [V] points FT at side while mouthing "looklooklook".

SIDE - Run palm of [B] down side referred to.

DON'T - (2h)[B], POs down, cross at wrists, then uncross moving horizontally and emphatically. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - don't.

COME-ON - (2h)[B^] move in circles pivoting at the wrists to beckon someone to do something.

DENY - (2h)NOT++.

BLESS - (2h)[O^], POs ><, held at mouth. Hands are brought downwards while opening to (2h)[5]. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Bless.

FLESH - SKIN.

alt.GIFT"each" - (2h)[X], POs ><, FOs > the recipents, hands move together from NDS to DS.

ADVANCED - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, move upwards arcing towards the signer. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Exalted.

MAGNIFY - (2h)[5], POs ><, FOs away, separate.

RESURRECT - [V], PO up, FO away, pronates and hops onto the palm of [B], PO up, FO away. For an animated gif of this sign, see Valley Bible Church - ASL dictionary: Resurrection.

MERCY-ON-us - (2h)[open 8], POs > signer, hands move in vertical circles normal (perpendicular) to the chest. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Mercy - 2.

OVERFLOW - [5], PO down, FO away, covers top of [S], PO > DS, FO away, and FT wiggle as the hand moves away, like a cup overflowing. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - overflow.

FROM-NOW-ON - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs away, DHFT touches knuckle side of NDHFT and moves away in a downward arc. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - from now on.

Lyrics:

English translation

Refrain: Alleluia, Alleluia, Alleluia.

1. Ye sons and daughters of the Lord,
the King of glory, King adored,
this day Himself from death restored.

2. All in the early morning gray
went holy women on their way,
to see the tomb where Jesus lay.

3. Of spices pure a precious store
in their pure hands these women bore,
to anoint the sacred Body o'er.

4. The straightaway one in white they see,
who saith, "seek the Lord: but He
is risen and gone to Galilee."

5. This told they Peter, told John;
who forthwith to the tomb are gone,
but Peter is by John outrun.

6. That self-same night, while out of fear
the doors where shut, their Lord most dear
to His Apostles did appear.

7. But Thomas, when of this he heard,
was doubtful of his brethren's word;
wherefore again there comes the Lord.

8. "Thomas, behold my side," saith He;
"My hands, My feet, My body see,
and doubt not, but believe in Me."

9. When Thomas saw that wounded side,
the truth no longer he denied;
"Thou art my Lord and God!" he cried.

10. Oh, blest are they who have not seen
their Lord and yet believe in Him!
eternal life awaiteth them.

11. Now let us praise the Lord most high,
and strive His name to magnify
on this great day, through earth and sky:

12. Whose mercy ever runneth o'er;
Whom men and Angel hosts adore;
to Him be glory evermore.

Latin original

R: Alleluia, Alleluia, Alleluia.

1. O filii et filiae,
Rex caelestis, Rex gloriae
morte surrexit hodie.
R.

2. Ex mane prima Sabbati
ad ostium monumenti
accesserunt discipuli.
R.

3. Et Maria Magdalene,
et Iacobi, et Salome
Venerunt corpus ungere
R.

4. In albis sedens angelus
praedixit mulieribus:
In Galilaea est Dominus.
R.

5. Et Ioannes apostolus
cucurrit Petro citius,
monumento venit prius.
R.

6. Discipulis astantibus,
in medio stetit Christus,
dicens: Pax vobis omnibus.
R.

7. Ut intellexit Didymus
quia surrexerat Iesus,
remansit fere dubius.
R.

8. Vide Thoma, vide latus,
vide pedes, vide manus,
noli esse incredulus.
R.

9. Quando Thomas vidit Christum,
pedes, manus, latus suum,
dixit: Tu es Deus meus.
R.

10. Beati qui non viderunt
et firmiter crediderunt;
vitam aeternam habebunt.
R.

11. In hoc festo sanctissimo
sit laus et iubilatio:
benedicamus Domino.
R.

12. Ex quibus nos humillimas
devotas atque debitas
Deo dicamus gratias.
R.
