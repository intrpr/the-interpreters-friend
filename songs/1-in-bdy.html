EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

Refrain: OUR BODY, HEART, MIND ONLY-ONE. LOVE CAUSE US UNITED. PRAY++ CHRIST LOVE US THROUGH EUCHARIST. #ALL-OF-US SHARE BREAD AND WINE.

Notes: CAUSE - (2h)[S] are held at the chest as if holding a rope and move forward while opening to (2h)[5]. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - cause.

UNITED - COOPERATE.

EUCHARIST - [F], PO > signer's face, FO up, makes the sign of the cross at the mouth.

#ALL-OF-US - [A], PO > NDS, FO up touches DS shoulder then [L^], PO > signer touches NDS shoulder.

Lyrics:

Refrain: One in body, heart and mind, we are one by love united. Christ be love within this sign, shared for all as bread and wine.
