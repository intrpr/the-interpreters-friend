Notes: COME-ON - (2h)[B^] move in circles pivoting at the wrists to beckon someone to do something.

GO-TOGETHER - (2h)[A], FOs away, hold palms together and move back and forth.

DON'T - (2h)[B], POs down, cross at wrists, then uncross moving horizontally and emphatically. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - don't.

SAY-#NO-TO-me - [3], PO > face, FO up, moves > face and closes like NO.

FAR-AWAY - [1], twists while moving away with tongue shuttling from side to side. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Far.

PART - (2h)[1], POs down, FOs away, at first move together but shortly move diagonally > their sepective sides.

WOW++ - Shake [5] with PO > signer.
