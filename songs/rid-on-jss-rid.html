Notes: RIDE-HORSE - [V], FO down, straddles, NDH [Bb], PO > DS, FO away, as if straddling an animal's body.

JORDAN - [L], FO up, touches thumb to forehead. For a film of this sign, click on Jordan - المملكة الأردنية الهاشمية. HE - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

CROSS - [C], PO away, FO up, traces cross by moving down, then across.

CONQUER - (2h)[S], DPO > NDS, NDPO down, FOs away, are held with DH on top of NDH and DH strikes NDH wrist on dorsal side (from above) and nods while staying at wrist.

THRONE - SIT + (2h)[B:], POs down, FOs away, behave as if they were on the arms of a throne.

RAISE-UP - (2h)[B], POs down, FOs away, lower slightly, supinate, and rise up.

HEAVEN - (2h)[B], POs down, FOs ><, hands are held high, circle around each other and then separate.

HAVE-MERCY-ON-us - (2h)[open 8], POs > signer, hands move in vertical circles normal (perpendicular) to the chest. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Mercy - 2.

JERUSALEM - [B with thumbs flush with other fingers but not folded into palm], touches mouth and then pronates.

PALM-TREE - NDH [5^] grabs the elbow of [3^] and the DH which is held straight up, twists at the wrist.

BRANCH - (2h)[1], POs down, FOs away, are held along side one another and touching. The DH moves away and branches out to the DS, while the NDH does not move.

SPREAD-OUT - Looks like SPREAD but with POs up, as if spreading the palm branches on the ground.

BLESS - (2h)[O^], POs ><, held at mouth. Hands are brought downwards while opening to (2h)[5]. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Bless.

HOLY - H-CLEAN. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Holy - 1.

LIBERATED - Starts like PRESSURE, DH [B], PO down, FO away, push down slightly on [S], PO > DS, FO away, then the DH is brought upwards suddenly and the signer mouths "off".

CALVARY - MOUNTAIN + POSITIVE held high as if it were a cross on top of the mountain just indicated.

INSIDE-SELF - The FT of [C] are held against the chest and stuff the FT of [O^] into the [C] twice.
