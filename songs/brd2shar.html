Notes: The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

FEAST - (2h)alt.EAT. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Feast.

PLENTY - (2h)[B], NDH PO up, FO away, DH touches thumb side to elbow end of ND arm and then arcs upwards and comes down to touch ND FT, indicating a pile of something.

ANYONE - [A dot], PO > NDS, FO up, twists rapidly at the wrist while moving in a horizontal circle.

GRACE - [O^] or [S], held high and PO > Heaven, supinates and opens to [5] while being brought towards head. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Grace.

MERCY - (2h)[open 8], POs > people who are receiving mercy, hands move in vertical circles normal (perpendicular) to the chest.

SORROW - SAD+.

JUSTICE - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, tap FT while moving in a horizontal circle.

JOURNEY - (2h)[V:], PO down, FO away, travels away in a spiral path.

KINDNESS - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs away, NDH is held close to heart and DH circles around it.

COMPASSION - HEART + (2h)[5^] descend slightly twice while closing to (2h)[O^] but hands close to heart.

UNSURE - (2h)[S], POs down, FOs away, hands alt. rise and fall.

LOOK-BACK - (2h)[H or V] are brought back over the shoulder, POs down, FOs back.

PARDON - Sign EXCUSE, switch dominance and sign EXCUSE again.

PAY-OFF - [open 8] runs middle finger up palm (> wrist), changes to a [B], PO down, and then moves back across palm.

SIN - (2h)[X] touch their respective sides of the mouth, descend to neutral space and circle antisymmetrically parallel to the chest. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Sin.

DEBT - [1^] taps FT against palm of [B], PO up, FO away. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Debt.

"ROOM" - Not as in "bedroom" but as in "elbow room". (2h)[S], elbows are pointed out somewhat and then pivot upwards twice, almost like "CHICKEN" (cowardly) but fists do not touch the body.

NEGLECTED - Index finger of [4] touches nose and moves in an outward arc that lands the little finger against the chest.

REJECT - Hands start like the final position of STOP and DH slides > DS while pronating.

EXCLUDE - [8], PO down is held in palm of NDH [B], and the middle finger is flicked towards who is being excluded.

(2h)#ALL - (2h)[A], POs ><, FOs up, are held at shoulder height and move away while opening to (2h)[L].

SENIOR - Like OLD but the hand passes the chin from the NDS and moves slightly upwards.
