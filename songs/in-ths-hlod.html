EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

HERE PLACE WE HONOR"all". NOW TIME-PERIOD HOLY. WE CHERISH COMMUNITY. REALLY-WANT ACCEPT LIFE SHORT BUT SWEET.

Notes: TIME-PERIOD - [T], PO > NDS, FO up, travels in a vertical plane parallel to the NDH and makes contact with NDH [B], PO > DS, FO away.

HOLY - H-CLEAN. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Holy - 1.

REALLY-WANT - the sign WANT but shaken back and forth to show intensity.

Lyrics:

In this hallowed place
And in this holy hour
And with this beloved company,
May we learn to embrace
The limits of life.
