THANKS, LORD; THANKS, LORD (vamp)
YOUR GRACE AND MERCY-ON-us (vamp)

Notes: The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

GRACE - [O^] or [S], held high and PO > Heaven, supinates and opens to [5] while being brought towards head. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Grace.

(2h)#NO - The emphatic single movement form of "no" is used.

TAKE-CARE-OF-me - (2h)[K], FOs > signer, cross at wrist and circle perpendicular to chest.

MERCY-ON-us - (2h)[open 8], POs > signer, hands move in vertical circles normal (perpendicular) to the chest. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Mercy - 2.

(vamp) - This means that the choir and soloist freely repeat portions from the lyrics and generally stretch out the words with obligati (highly ornamented notes).
