Notes: MATURE - [M], PO diagonally away, FO up, slide up palm of [B], PO diagonally away, FO up.

ATTRACTED - [C], PO > NDS, FO up, is held in front of face and moves away while closing to an [S].

#BACK - The [B] hand transitions to a [C] and then a [K] while moving away from signer.

KNOW-THAT - Starts like KNOW but twists and descends, looking almost like DON'T-KNOW except the hand twists down at the end.

INSIDE-SELF - The FT of [C] are held against the chest and stuff the FT of [O^] into the [C] twice.

IDEA~LIKE - a blending where IDEA has a [Y] instead of an [I] in anticipation of LIKE which - signed smoothly right afterwards.

SUN PULL-ON-lf COMET - [C], strikes thumbside around eyesocket, then look down > left and mime pulling on a rope once, then cross (2h)[S] at wrists and move > right while DH changes to a [5wg] to show tail.

alt.5:-CL - (2h)[5:], alt. descend while hands move forward to show stones in a river.

FROM-NOW-ON - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs away, DHFT touches knuckle side of NDHFT and moves away in a downward arc.

PART - (2h)[1], POs down, FOs away, at first move together but shortly move diagonally > their sepective sides.

LEAVE@heart - (2h)[5], strikes sides of heart with FTs.

IMPRESSION - DH thumb is held against palm of [B] as in IMPRESS but moves > signer twice,.

NAVIGATE - Hands held like BOAT move away in a curvy pattern.

BIG-QUESTION - like the sign QUESTION-MARK but both hands and all fingers (not the thumb) form a symmetric shape that - a question mark in the right hand and a reversed question mark in the left hand.

The signs between "||:" are repeated.
