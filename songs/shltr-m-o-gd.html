EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

Refrain: PROTECT ME IDEA~LIKE FORTRESS, GOD. YOUR-rt WINGS HIDE ME; SUNSHINE STRONG, NOT HURT. MY HOPE DEPEND-ON-rt YOU-rt.

1. HAPPEN ENEMY SURROUND-me, PLEASE PLACE-ABOVE-them ME, TOUCH CAN'T. IF ME SUMMON YOU-rt, PLEASE ATTEND-TO-me.

2. IDEA~LIKE MOTHER BIRD TAKE-CARE-OF HER-lf CHILDREN. THEY-rt ASSEMBLE, MOTHER FOLD-WINGS-OVER. PLEASE PROTECT ME SAME-AS-rt.

3. KNOW-THAT ME WALK PLACE DARK, CROWDED, 1-CL'walk crooked path', YOU-rt IGNORE-me ME NEVER. SUPPORT++.

Notes: ME - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

IDEA~LIKE - a blending where IDEA has a [Y] instead of an [I] in anticipation of LIKE which is signed smoothly right afterwards.

FORTRESS - (2h)[S], POs down, FOs ><, strike the whole length of the forearms together like TABLE then DH is held under NDH and the arms strike again.

WINGS - (2h)[B] flutter like wings.

SURROUND-me - (2h)[1], POs > signer, FOs up, start with hands touching and move symmetrically in a semi-circle to show that signer is surrounded.

PLACE-ABOVE-them - DH [S], PO > NDS, FO away, is held below downturned palm, and DH moves so that it is then above the NDH.

ATTEND-TO-me - PAY-ATTENTION-TO is directed towards signer.

ASSEMBLE - (2h)[S], FO > signer, move > signer while opening to (2h)[5wg].

FOLD-WINGS-UNDER - (2h)[B] are folded with FOs down rather than up as in REST.

KNOW-THAT - Starts like KNOW but twists and descends, looking almost like DON'T-KNOW except the hand twists down at the end.

CROWDED - (2h)[S] press wrists together and twist like CHANGE with hunched shoulders and "zz".

1-CL'walk crooked path' - [1], PO away, FO up, moves away from signer in a crooked path.

Lyrics:

Refrain: Shelter me, O God; hide me in the shadow of your wings. You alone are my hope.

1. When my foes surround me, set me high above their reach. Hear me when I call your name.

2. As a mother gathers her young beneath her care, gather me into your arms.

3. Though I walk in darkness, through the needle's eye of death, you will never leave my side.
