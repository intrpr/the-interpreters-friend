Refrain: ||: SOME-DAY HAVE,
SOMEDAY HAVE,

2. ME FOOL-AROUND, GET-AROUND
ALL-ALONG.
ME GROW-UP, ALL-ALONG.
SOME LABEL ME FREE,

3. (role play Jesus) ALLOW MY LOVE SEND WARMTH,
ALL YOUR TEARS DISSOLVE DRY.
DON'T FEAR. WHY? ME GO-WITH YOU.
(role play end) ME WILL NOT FEAR, NOT FEAR. WHY? YOU-lf WITH ME HERE. Refrain

Notes: MERGE-WITH - MAINSTREAM.

The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

SOMEDAY - WILL/FUTURE but fingers wg as the hand moves forward.

FOOL-AROUND - [1], PO down, FO away, moves in a wavey pattern as it passes under NDH [B], PO down, FO away.

GET-AROUND - (2h)[1], POs > signer, circle around each other while both hands move > DS.

ALL-ALONG - (2h)[1], POs down, FOs away, fingers run up the chest somewhat slowly on DS until POs are > signer, and FOs are up. Also the tongue - shuttled from side to side.

LABEL - [H] moves palmar side of FTs across palm of NDH [B], PO away, FO up, as if applying a label.

WILD - (2h)[4], POs down, FOs ><, start at temples and alt. spiral outwards.

DON'T - (2h)[B], POs down, cross at wrists, then uncross moving horizontally and emphatically. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - don't.

FEAR - (2h)[5], POs away, FOs up, hands are held far from body and move back to body in a wavy pattern.

YOU - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.
