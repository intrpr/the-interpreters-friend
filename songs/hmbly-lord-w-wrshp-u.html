EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

1. WE WORSHIP YOU-lf HUMBLE, LORD,
OUR ETERNAL KING.
YOU-lf DIE GIFT-TO-us US LIFE,
PAY-ATTENTION WHILE WE SING.

Refrain: JESUS, GOD AND LORD RULE (2h)#ALL.
COME-TO US, WE PRAY.
MEAN WE BECOME UNITED THROUGH YOUR-lf LOVE
AND CONTINUE SAME WAY EVERYDAY.

2. JESUS LORD, WE OFFER YOU-lf
EVERYTHING WE DO TODAY.
PRAY WE LIVE OUR LOVE FOR YOU-lf
AND YOUR WILL OBEY.

3. LORD FORGIVE US OUR alt.MISTAKE.
LIKE WE FORGIVE OTHERS TOO.
REALLY-WANT STRIVE WITH ALL OUR SOUL
LIVE LIKE PROPER CHRISTIAN FROM-NOW-ON.

4. EACH SOUL WE MEET WE SEE YOU-lf THERE-ctr.
OUR ETERNAL GOAL, EVERYTHING WE DO, SAME-"in circle".

Notes: WORSHIP - NDH is clasped over DH [S] and hands circle perpendicular to the chest. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Worship.

YOU - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

ETERNAL - ALWAYS + DH produces STILL in the usual way, and the NDH produces STILL so that it moves back over the shoulder. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Eternal.

GIFT-TO-us - (2h)[X], POs ><, FOs up, hands tip back towards signer to show that something is given as a gift.

RULE - CONTROL.

(2h)#ALL - (2h)[A], POs ><, FOs up, are held at shoulder height and move away while opening to (2h)[L].

WILL/REALLY-WANT - WANT but shaken back and forth to show intensity.

- Hands are oriented like the sign GOAL but the DH moves in a vertical circle as if trying to get to the goal, but not yet successful.

ALL - (2h)[B^], POs up, FOs ><, DH circles around NDH and lands on its palm. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Whole.

PROPER - (2h)[1], POs ><. FOs away, DH circles and hits the top of NDH at the bottom of its cycle.

FROM-NOW-ON - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs away, DHFT touches knuckle side of NDHFT and moves away in a downward arc. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - from now on.

ETERNAL - ALWAYS + DH produces STILL in the usual way, and the NDH produces STILL so that it moves back over the shoulder. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Eternal.

SAME-"in circle" - (2h)[Y], POs down, FOs away, circle horizontally. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - standard.

Lyrics:

1. Humbly Lord we worship you, our eternal King.
You who died to give life, hear us as we sing.

Refrain: Jesus, God and Lord of all, come to us, we pray.
Thus united in your love, may we live this day.

2. Jesus, Lord, we offer you every act this day.
May we live our love for you and your will obey.

3. Lord forgive us all our faults, others we forgive.
May we strive with all our souls, Christian lives to live.

4. May we love you in each soul and each soul in you;
One in our eternal goal, one in all we do.
