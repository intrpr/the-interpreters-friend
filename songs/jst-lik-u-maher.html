EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

1. REMIND-heart MY HEART, REMIND-soul MY SOUL.
YOU-rt LORD MAKE ME WHOLE.
NOT-NEED WANDER FULL FEAR. WHY?
YOU-rt MY GOD, MY LORD AND LOVER.
MY SOUL BURN, REALLY-WANT ONLY YOU-rt.

Refrain: ME WANT LIVE HOLY, EXACT LIKE YOU-rt.
ME WANT GO PLACE YOU-rt LEAD"over time".
HAPPEN YOUR-rt TRUTH DANGEROUS, STILL GO-AHEAD.
WANT FALL-IN-LOVE YOU-rt MORE-AND-MORE.

2. HEAVENLY-FATHER, LIFT-UP ME.
NONE HERE WORLD CAN EQUAL YOU-rt.
ME APPROACH YOU-rt WHILE DANCE
#ALL-FIVE SENSE OVERFLOW YOUR-rt GLORY.
BECOME MY ONLY DESIRE LIKE FIRE OVERWHELM.

Notes: REMIND-heart/soul- Tap on heart/center of chest with FT of [B^].

YOU - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

WHOLE - (2h)[B^], POs up, FOs ><, DH circles around NDH and lands on its palm. For a QuickTime file of COMPLETE, see ASL Pro: Whole.

FEAR - (2h)[5], POs away, FOs up, hands are held far from body and move back to body in a wavy pattern.

REALLY-WANT - WANT but shaken back and forth to show intensity.

HOLY - H-CLEAN. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Holy - 1.

GO-AHEAD - (2h)[B^ dot], POs ><, FOs away, move forward in a jerk.

MORE-AND-MORE - MORE is repeated with a vertical circular motion parllel to the chest.

HEAVENLY-FATHER - (2h)[A], FOs up, DH is held against forehead, NDH close to but lower, both hands open to (2h)[5] while moving upwards. HEAVEN may be added to this. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Heavenly Father - 1.

LIFT-UP - (2h)[B], PO down are held high and swoop down while supinating and then rise up again as if helping something mature and the hands continue up and FTs are directed > Heaven.

APPROACH - (2h)[B], FOs up, DH PO away, ND is held in front of NDH and palm faces it, then both hands approach one another slightly.

#ALL-FIVE - NDH [5], PO > DS, FO away, DH [A], PO > signer, descends while opening to an [L].

SENSE - [open 8] is drawn up the chest in one long movement. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - sense (1).

OVERFLOW - [5], PO down, FO away, covers top of [S], PO > DS, FO away, and FT wiggle as the hand moves away, like a cup overflowing. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - overflow.

GLORY - (2h)[5], POs ><, FOs away from signer, DH which is on top wiggles fingers while ascending. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Glory.

DESIRE - WISH.

Lyrics:

1. Revive my heart, renew my soul.
In you O Lord, I am made whole.
No more in fear will I wander,
Cause You're my God, my Lord and lover.
Like a burning fire, be my one desire.

Refrain: I want to be holy just like You,
I want to go where you lead me to.
With reckless abandon in Your truth,
I want to fall deeper in love with You.

2. Oh lift me up, God and Father.
In all this world there is no other.
I'm dancing in Your holy presence,
And Your glory fills all my senses.
Like a burning fire, be my one desire.
