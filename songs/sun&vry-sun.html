EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

1. ||: (3 times) SOON*, YES VERY SOON*, WE WILL SEE OUR KING. :|| HALLELUJAH! HALLELUJAH! WE WILL SEE OUR KING.

2. ||: (3 times) HAVE-NONE CRY+ THERE, WE WILL SEE OUR KING. :|| HALLELUJAH! HALLELUJAH! WE WILL SEE OUR KING.

3. ||: (3 times) HAVE-NONE DIE THERE, WE WILL SEE OUR KING. :|| HALLELUJAH! HALLELUJAH! WE WILL SEE OUR KING.

Notes: The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

HALLELUJAH! - CLAP-HANDS~CELEBRATE + open hands into (2h)[5] while lifting.

HAVE-NONE - (2h)[B], POs > signer move upwards past mouth and swing upwards and apart while mouth blows on hands as they pass.

THERE is the honorific index (HON.-IX), using the FT of the upturned palm to index heaven.

Lyrics:

1. ||: (3 times) Soon and very soon, we are going to see the King. :|| Hallelujah! Hallelujah! We're going to see the King.

2. ||: (3 times) No more crying there, we are going to see the King. :|| Hallelujah! Hallelujah! We're going to see the King.

3. ||: (3 times) No more dying there, we are going to see the King. :|| Hallelujah! Hallelujah! We're going to see the King.
