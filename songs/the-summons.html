The summons - KELVINGROVE

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 6 February 2005, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

1. (Role play God) (2h)YOU WILLING FOLLOW ME IF ME SUMMON YOUR"all" NAME? (2h)YOU WILLING GO PLACE NEW*, NEVER STAY SAME-OLD-THING? (2h)YOU WILLING SHOW"all" MY LOVE? (2h)YOU WILLING GOSPEL PREACH? (2h)YOU WILLING ME GROW INSIDE-SELF (2h)YOU AND (2h)YOU IN ME?

2. (2h)YOU WILLING SELF SET-ASIDE, IF ME SUMMON YOUR"all" NAME? WILLING TAKE-CARE-OF (<) CRUEL, (>) KIND, NEVER STAY SAME-OLD-THING? (2h)YOU WILLING ACCEPT HURT, IF PEOPLE JEALOUS, SCARED. (2h)YOU WILLING ACCEPT MISSION IN (2h)YOU AND (2h)YOU IN ME?

3. (2h)YOU WILLING HELP BLIND SEE, IF ME SUMMON YOUR NAME? (2h)YOU WILLING LIBERATE, NEVER STAY SAME-OLD-THING? (2h)YOU WILLING L-E-P-E-R HELP PLUS DO THINGS OTHERS alt.OVERLOOK? ADMIT ME IMPORTANT IN (2h)YOU AND (2h)YOU IN ME?

4. (2h)YOU WILLING LOVE SELF (2h)YOU HIDE, IF ME SUMMON YOUR NAME? WILLING CALM-DOWN FEAR INSIDE-SELF, NEVER STAY SAME-OLD-THING? WILLING APPLY FAITH (2h)YOU FIND, HELP IMPROVE WORLD IN-GENERAL? THROUGH MY VISION, THROUGH MY TOUCH IN (2h)YOU AND (2h)YOU IN ME?

5. (Role play to God) LORD, YOU-rt INVITE, THAT SEEM RIGHT WHEN YOU-rt SUMMON MY NAME. HELP ME TURN-AROUND AND FOLLOW YOU-rt. DON'T-WANT STAY SAME-OLD-THING. ME INTERACT YOU-rt, PLEASE LEAD ME WITH LOVE. SHOW-me ME RIGHT WAY. HAPPEN? ME MOVE, LIVE IN YOU-rt AND YOU-rt IN ME.

Notes: (2h)YOU

WILLING - [B] touches midchest, moves away while change PO to up.

SUMMON - DH [B] taps NDH [B], PO down, FO > signer, then moves > signer while closing to an [A], PO away from signer, FO up, and moving up. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - call.

SAME-OLD-THING - (2h)[Y], POs down, FOs away, circle vertically and symmetrically in a plane parallel to the chest.

GOSPEL - G-NEW. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Gospel - 2.

INSIDE-SELF - The FT of [C] are held against the chest and stuff the FT of [O^] into the [C] twice.

SET-ASIDE - (2h)[B], POs > DS, with DH higher, palms are pressed against imaginary wall that is pushed to one side.

LIBERATE - Starts like PRESSURE, DH [B], PO down, FO away, push down slightly on [S], PO > DS, FO away, then the DH is brought upwards suddenly and the signer mouths "off".

OTHERS - [A dot], PO > NDS, FO away, twists repeatedly while moving in a horizontal circle.

ADMIT - (2h)[B], POs > chest, FOs down, touch torso, run FTs up it, then turn FTs away. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Admit.

CALM-DOWN - (2h)[B], POs down, FOs away, descend slightly at NDS then at DS.

FEAR - (2h)[5], POs away, FOs up, hands are held far from body and move back to body in a wavy pattern.

IN-GENERAL - (2h)[B], FO away, palms are "held heel to heel" with FTs pointing > respective sides slightly and move away on a diagonal.

VISION - (2h)[S] are held at forehead with DH closer to head, and hands open to (2h)[5] while separating while FT wg. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Vision - 1.

TURN-AROUND - (2h)[1], POs > signer, circle around each other.

Lyrics:

1. Will you come and follow me, If I but call your name? Will you go where you don't know And never be the same? Will you let my love be shown, Will you let my name be known, Will you let my life be grown In you and you in me?

2. Will you leave your self behind, If I but call your name? Will you care for cruel and kind And never be the same? Will you risk the hostile stare, Should your life attract or scare, Will you let me answer pray'r In you and you in me?

3. Will you the blinded see, If I but call your name? Will you set the pris'ners free And never be the same? Will you kiss the leper clean, And do such as this unseen, And admit to what I mean In you and you in me?

4. Will you love the 'you' you hide, If I but call your name? Will you quell the fear inside And never be the same? Will you use the faith you've found, To reshape the world around, Through my sight and touch and sound In you and you in me?

5. Lord your summons echoes true, When you but call my name. Let me turn and follow you And never be the same. In your company I'll go Where your love and footsteps show. Thus I'll move and grow In you and you in me.
