Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 6 April 2006, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

||: WE REALLY-WANT SEE JESUS LIFT-UP HIGH*.
IDEA~LIKE FLAG WAVE-FLAG WORLD GENERAL.
MEAN (2h)#ALL SEE TRUTH AND KNOW*
HIMSELF-lf,hi ONLY WAY ARRIVE HEAVEN. :||

||: WE REALLY-WANT SEE, WE REALLY-WANT SEE,
WE REALLY-WANT SEE JESUS LIFT-UP HIGH*. :||

STEP-BY-STEP WE MARCH+.
STEP-BY-STEP WE TAKE-HOLD-OF+.
EACH PRAYER POWERFUL WAY
FORCE DEVIL FORTRESS BREAK-DOWN
COMING-DOWN"bit by bit".

Notes: The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

REALLY-WANT - WANT but shaken back and forth to show intensity.

LIFT-UP - (2h)[B], PO down are held high and swoop down while supinating and then rise up again as if helping something mature and the hands continue up and FTs are directed > Heaven.

HIGH* - [H], PO > NDS, FO away, rises suddenly and high.

IDEA~LIKE - a blending where IDEA has a [Y] instead of an [I] in anticipation of LIKE which is signed smoothly right afterwards.

WAVE-FLAG - [B], PO > NDS, FO away, hold NDH finger against base of palm and wave flag from side to side with DH flapping in the wind.

(2h)#ALL - (2h)[A], POs ><, FOs up, are held at shoulder height and move away while opening to (2h)[L].

STEP-BY-STEP - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs away, DH is held ahead of NDH, then NDH leapfrogs over DH, and DH over NDH. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - procedure.

MARCH - (2h)[4] bend at wrist and straighten out hands as if marching forward. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: March.

TAKE-HOLD-OF - (2h)[5:], POs away, FOs up, move down quickly and close to (2h)[S], POs down, FOs away, as if grabbing hold of something.

FORTRESS - (2h)[S], POs down, FOs ><, strike the whole length of the forearms together like TABLE then DH is held under NDH and the arms strike again.

BREAK-DOWN - (2h)[5:] interlaced, POs ><, FTs move down while remaining interlaced so that the PO becomes down. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - break-down.

COMING-DOWN"bit by bit" - (2h)[5], POs down, FOs away, hands wg and descend.

Lyrics:

We want to see Jesus lifted high,
A banner that flies across this land
That all men may see the truth and know
That He is the way to heaven (2x)

We want to see, we want to see,
We want to see Jesus lifted high (2x)

Step by step we're moving forward,
Little by little we're taking ground,
Every prayer a powerful weapon,
Strongholds come tumbling down,
And down, and down, and down.
