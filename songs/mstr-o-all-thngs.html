(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

MASTER RULE (2h)#ALL, YOU-rt RULE JEWISH PEOPLE.
WE YOUR-rt PEOPLE, YOU-rt OUR GOD.
TEACH US FOLLOW, OBEY YOUR-rt COMMANDMENTS
JEWISH PEOPLE MANY-LOOK-AT-me PAY-ATTENTION.

THE-rt LORD OUR GOD. THE-rt LORD ONLY-ONE*.
YOUR KINGDOM SHINY. SET-UP WHERE? FAR-AWAY HEAVEN.
YOUR-rt NAME SHINY, RULE EARTH SPREAD-ON-earth.
WE TEACH"each" YOUR-rt WAY. YOURSELF-rt CREATE US.
JEWISH PEOPLE MANY-LOOK-AT-me PAY-ATTENTION.

THE-rt LORD OUR GOD. THE-rt LORD ONLY-ONE*.
ME SING TO GOD. ME PRAISE MY LORD.
EVERY MOMENT FROM-NOW-ON FOREVER,
ME SING HALLELU-, HALLELU-, HALLELUJAH.
SING HALLELU-, HALLELU-, HALLELUJAH.

Notes: RULE - CONTROL.

(2h)#ALL - (2h)[A], POs ><, FOs up, are held at shoulder height and move away while opening to (2h)[L].

YOU - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

COMMANDMENT - C-LAW.

MANY-LOOK-AT-me - [4], POs down, FOs away, turn FTs towards the signer's face.

KINGDOM - KING + [5], PO down, FO away, circles horizontally. For an animated gif of this sign, see Valley Bible Church - ASL dictionary: Kingdom.

SHINY - (2h)[open 8], POs down, FOs away, twist rapidly at wrists while rising. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Shine.

SPREAD-ON-earth - SPREAD moves as if it were covering the sphere of the Earth.

CREATE - C-MAKE.

FROM-NOW-ON - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs away, DHFT touches knuckle side of NDHFT and moves away in a downward arc. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - from now on.

HALLELU- CLAP-HANDS~CELEBRATE.

HALLELUJAH! - CLAP-HANDS~CELEBRATE + open hands into (2h)[5] while lifting.
