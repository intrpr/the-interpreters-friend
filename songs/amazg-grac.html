Amazing grace - John Newton

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 4 June 2004, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

1. AMAZING GRACE, WORD++ INSPIRED SWEET.
YOU SAVE SINNER LIKE ME.
USED-TO-BE MY SOUL LOST, BUT NOW YOU FIND-me ME.
USED-TO-BE BLIND, NOW ME SEE TRUTH

2. GRACE TEACH MY HEART BOW-DOWN GOD.
GRACE MAKE MY FEAR DISSOLVE.
YOUR GRACE, ME CHERISH FAST, NOT DELAY.
FIRST HOUR ME BELIEVE TRUE-BIZ.

3. UP-TO-NOW ME (2h)EXPERIENCE (<) DANGER, (>) TEMPTATION.
ME STRUGGLE NOT GIVE-UP.
GRACE ITSELF BRING HERE ME SAFE.
GRACE LEAD HOME HEAVEN WILL.

4. LORD HIMSELF PROMISE GOOD THINGS FOR ME.
HIS WORD CAUSE MY HOPE STRONG.
HE PROTECT ME IDEA-LIKE SHIELD
UNTIL LAST DAY ME LIVE.

5. KNOW-THAT WHEN MY BODY WILL FAIL.
MY HEART-BEAT STOP, LIFE HIT* GONE.
ME #GO-TO HEAVEN LIVE THERE.
FROM-THEN-ON HAPPY, AT-PEACE.

6. SOMEDAY EARTH DISSOLVE IDEA-LIKE SNOW.
GOD BECOME OUR SUNSHINE.
NOW+ ME CONTINUE WITH MY GOD.
TWO-OF-US GO-TOGETHER FOREVER.

7. SUPPOSE WE LIVE TEN THOUSAND YEAR.
OUR BODY body-SHINY IDEA-LIKE SUNHINE.
WE STILL HAVE NO LESS TIME PRAISE HIM.
FOREVER-AND-EVER, NOT END.

Notes: GRACE - [O^] or [S], held high and PO > Heaven, supinates and opens to [5] while being brought towards head. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Grace.

SINNER - (2h)[X] touch their respective sides of the mouth, descend to neutral space and circle antisymmetrically parallel to the chest + AGENT. For an animated gif of the fist sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Sin.

ME - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

FEAR - (2h)[5], POs away, FOs up, hands are held far from body and move back to body in a wavy pattern.

TRUE-BIZ - TRUE + (2h)[S], POs down, FOs away, DH brushes from side to side across NDH wrist.

TEMPTATION - FT of [X] taps elbow. For an animated gif of this sign, see Valley Bible Church - ASL dictionary: Temptation.

GIFT-TO - (2h)[X], POs ><, FOs up, nod downwards at wrist to show that something is given as a gift.

FROM-THEN-ON - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs away, DHFT touches knuckle side of NDHFT and moves away in a downward arc. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - from now on.

KNOW-THAT - Starts like KNOW but twists and descends, looking almost like DON'T-KNOW except the hand twists down at the end.

HEART-BEAT - (2h)[B], POs > signer, FOs ><, NDH is held in front of heart and DH moves between, striking chest and palm of NDH.

#GO-TO - [G], PO > NDS, FO away, moves > DS while closing to an [O^].

AT-PEACE - (2h)[V], POs away, FOs up, are held at shoulder level.

SOMEDAY - WILL/FUTURE but fingers wg as the hand moves forward.

IDEA-LIKE - A blending where IDEA has a [Y] instead of an [I] in anticipation of LIKE which is signed smoothly right afterwards.

body-SHINY - (2h)[open 8], POs down, FOs away, twist rapidly at wrists while rising. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Shine.

FOREVER-AND-EVER - ALWAYS + DH [Y], PO > signer, FO up, swings outwards with PO turning away. With DH still held in its final position, NDH [Y], PO > signer, FO up, swings outwards with PO turning away.

Lyrics: (Some versions sing 1, 2, 4, 3, and 7, omitting 5 & 6)

1. Amazing grace! How sweet the sound
That saved a wretch like me!
I once was lost, but now am found;
Was blind, but now I see.

2. 'Twas grace that taught my heart to fear,
And grace my fears relieved;
How precious did that grace appear
The hour I first believed.

3. Through many dangers, toils and snares,
I have already come;
'Tis grace hath brought me safe thus far,
And grace will lead me home.

4. The Lord has promised good to me,
His Word my hope secures;
He will my Shield and Portion be,
As long as life endures.

5. Yea, when this flesh and heart shall fail,
And mortal life shall cease,
I shall possess, within the veil,
A life of joy and peace.

6. The earth shall soon dissolve like snow,
The sun forbear to shine;
But God, Who called me here below,
Shall be forever mine.

7. When we've been there ten thousand years,
Bright shining as the sun,
We've no less days to sing God's praise
Than when we'd first begun.
