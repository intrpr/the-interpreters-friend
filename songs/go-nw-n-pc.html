EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

||: LEAVE NOW, INSIDE-SELF PEACE. :||

REALLY-WANT LOVE SPIRIT AROUND (2h)YOU.

EVERY PLACE, (pause) EVERY PLACE (2h)YOU GO"each".

Notes: The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

INSIDE-SELF - Hold the FT of [C] against the chest and stuff the FT of [O^] into the [C] twice.

REALLY-WANT - WANT but shaken back and forth to show intensity.

(2h)YOU - The honorific index for you plural.

Text:

Go now in peace. May the love of God (or: spirit of love) surround you everywhere you may go.

To hear a performance of this song, go to Unitarian Universalist Church of Nashua recordings: Go now in peace.
