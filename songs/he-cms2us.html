(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

1. HE-lf COME-TO US LIKE STRANGER MEET*, LIKE BREATHE-OUT, GO-UP-LIKE-SMOKE DISSOLVE. LIKE LAST~NIGHT PLANT-SEEDS, TOMORROW~MORNING HIT* GROW. ||: WE SLOW REALIZE GOD (2h)THERE, OH-I-SEE. :||

2. WHILE PEOPLE LIE-DOWN QUIET, WORK GET-OFF-MIND, HE-lf COME. BUT WE VISUALIZE HIM-lf VAGUE. OUR HEART IDEA~LIKE DARK SKY. ||: HE-lf COME-TO-us LIKE STAR F-CL'falling star'. :||

3. HAPPEN WATER CRASH-AGAINST-ROCKS"slow"+, WE SEE HIM-lf STRONG. WHEN WIND CAUSE FOREST (2h)5-CL'sway', WE SEE HIM-lf SOFT. ||: NATURE SHOW HE-lf SUMMON-us HOME. :||

4. LONG-AGO HE-lf COME-TO-us, BORN LIKE US, BRING LOVE SPREAD. (<) LIFE, (>) DEATH, HE-lf ACCEPT BOTH. FOR-FOR? ||: SAVE EVERYONE BORN HERE EARTH. :||

5. HAPPEN FAITH GROW, HE-lf COME-TO-us BRING-TO-us TRUTH. COME-ON BELIEVE, ADORE HIM-lf. HOLY BOOK SHOW"all" JESUS GLORY. WE SEE, NOT-YET, BUT KNOW HIM-lf. ||: (<) OUR SAVIOR, (>) OUR LORD. :||

Notes: BREATHE-OUT - (2h)[O^] thumbs are held at nostrils and the fingers spread out to (2h)[5].

GO-UP-LIKE-SMOKE - (2h)[5] swirl around each other while ascending.

PLANT-SEEDS - [O^], PO down, changes to [A dot] in the direction that seeds are being sown.

GET-OFF-MIND - (2h)[open 8], touch forehead, pronate and move away.

IDEA~LIKE - a blending where IDEA has a [Y] instead of an [I] in anticipation of LIKE which - signed smoothly right afterwards.

The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

CRASH-AGAINST-ROCKS"slow"+ - DH [5wg], PO down, moves > NDH [B], PO down, FO away, crashes on it and moves upwards as if a wave, then returns to its place.

(2h)5-CL'sway' - (2h)[5] held like trees that are swaying together in the breeze.

COME-ON - (2h)[B^] move in circles pivoting at the wrists to beckon someone to do something.

ADORE - NDH is clasped over DH [S] and hands circle perpendicular to the chest. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Worship.

GLORY - (2h)[5], POs><, FOs away from signer, DH which is on top wiggles fingers while ascending. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Glory.

Lyrics:

1. He comes to us as one unknown,
a breath unseen, unheard;
as though within a heart of stone,
or shriveled seed in darkness sown,
a pulse of being stirred,
a pulse of being stirred.

2. He comes when souls in silence lie
and thoughts of day depart,
half-seen upon the inward eye,
a falling star across the sky
of night within the heart,
of night within the heart.

3. He comes to us in sound of seas,
the ocean's fume and foam;
yet small and still upon the breeze,
a wind that stirs the tops of trees,
a voice to call us home,
a voice to call us home.

4. He comes in love as once he came
by flesh and blood and birth;
to bear within our mortal frame
a life, a death, a saving name
for every child of earth,
for every child of earth.

5. He comes in truth when faith is grown;
believed, obeyed, adored:
the Christ in all the scriptures shown,
as yet unseen, but not unknown,
our Savior, and our Lord,
our Savior, and our Lord.
