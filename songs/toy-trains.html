Notes: TRACK - (2h)[G], NDH PO >DS, FO away is held as a foundation and DH PO down, FO away, starts at NDH and moves away in an outward arc, describing part of a railroad track.

SACK - (2h)[S] hold sack and giggle it while signer looks back at it.

DRESSED-UP - (2h)[5], POs down, FOs away, alt. brush down chest.

BRING++ - (2h)[B], POs up, FOs away, are both held at DS and move in upward arcs to the center of the signing field.

LET-you-KNOW - [O^] is held at temple, moves down and then outward while opening to a [5], PO up, FO away.

WE'LL-SEE - [V], PO away, FO up, taps index finger under eye.

(2h)#ALL - (2h)[A], POs ><, FOs up, move away while opening to (2h)[L].

SLEIGH - (2h)[X], POs > signer, FOs up, move towards signer in a downward arc, describing the runners of a sleigh.

RING-BELL - DH [5^], POd own, FO away, sings at wrist and knocks against palm of [B], PO > DS, FO up.
