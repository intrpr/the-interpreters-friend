EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

1. ||: THOSE-lf TRUST THE-rt LORD
IDEA-LIKE MOUNTAIN.
THEY-lf REFUSE, THEY-lf REFUSE CHANGE FAITH. NO-WAY. :||

Refrain: ||: MAYBE WORLD CHANGE++ LIKE CRAZY.
YOU-lf ALONE KEEP FAITH.
JESUS, YOU-lf NEVER, NEVER CHANGE. YOU-lf NEVER, NEVER CHANGE. :||

2. ||: THOSE-lf TRUST THE-rt LORD
IDEA-LIKE TEMPLE MOUNTAIN.
THEY-lf REFUSE, THEY-lf REFUSE CHANGE FAITH. NO-WAY. :|| Refrain

3. ||: CHRIST, HIMSELF-lf KING HIS-lf FEET SET
ON FOUNDATION STAY FOREVER.
THEY-lf REFUSE, THEY-lf REFUSE CHANGE FAITH. NO-WAY. :|| Refrain

Notes: The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

THOSE - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

FAITH - F-TRUST.

NO-WAY - (2h)[B], POs down, cross at wrists, then uncross moving horizontally and emphatically. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - don't.

IDEA-LIKE - A blending where IDEA has a [Y] instead of an [I] in anticipation of LIKE which is signed smoothly right afterwards.

TEMPLE - T-CHURCH.

FOUNDATION - NDH [S], PO > signer, FO > DS, is held out with arm parallel to the chest and DH [S], PO > signer, strikes hand from below and then strikes elbow from below.
