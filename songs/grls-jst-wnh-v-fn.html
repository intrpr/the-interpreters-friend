Notes: COME-ON - (2h)[B^] move in circles pivoting at the wrists to beckon someone to do something.

YOU - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

(2h)#ALL - (2h)[A], POs ><, FOs up, are held at shoulder height and move away while opening to (2h)[L].

REALLY-WANT - WANT but shaken back and forth to show intensity.

RING - [Y] is place on [S] like a receiver in its cradle with the [Y] vibrating.

KNOW-THAT - Starts like KNOW but twists and descends, looking almost like DON'T-KNOW except the hand twists down at the end.

NUMBER-ONE - [1], PO > signer, FO > NDS, hand is held at eye level and moves rapidly from NDS to DS.

GET-OFF-MIND - (2h)[open 8], touch forehead, pronate and move away.

GRAB - [S] grabs [1], PO > DS, FO up.

PUT-AWAY - (2h)[O^], POs away, puts something away.

NO-NO - [1], PO away, FO up, is shaken side to side with hu.
