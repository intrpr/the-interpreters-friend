Notes: MAGIC - (2h)[O^], hold out index fingers which describe a circle in the air symmetrically, starting together, rising, separating, etc., and end with the index fingers retracting back to (2h)[O^] and then open to (2h)[5^].

CAUSE - (2h)[S] are held at the chest as if holding a rope and move forward while opening to (2h)[5]. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - cause.

GO-FOR-WALK - [F], PO down, FO away, is held bent at the wrist, then repeatedly moves in steps while straoghtening, then bending.

DRAW-THE-LINE - [I] touches tongue then draws a horizontal line in front of body.

GO-AHEAD - (2h)[B^ dot], POs ><, FOs away, move forward in a jerk.

CROSS-OVER - (2h)[B], DH PO > NDS, FO away, slides over dorsal side (back) of ND hand, PO down, FO > DS.

THAT'S-ALL - FINISH with the mouthing "that's all".

GO-ALONG - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs away, DH is held ahead of NDH, then NDH leapfrogs over DH, and DH over NDH. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - procedure.

APPEARANCE - [5], PO > face, circles face.

The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.
