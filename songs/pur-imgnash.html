Notes: (2h)YOU - Underlined prounouns (ME, YOU, HE) are honorific index (HON.-IX), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

PURE - Like NICE but the DH moves backwards slightly on the palm, pauses, then moves forward rapidly to show intensity.

INSIDE-SELF - The FT of [C] are held against the chest and stuff the FT of [O^] into the [C] twice.

SPIN - Index fingers spin around each other with NDH FO up, DH PO down.

alt.CREATION - INVENT.

#DO-IT - Fingerspelled.

DO-MAGIC/MAGIC - (2h)[O^], hold out index fingers which describe a circle in the air symmetrically, starting together, rising, separating, etc., and end with the index fingers retracting back to (2h)[O^] and then open to (2h)[5^].

NOTHING-TO-IT - (2h)[F], POs away, FOs up, hands move slightly and symetrically from side to side.

TRUE-BIZ - TRUE + (2h)[S], POs down, FOs away, DH brushes from side to side across NDH wrist.

SENSATION - [open 8] is drawn up the chest in one long movement. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - sense (1).

alt.5:-CL'islands' - (2h)[5:], POs away, FOs up, alt. plop each hand down to show the random placement of islands.

REALLY-WANT - the sign WANT but shaken back and forth to show intensity.

THERE'tap brain' - Tap with index finger.
