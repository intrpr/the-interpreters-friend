Notes: GREAT - Like WONDERFUL but a single pause and then an abrupt movement.

UPRIGHT - Elbow of [S], PO > NDS, FO up, is struck against the fingers of NDH [B] held like part of TABLE.

CHARIOT - Use the NDH [C], PO > DS, FO away, as the front part that shields the driver and an [N] inside the [C] to show someone standing there, and both hands move towards NDS.

COWER - (2h)[1^], POs down, FOs away, are held side by side and tremble like knees knocking.

CALL-ON - DH [B] taps NDH [B], PO down, FO away from signer, then moves > signer while closing to an [A], PO away from signer, FO up, and moving up. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - call.

TAKE-UP - (2h)[5:], POs away, FOs up, move down quickly and close to (2h)[S], POs down, FOs away, as if grabbing hold of something, then hands ascend.

SWORD - (2H)[S], NDH acts as scabbard (sword holder) and DH pulls out sword.

SHIELD is [S] is held as if holding shield and FT of [1] traces a circle where the shield would be.

BOLD - (2h)[1] point to ears, then hands move forward and open to (2h)[5], PO > back of signer, FOs up, with brr.

THEREABOUTS - [5], PO down, FO away, circles parllel to the floor. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - approximately.
