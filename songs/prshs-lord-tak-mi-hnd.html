Precious Lord, take my hand - Thomas A. Dorsey

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 26 February 2008, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

1. PRECIOUS LORD, TAKE MY HANDS.
LEAD ME CONTINUE"over time". SUPPORT+ ME.
ME TIRED; ME WEAK; ME WORN-OUT.
DURING STORM, ALL-NIGHT.
LEAD ME TO YOUR-rt LIGHT.
TAKE MY HANDS, PRECIOUS LORD.
LEAD ME HOME INDEX-Heaven.

2. HAPPEN LIFE DRY-AS-DUST FINISH,
PRECIOUS LORD, STAY+ CLOSE.
HAPPEN LIFE SOON GONE,
ATTEND-TO ME CRY-OUT; ATTEND-TO ME SUMMON.
SUPPORT+, DON'T-WANT RELAPSE.
TAKE MY HANDS, PRECIOUS LORD.
LEAD ME HOME INDEX-Heaven.

3. HAPPEN DARK COVER-UP DAY
AND NIGHT ARRIVE"step by step",
HAPPEN DAY DISSOLVE, NOW GONE.
ME STAND RIVER DEATH
GUIDE MY FEET, TAKE MY HANDS.
TAKE MY HANDS, PRECIOUS LORD.
LEAD ME HOME INDEX-Heaven.

Notes: PRECIOUS - (2h)[5:], hold FTs by the sides of the chin and move down while maintaining contact and closing to (2h)[S].

TAKE - (2h)[5:], POs away, FOs up, move down quickly and close to (2h)[S], POs down, FOs away, as if grabbing hold of something, then hands ascend.

ME - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

WORN-OUT - (2h)[A], palms together, FO away, hands separate while changing to (2h)[5:], POs up, FOs away.

LIGHT - (2h)[O^], POs away, FOs up, open to (2h)[5], while separating. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Light - 1.

DRY-AS-DUST - [5:], PO > NDS, FO up, moves > DS under chin while closing to [S].

ATTEND-TO - PAT-ATTENTION.

CRY-OUT - SHOUT.

SUMMON - CALL-ON.

RELAPSE - [V] touches FT to [B], PO up, and "slips and falls".

COVER-UP - DH [B], PO down, FO away, brushes over NDH, [B], PO down, FO > DS, from FT to wrist.

ARRIVE"step by step" - ARRIVE is signed by with the DH pauses several times on its advance to the palm of the NDH.

Lyrics:

1. Precious Lord take my hand
Lead me on, let me stand
I am tired, I am weak, I am worn
Through the storm, through the night
Lead me on to the light
Take my hand precious Lord, lead me home

2. When my way grows drear
precious Lord linger near
When my life is almost gone
Hear my cry, hear my call
Hold my hand lest I fall
Take my hand precious Lord,lead me home

3. When the darkness appears and
the night draws near
And the day is past and gone
At the river I stand
Guide my feet, hold my hand
Take my hand, precious Lord, lead me home
