Freedom is coming - African American Spiritual

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 10 January 2007, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

(This spiritual may vary as to how parts are repeated, so stay flexible.)

(<) FREEDOM COME SOON. (>) FREEDOM COME SOON.
FREEDOM COME SOON. YES, ME KNOW*.

(<) JUSTICE COME SOON. (>) JUSTICE COME SOON.
JUSTICE COME SOON. YES, ME KNOW*.

(<) JESUS COME SOON. (>) JESUS COME SOON.
JESUS COME SOON. YES, ME KNOW*.

Notes: FREEDOM - FREE.

ME - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

KNOW* - Make it emphatic by pausing slightly and then striking the temple once, rather than just tapping.

JUSTICE - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, tap FT while moving in a horizontal circle.
