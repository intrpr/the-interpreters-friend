EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

1. EVERY PLACE YOU FIND KINDNESS, EVERY PLACE YOU FIND LOVE, GOD THERE.
CHRIST HIS LOVE INSPIRE US ASSEMBLE COOPERATE.
GO-AHEAD CELEBRATE, ENJOY GOD POSS.++.
WE SHOULD BOW-DOWN GOD, WE SHOULD LOVE HIMSELF-God LIVE INSIDE-SELF. PRAY++ WE LOVE EACH-OTHER TRUE-BIZ.

2. EVERY PLACE YOU FIND KINDNESS, EVERY PLACE YOU FIND LOVE, GOD THERE.
HAPPEN WE ASSEMBLE TOGETHER, WARN++,
DON'T-WANT DISAGREE"each".
TEMPTED GO-AHEAD BAD, RESIST. DON'T ARGUE*"pow".
REALLY-WANT CHRIST OUR GOD GO-TOGETHER"over time" US.

3. EVERY PLACE YOU FIND KINDNESS, EVERY PLACE YOU FIND LOVE, GOD THERE.
WE WANT JOIN SAINT GROUP.
SEE GOD HIS 5-FACE face-SHINY++.
PLEASE CHRIST OUR GOD, GIVE-TO-us US HAPPY
OVERFLOWING FOR RIGHT REASON. FOREVER, FOREVER, AMEN.

Notes: ASSEMBLE - (2h)[S], FO > signer, move > signer while opening to (2h)[5wg].

INSIDE-SELF - Hold the FT of [C] against the chest and stuff the FT of [O^] into the [C] twice.

TRUE-BIZ - TRUE + S-BUSINESS.

REALLY-WANT - WANT but shaken back and forth to show intensity.

GO-TOGETHER"over time" - (2h)[A] hold palms together and move back and forth slightly.

SAINT - [S], PO down, FO away, moves > DS across palm of [B], PO up, FO > DS.

5-FACE face-SHINY++ - [5], PO > signer, FO up, circles face, then (2h)[open 8] point middle fingers at face and move away while twistsing at the wrists.

OVERFLOWING - [5], PO down, FO away, covers top of [S], PO > DS, FO away, and FT wiggle as the hand moves away, like a cup overflowing.

Lyrics:

1. Where charity and love are, God is there.
Christ's love has gathered us into one.
Let us rejoice and be pleased in Him.
Let us fear, and let us love the living God.
And may we love each other with a sincere heart.

2. Where charity and love are, God is there.
As we are gathered into one body,
Beware, lest we be divided in mind.
Let evil impulses stop, let controversy cease,
And may Christ our God be in our midst.

3. Where charity and love are, God is there.
And may we with the saints also,
See Thy face in glory, O Christ our God:
The joy that is immense and good,
Unto the ages through infinite ages. Amen.
