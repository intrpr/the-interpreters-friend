Notes: EXHAUSTED - TIRED + [V], PO up, FO > NDS, is drawn with effort across the palm of [B], PO up, FO away.

INSIDE-SELF - The FT of [C] are held against the chest and stuff the FT of [O^] into the [C] twice.

ME - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

QM - [1], PO away, FO up, traces the shape of a question mark curling and uncurling the fingers once.

HOLY - H-CLEAN. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Holy - 1.

HEAVENLY-FATHER - (2h)[A], FOs up, DH is held against forehead, NDH close to but lower, both hands open to (2h)[5] while moving upwards. HEAVEN may be added to this. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Heavenly Father - 1.

GIVE-BIRTH - S-ENTER. Not to be confused with BORN. The child is BORN, the mother GIVE[s]-BIRTH.

BURDEN - (2h)[C], POs ><, FOs up, are placed on DS shoulder and press it down so that the body tips > DS.

The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

GO-WITH - (2h)[A] press palms together and move forward.

BREATHE-DOWNwg - - Look down, hold FTs of (2h)[O^] to nostrils, then move hands away to SPREAD and descend with a wg.

UNIFIED - COOPERATE.

SPILL-OVER - (2h)[C] pours an imaginary bucket over head with a slight horizontal circle.

HAVE-MERCY-ON-us - (2h)[open 8], POs > signer, hands move in vertical circles normal (perpendicular) to the chest. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Mercy - 2.

I am frightened by the load i bear.
In a world as cold as stone,
