Notes: SCADS-OF - (2h)[5:], POs down, FOs away, move forward to show many beings.

GO-AHEAD - (2h)[B^ dot], POs ><, FOs away, move forward in a jerk.

THROWN-OFF - (2h)[C], POs, ><, is held above NDH, then POs reverse and are thrown to side.

INADEQUATE"step by step" - Thumb of [C], PO > NDS, rests on backhand of [B], PO down, FO > DS, and DH flattens bit by bit.

INSIDE-SELF - The FT of [C] are held against the chest and stuff the FT of [O^] into the [C] twice.

DARK"step by step" - (2h)[B], POs > back, FOs up, hands cross bit by bit.

COLOR - use NDH.

(<) SHINY, (>) SHINY - The basic sign is (2h)[open 8], POs down, FOs away, twist rapidly at wrists while rising. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Shine. Here, one shifts to the one side and then the other, changing dominance.

DON'T - (2h)[B], POs down, cross at wrists, then uncross moving horizontally and emphatically. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - don't.

(2h)"WOW" - (2h)[5] shake out hands, swiveling at elbows.

CRAZY-FOR - [5:], PO > singer, FO up, - shaken slightly from side to side.

COME-ON - (2h)[B^] move in circles pivoting at the wrists to beckon someone to do something.

NOT-THAT - (2h)[5], POs away, FOs up, wave from side to side symmetrically.

(2h)THIS - (2h)[1^], POs down, FOs away, hands descend.

CAUSE - (2h)[S] are held at the chest as if holding a rope and move forward while opening to (2h)[5]. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - cause.

HAD-IT - [5], PO down, FO > NDS, moves under chin > DS while closing to an [S].

THAT'S-ALL - FINISH with the mouthing "that's all".

You with the sad eyes.
Don't be discouraged.
Oh I realize,

And the darkness inside you,
can make you feel so small

But I see your true colors
shining through.
I see your true colors,
and that's why I love you.
So don't be afraid to let them show
your true colors.
True colors are beautiful,
like a rainbow.

Show me a smile then.
Don't be unhappy. Can't remember
when I last saw you laughing.
If this world makes you crazy,
and you've taken all you can bear,
you call me up,
Because you know I'll be there,

And I'll see your true colors
shining through.
I see your true colors,
And that's why I love you.
