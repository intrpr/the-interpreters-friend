Notes: "Tears in Heaven" is a heartfelt ballad written and performed by Eric Clapton about the pain he felt following the 1991 death of his four-year-old son, Conor, who fell from a 53rd-story window in his mother's New York City condominium. - WIKIPEDIA.

me-WONDER - [1], PO > temple, FO up, FT circles around temple. I just added the "me" to show what it means contextually.

MEET - This is the one person meets one person son, rather than the multiple MEETING.

HEAVEN - (2h)[B], POs down, FOs ><, hands are held high, circle around each other and then separate.

SAME - (2h)[1], POs down, FOs away, hands meet from a little distance.

QM - [1], PO away, FO up, curls and uncurls while tracing the shape of a question mark.

HOLD-HANDS - Hold hands together as in WEDDING and swing them to and fro.

BEHAVE - (2h)[B], POs down, FOs away, sweep from side to side antisymmetrically.

OPPRESSED - DH [B], PO down, FO > NDS, pushs down on [S], PO > DS, FO away, which is held against chest.

(pause) - There is a significant pause in the middle of this verse that should be observed rather than rushing on to the next part in sign.

HAVE-MERCY-ON-me - (2h)[open 8], POs > signer, hands move in vertical circles normal (perpendicular) to the chest. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Mercy - 2.

AT-PEACE - (2h)[V], POs away, FOs up, are held at shoulder level.

NO-MORE - (2h)[B], POs down, cross at wrists, then uncross moving horizontally and emphatically. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - don't.
