Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 22 June 2004, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

PEOPLE REJECT ME, SAME-OLD-THING.

PEOPLE DISREGARD-me, SAME-OLD-THING.

INFORM"all", PEOPLE REJECT ME, SAME-OLD-THING.

PEOPLE GOSSIP AGAINST ME, DISGUSTED.

TROUBLE HAVE'vava' WORLD GENERAL.

TRUE~BIZ, TROUBLE HAVE'vava' WORLD GENERAL.

||: (3 times) INFORM"all", MY RELIGION CHERISH ALWAYS. :||

Notes: REJECT - Middle finger is flicked aaginst the thumb while resting on the palm of [B], PO up, FO away, as if flicking something away.

SAME-OLD-THING - (2h)[Y], POs down, FOs away, circle vertically and symmetrically in a plane parallel to the chest.

DISREGARD-me - [S], PO > NDS, FO up, is held against nose, the hand moves diagonally > DS, then reurns and slaps palm of [5] against chest.

HAVE'avavav' - HAVE but moves lips to say "av av av".

TRUE~BIZ - TRUE + S-BUSINESS.

The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.
