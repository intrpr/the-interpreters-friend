EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

||: TIME GO-ALONG'fast'. BETTER GET-BENEFIT NOW! :|| EVERY-MORNING DAWN MAKE DARK SKY BRIGHT. PARALLEL ME KNOW HAVE REASON HOPE, BEST HAPPEN WILL. EACH TIME SKY BRIGHT AGAIN HAPPEN? ME ANNOUNCE, ||: TIME GO-ALONG'fast'. BETTER GET-BENEFIT NOW! :||

Notes: The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

GO-ALONG'fast' - Like NEXT with hands leapfrogging over one another but signed faster than usual. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - process.

GET-BENEFIT - Sometimes called TAKE-ADVANTAGE, but not the sign that can imply negativity. Here is means [5:], PO > signer, FOs > NDS, is laid at the end of the NDH fingers and moves along the palm > the signer, ending by closing to an [S] and moving upwards.

ME - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

UP++ - (2h)[B], POs up, FOs away, hands bend upwards at wrists repeatedly while rising with each bend.

GO-AHEAD - (2h)[B^ dot], POs ><, FOs away, move forward in a jerk.

GROUP-TOGETHER - (2h)[C], POs ><, FOs away, hands are brought together as if pushing people/things together into a group.

SAME-OLD-THING - (2h)[Y], POs down, FOs away, circle vertically and symmetrically in a plane parallel to the chest.

SECOND-NATURE - [5:], PO down, is held under the chin at the NDS and moves > DS while closing to [S].

KNOW-THAT - Starts like KNOW but twists and descends, looking almost like DON'T-KNOW except the hand twists down at the end.

#GO - [G], PO > NDS, FO away, moves > DS while closing to an [O^].

GUT - [B^], PO > signers waist, FO down, FT tap waist on DS.

SURROUND - [5], PO down, FO away, circles above NDH [1], PO > DS, FO up.
