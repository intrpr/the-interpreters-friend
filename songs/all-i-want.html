All I want to do is love you

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 1 September 2004, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

ME GO-TOWARDS-GOAL TRUE~BIZ LOVE (2h)YOU.
ME GO-TOWARDS-GOAL, WORSHIP (2h)YOU.
ME GO-TOWARDS-GOAL KNEEL
BOW-DOWN (2h)YOU.

MY HANDS REACH-UP-TO HEAVEN
GO-AHEAD YOUR FIRE BURNING-FROM-Heaven-TO-inside-self
INSPIRED NEW* LORD.
LIKE USED-TO-BE.

Notes: GO-TOWARDS-GOAL is like the sign GOAL but the DH moves in a vertical circle while going towards the goal to show a constant process.

TRUE-BIZ is TRUE + S-BUSINESS.

REACH-UP-TO is both hands reaching up to Heaven.

GO-AHEAD is (2h)[B^ dot], POs ><, FOs away, move forward in a jerk.

BURNING-FROM-Heaven-TO-inside-self is (2h)[5wg] moves from high above signer to signer's chest.

Lyrics:

All I want to do is love you.
All I want to do is worship you.
All I want to do is lay down,
Right at your feet.

As I lift my hands to heaven,
Let your fire fall down on me.
Rekindle the fire within me, Lord,
Once again.
