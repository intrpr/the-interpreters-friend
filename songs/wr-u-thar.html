Were you there? - African American spiritual

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 21 March 2005, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

1. ||: CAN (2h)YOU VISUALIZE HOW THEY-rt CRUCIFY MY LORD? :|| Refrain and then repeat this verse one more time.

Refrain: CRAZY-FOR LOOK-BACK. SOMETIMES THAT VISUALIZE CAUSE ME TREMBLE... TREMBLE... TREMBLE.

2. ||: CAN (2h)YOU VISUALIZE HOW THEY-rt DRIVE-NAIL@lf DRIVE-NAIL@rt THE-ctr CROSS? :|| Refrain and then repeat.

3. ||: CAN (2h)YOU VISUALIZE HOW THEY-rt WITHDRAW-SWORD DRIVE-SWORD-INTO-lf-side MIME-SWORD-INTO-Jesus-SIDE HIS-lf SIDE? :|| Refrain and then repeat.

4. ||: CAN (2h)YOU VISUALIZE HOW THE SUN REFUSE SHINE-DOWN? :|| Refrain and then repeat.

5. ||: CAN (2h)YOU VISUALIZE HOW THEY-lf PUT-ONTO-SLAB IN GRAVE? :|| Refrain and then repeat.

6. ||: CAN (2h)YOU VISUALIZE HOW THEY-high-rt ROLL-STONE-TO-rt THE STONE? :|| Refrain and then repeat.

Notes: The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

(2h)YOU - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

VISUALIZE - (2h)[S] are held at forehead with DH closer to head, and hands open to (2h)[5] while separating while FT wg. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Vision - 1.

CRUCIFY - Pinky side of [S] is "driven" into palm of [B], PO away, FO up, then vice versa. Notice the difference from CRUCIFIED. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - crucify.

CRAZY-FOR LOOK-BACK - This is meant to be signed during the notes that are sung without words at this point. It is an awkward place to stop signing, especially since the choirs mouths will continue to move. [5:], PO > singer, FO up, is shaken slightly from side to side + (2h)[H or V] are brought back over the shoulder, POs down, FOs back.

CAUSE - (2h)[S] are held at the chest as if holding a rope and move forward while opening to (2h)[5]. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - cause.

TREMBLE - (2h)[1^], POs down, FOs away, hands vibrate from side to side symmetrically to show legs shaking.

DRIVE-NAIL - [bO] holds nail high in the air and [bX] swings hammer to drive the nail home into the left side and then the right side.

CROSS - [C], PO away, FO up, traces cross by moving down, then across.

WITHDRAW-SWORD - Mime pulling a sword out of a scabbard.

DRIVE-SWORD-INTO-lf-side - [S] stabs into left side of signer.

MIME-SWORD-INTO-Jesus-SIDE - Thrust the sword forward as if driving it into Jesus' side.

SIDE - Left hand (whether DH or NDH) slides palm down left side.

SHINE-DOWN - [O^] is held high PO > signer; hands open to [5^] while moving > signer.

PUT-ONTO-SLAB - (2h)[B^] wrap arms around vertical body of Jesus and swing Jesus' body around so that his head is pointing to one side and lay the body down on a stone slab.

ROLL-STONE-TO-rt - Imagine a large circular stone that rolls on a track in front of a cave (this is the kind used during Jesus' time) and roll it so that it will cover the tomb entrance.

Lyrics:

Were you there when they crucified my Lord? (Refrain and then repeat this verse one more time.)

Refrain: Sometime it causes me to tremble, tremble, tremble.

Were you there when they nailed him to the tree? (Refrain and repeat.)

Were you there when they pierced him in the side? (Refrain and repeat.)

Were you there when the sun refused to shine? (Refrain and repeat.)

Were you there when they laid him in the tomb? (Refrain and repeat.)

Were you there when they rolled the stone away? (Refrain and repeat.)
