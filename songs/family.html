Notes: (2h)YOU - Underlined prounouns (ME, YOU, HE) are honorific index (HON.-IX), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

#ALL-OF-US - [A], PO > NDS, FO up touches DS shoulder then [L^], PO > signer touches NDS shoulder.

IDEA~LIKE - A blending where IDEA has a [Y] instead of an [I] in anticipation of LIKE which is signed smoothly right afterwards.

OFF-our-MIND - (2h)[open 8] touch forehead and palms turn away and descend.

ASSEMBLE - (2h)[S], FO > signer, move > signer while opening to (2h)[5wg].

EXPANDwg - DH [S] is held inside of NDH [C], FOs away. Hands separate while opening into (2h)[5:] and cheeks are puffed out.

alt.GO-AWAY - (2h)[5^] alt. move diagonally away while closing to an [O^].

NOT-THAT - (2h)[5], POs away, FOs up, wave from side to side symmetrically.

(2h)#ALL - [A], PO up, FO > DS is held at DS then swings away in an arc and ends [L^], PO up, FO > NDS.

(2h)C-CL'trunk' - (2h)[C], POs <, FOs away, rise to show the trunk of a tree.

(2h)5-CLwg'branching out'0 (2h)[5wg], POs away, expand out like branches.

GROWwg'tall' - Like GROW, but DH FT wg and rise farther than normal.
