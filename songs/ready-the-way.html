EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

1. ||: MAKE READY WAY FOR THE-ctr LORD. :|| HAVE VOICE ANNOUNCE IN WILDERNESS, MAKE READY WAY FOR THE-ctr LORD.

2. ||: EVERY VALLEY ADVANCEDWILL . :|| EVERY MOUNTAIN WILL BECOME-LOW. EVERY VALLEY ADVANCED WILL.

Refrain: HE-ctr YOUR GOD. FINISH COME TAKE-AWAY SIN. LOOK-UP-TO-God GOD, SEE HIS-ctr POWER CAN SAVE.

3. ||: WILDERNESS, BLOSSOM WILL. :|| GLORY, GLORY SPREAD HERE LAND. WILDERNESS, BLOSSOM WILL. [no refrain]

4. ||: THEY-rt NOW BLIND, SEE WILL. :|| THEY-lf NOW LAME, JUMP HAPPY WILL. THEY-rt NOW BLIND, SEE WILL. Refrain

5. ||: PEOPLE WEAK BECOME STRONG. :|| PERSON SCARED, GO-AHEAD COMFORT HIM-ctr. PEOPLE WEAK BECOME STRONG. Refrain

Notes: The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

THE - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

WILDERNESS - COUNTRY SETTLE, NOT-YET. Where the second sign is (2h)[5], POs down, FOs away, descend with several stops along the way.

Notice that some verses have a refrain, and some don't.

ADVANCED - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, move upwards arcing towards the signer. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Exalted.

BECOME-LOW - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, move downwards arcing towards the signer.

BLOSSOM - (2h)[A] are held together but the hands are twisted like the beginning of CHANGE. Hands twist back while opening to (2h)[5:] with the hands still held together.

SHINY - (2h)[open 8], POs down, FOs away, twist rapidly at wrists while rising. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Shine.

LAME - (2h)[1^], POs down, FOs away, move in circles with DS "limping".

GO-AHEAD - (2h)[B^ dot], POs ><, FOs away, move forward in a jerk.

COMFORT - Same sign as COMFORTABLE but slowly.

Lyrics:

1. ||: "Ready the way for the Lord." :|| A voice cries out in the wilderness: "Ready the way for the Lord."

2. ||: "Let every valley be filled. :|| Let every mountain be humbled. Let every valley be filled."

Refrain: Here is your God, coming with your vindication. Look and behold the saving power of God.

3. ||: Desert and waste land will bloom. :|| Glory and splendor will fill the land. Desert and waste land will bloom.

4. ||: Those who are blind will then see. :|| Those who are lame will then leap for joy. Those who are blind will then see. Refrain

5. ||: Strengthen the ones who are weak. :|| Say to the frightened: "Have courage." Strengthen the ones who are weak. Refrain
