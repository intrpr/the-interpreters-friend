You are the healing - Scott Soper

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 29 January 2008, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

1. JESUS, YOU-lf CAUSE US HEAL. YOU-lf COME MAKE US WHOLE AGAIN.
JESUS, YOU-lf CAUSE US HEAL. COME-ON TEACH US LIVE RIGHT.
||: (3x) JESUS, YOU-lf CAUSE US HEAL. :|| COME-ON TEACH US LIVE RIGHT.

2. JESUS, YOU-lf MAKE US FREE. YOU-lf BREAK OUR CHAINS FOREVER.
JESUS, YOU-lf MAKE US FREE. COME-ON TEACH US LIVE RIGHT.
||: (3x) JESUS, YOU-lf MAKE US FREE. :|| COME-ON TEACH US LIVE RIGHT.

3. JESUS, YOU-lf POWERFUL. (<) SIN, (>) DEATH, YOU-lf CONQUER.
JESUS, YOU-lf POWERFUL. COME-ON TEACH US LIVE RIGHT.
||: (3x) JESUS, YOU-lf POWERFUL. :|| COME-ON TEACH US LIVE RIGHT.

4. JESUS, YOU-lf RESURRECT. YOU-lf RAISE-UP US HAVE LIFE FOREVER.
JESUS, YOU-lf RESURRECT. COME-ON TEACH US LIVE RIGHT.
||: (3x) JESUS, YOU-lf RESURRECT. :|| COME-ON TEACH US LIVE RIGHT.

Notes: YOU - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

CAUSE - (2h)[S] are held at the chest as if holding a rope and move forward while opening to (2h)[5]. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - cause.

WHOLE - (2h)[B^], POs up, FOs ><, DH circles around NDH and lands on its palm. For a QuickTime file of COMPLETE, see ASL Pro: Whole.

The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

COME-ON - (2h)[B^] move in circles pivoting at the wrists to beckon someone to do something.

POWERFUL - [C] or [5:] brings FTs rapidly to the ND biceps and bounces slightly.

RESURRECT - [V], PO up, FO away, pronates and hops onto the palm of [B], PO up, FO away. For an animated gif of this sign, see Valley Bible Church - ASL dictionary: Resurrection.

RAISE-UP - (2h)[B], POs down, FOs away, lower slightly, supinate, and rise up.

Jesus, you are the power, come show us how to live.

Jesus, the resurrection, come show us how to live.
