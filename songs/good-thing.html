EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

Notes: GIVE-IN - [B], FO > NDS, is held against the chest, there is a pause, and then hand is brought to neutral space, PO up, FO > NDS.

ATTEND - (2h)[1], PO away, FO up, fingers are kept stiff and the hands stay in place while the handshape oscillates between (2h)[1] and (2h)[1^].

OVER-MY-HEAD - (2h)[S] flick out their index fingers. like UNDERSTAND, but both hands travel over shoulders.

HOLD-ON - (1h) or (2h)[B] puts palms out with FTs up with a slight movement forward after the palms are presented. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - hold.

READ-my-MIND - (2h)[S] flick their index fingers out several times as if a signal is being sent from one peron's mind to the other. DH FO > signer's forehead, and NDH FO is in the opposite direction.

INSIDE-SELF - The FT of [C] against the chest and stuff the FT of [O^] into the [C] twice.

#GO - [G], PO > signer, FO up, held at eye level, moves away while closing to a [bO^].

YUCK - Thumb of [5], PO > NDS, FO up, touches DS of chin and moves forward suddenly like VOMIT but the head and shoulder do not move forward.

BOOTS - (2h)[B], PO taps at elbow joint from above.

(2h)F-CL'pole' USE-SKI-POLES - (2h)[F] touch and separate like the shape of an upright pole that both hands move as if they are pushing the signer forward on ski poles.

TEETH-CHATTER - (2h)[V:], DHPO down, NDHPO up, are held one above the other and the hands move alt. from side to side like chattering teeth.

(2h)B-CL'straight down' - (2h)[B], POs > signer, FOs ><, DH is held directly below NDH and moves down like the sheer drop of the snowy cliff.

FUNNY~NONE - [H] touches FT to nose and descends while closing to an [O], PO up, FO away.

BACK-OF-MIND - FT of [X] taps back of head.

GIVE-ME-A-BREAK - [S], PO > NDS, FO up, taps chin once and then chest twice.

MORE-AND-MORE - like repeating the sign MORE but the NDH stays in place.

PUFF-CIGAR - [R^] holds knuckles against DS of mouth which acts as if it were puffing on the cigar which moves slightly.

SUSHI - DH [H], PO down, FOs > NDS, pats the palm of [B^], PO up, several times.

GROSS - (2h)[open 8], POs ><, FOs away, repeatedlt approach and separate from one another.

C,V-CL-'poke at sushi' - [C], PO up, FO away, behaves like a food bowl and the DH [V] acts like chopsticks unethusiatically playing with the sushi.

MIME'fish faces looks up at me' - Look up and to the right (since you were previously looking down and to the left when you were poking at the sushi), put the FTs of (2h)[B] against the sides of the head like fins and move them back and forth while maintaining contact with pursed lips like a fish.

C,U-CL'sushi jumps off seaweed and jumps into ocean' - [U] curls up, jumps off [C], and swims away.

C,V-CL'eat sushi with chopsticks' - [C], PO up, FO away, behaves like a food bowl and the DH [V] acts like chopsticks being used to feed a person.

LISTEN-BORED - Put your head against your hand as if listening while bored.

B,V-CL'look over steep incline' - DH [V] looks over [B], PO down, FO > DS.

NO-NO - [1], PO away, FO up, is shaken side to side.

INDEX-LIST-OF-3 - DH [1] points to thumb, index finger and middle finger of [5], PO > signer, FO > DS.

DONE-TO-DEATH - [5], PO down, FO > NDS, is slid under chin and closes to an [S].

TAKE-OPPORTUNITY"each" - [5:], PO > signer, FO > DS, is slid down palm of [B], PO up, FO away and closes to an [S] while DH moves > DS.

MUCH-LATER - [L], PO away, FO up, moves fara awy while pronating to PO down.

USED-TO-IT - (2h)[5:], POs down, FOs away, hands descend while closing to (2h)[S].

WE'LL-SEE - [V], PO away, FO up, is tapped against the DS cheek.
