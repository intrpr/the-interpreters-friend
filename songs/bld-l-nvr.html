The blood will never lose its power - Andraé Crouch

Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

Created 4 September 2004, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

JESUS BLOOD BLOOD-FLOW-FROM-STIGMATA FOR ME, LONG-AGO CALVARY,
THAT BLOOD GIVE-me STRENGTH EVERY-DAY.
HIS BLOOD LOSE ITS POWER NEVER.

Refrain: THAT POWER SPREAD EVERY PLACE, MOUTAIN HIGH, VALLEY LOW.
THAT BLOOD GIVE-ME STRENGTH EVERY-DAY.
HIS BLOOD LOSE ITS POWER NEVER.

MY DOUBT, THAT BLOOD COMFORT, FEAR CALM, MY TEARS WIPE-AWAY.
THAT BLOOD GIVE-me STRENGTH EVERY-DAY.
HIS BLOOD LOSE ITS POWER NEVER. Refrain

Notes: BLOOD-FLOW-FROM-STIGMATA is [4], PO > signer, FO > NDS, "leaks" blood from the palm.

CALVARY - MOUNTAIN + (2h)[1], cross fingers at "top" of mountain signed.

DOUBT - (2h)[S], POs down, FOs away, alt. rise and fall.

WIPE-AWAY - both hands wipe tears from the signer's cheeks.

Lyrics:

The blood that Jesus shed for me way back on Calvary,
The blood that gives me strength from day to day,
It will never loose its power

Refrain: It reaches to the highest mountain;
It flows to the lowest valley
the blood that gives me strength from day to day
It will never loose its power

It soothes my doubt and calms my fears,
and it dries all my tears;
The Blood that gives me strength from day to day
it will never loose its power.
