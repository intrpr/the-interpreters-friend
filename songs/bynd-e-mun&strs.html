Refrain: BEYOND OUR MOON AND STARS, DEEP LIKE NIGHT,
LORD, WE REALLY-WANT SEE YOUR-ctr LIGHT.
YOU-ctr PROVIDE BIRD HAVE NEST, YOUR WINGS-FOLD.
PARALLEL WE NEED FIND REST WITH ANGEL SING.

1. OUR EYES THIRST SEE YOUR-ctr LOVING FACE.
NEED LIVE IN YOUR-ctr TEMPLE EVERY-DAY CONNECT-TO-God.

2. YOUR-ctr ROAD LEAD US, LORD, ACROSS DESERT WIDE.
WE LOOK-FORWARD, ENVISION TOGETHER YOU-ctr FOREVER.

3. LORD YOU-ctr SHINE-DOWN OUR DARK PLACE.
YOU-ctr COME LIVE WITH US WITH FLESH AND BONE.

4. EVERY-NIGHT YOUR-ctr STAR LEAD US. EVERY-DAY YOUR CLOUD

Notes: BEYOND - (2h)[B^], touch FTs, then DH moves up in an arc towards the signer and away. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - more than.

STARS - STAR + (2h)[5:], POs away, FOs up, move slightly forwards multiple times while descending to show how the stars "dot the heavens".

REALLY-WANT - WANT but shaken back and forth to show intensity.

LIGHT - (2h)[O^], POs away, FOs up, open to (2h)[5], while separating. For a QuickTime video of this sign, see ASL Pro: Light - 1.

YOU - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

NEST - [bO^] represents beak and is stuck into side opening of [C], PO away. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - nest.

WINGS-FOLD - Second half of ANGEL: arms extend, bend, and move slightly as if flying gently + arms cross in front of signer.

PARALLEL - (2h)[1], POs down, FOs away, move forward. For a QuickTime video of this sign, see ASL Pro: Parallel.

LOVING - LOVE+.

TEMPLE - T-CHURCH.

DESERT - DRY THEREABOUTS. For a QuickTime video of this sign, see ASL Pro: Desert.

ENVISION - (2h)[S] are held at forehead with DH closer to head, and hands open to (2h)[5] while separating while FT wg. For a QuickTime video of this sign, see ASL Pro: Vision - 1.

FLESH - Pinch cheek and pull it slightly side to side.

BONE - [V:], PO > NDS, FO away, strikes dorsal side (back hand) of [S], PO down, FO > DS.

HOLY - H-CLEAN. For a QuickTime video of this sign, see ASL Pro: Holy - 1.
