EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

Refrain: ||: LORD HELP-me HOW? :||
HE MAKE MY BODY HEAL.
HE ALSO TOUCH MY MIND.
SAVE ME BY-A-HAIR.
ME PRAISE HIS NAME WILL.
EVERYDAY SAME-OLD-THING.
COME-ON PRAISE HIS NAME.
LORD HELP-me HOW? LONG-LIST.

Notes: The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

HELP-me - HELP but moved towards signer.

BY-A-HAIR - [F], pulls out a hair with a grimace of pain.

SAME-OLD-THING - (2h)[Y], POs odwn, FOs away, move in vertical circles parallel to the chest.

COME-ON - (2h)[B] move in circles pivoting at the wrists to beckon someone to do something.

LONG-LIST - (2h)[O^], NDH is held above and touches DH with overlap of fingertips. DH descends suddenly while both open to (2h)[5] with pc.
