Notes: TIME-PERIOD - [T], PO > NDS, FO up, travels in a vertical plane parallel to the NDH and makes contact with NDH [B], PO > DS, FO away. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Period - Time.

HON.-INDEX - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

IDEA~LIKE - a blending where IDEA has a [Y] instead of an [I] in anticipation of LIKE which is signed smoothly right afterwards.

LATER++ - [L], PO away, FO up, nods at wrist, moving forward a little and then repeats this movement twice.

#ALL-OF-US - [A], PO > NDS, FO up touches DS shoulder then [L^], PO > signer touches NDS shoulder.

SCADS-OF - (2h)[5:], POs down, FOs away, move forward to show many beings.

body-SHINY - SHINY - (2h)[open 8], POs > body, FOs away, twist rapidly at wrists while moving away.

The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

star-TWINKLE - (2h)[5^], POs awaqy, FOs up, flutter the fingers while separately slowly.
