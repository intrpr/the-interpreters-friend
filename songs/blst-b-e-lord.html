EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

Refrain: ||: PRAISE LORD HON.-INDEX. :|| GOD TOUCHED, WANT HELP US. GOD SAVE CAN. (<) ALL-NIGHT DARK, (>) ALL-DAY WAR, NOT SCARED ME. GOD TOUCHED, WANT HELP US. GOD SAVE CAN.

IF MY ENEMY THEY-lf TRY GRAB-HOLD-OF-me, HE HELP-me FREE. THEIR-lf WICKED PLAN, HE OVERTURN. HIS WINGS PROTECT, SUNSHINE NOT HURT. ME CELEBRATE, WHY? MY LIFE COMFORTABLE TOGETHER HIM. Refrain.

NIGHT TIME-PERIOD ME NOT NEED FEEL FEAR. DURING DAY ME NOT ALONE, GOD SUPPORT ME. DANGER CAN'T TOUCH ME. A-R-R-O-W (2h)alt.1-CL'arrows pass by me', NOTHING BAD HAPPEN UPSET MY SOUL WILL. Refrain.

SUPPOSE ONE THOUSAND MY SOLDIER DIE, ME CONFIDENT LORD PROTECT ME WILL. HIS LOVE STAY WITH ME. PROTECT ME LIKE SHIELD (1 traces around forearm). ME FIGHT AGAINST ENEMY STRONG. Refrain

Notes: The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

TOUCHED - Middle finger of [open 8] strikes heart.

GRAB-HOLD-OF-me - Both hands roughly grasp shirt at chest.

WICKED - (2h)[3] are repeatedly drawn to the temples while wg.

OVERTURN - (2h)[5:], DH PO down and held higher, NDH PO up and held lower exchange POs and respective levels.

(2h)alt.1-CL'arrows pass by me' - (2h)[1^], POs > signer, moves as if whizzing by head.

SHIELD - [S] - Held as if holding shield and FT of [1] traces a circle where the shield would be.

Lyrics:

Refrain: Blest be the Lord, blest be the Lord,
The God of mercy, the God who saves.
I shall not fear, the dark of night
Nor the arrow that flies by day.

He will release me from the nets of sinful men.
He will protect me from their wicked hands.
Beneath the shadow of His wings I will rejoice
To find a dwelling place secure.

I need not shrink before the terrors of the night
Nor stand alone before the light of day.
No harm shall come to me, no arrow strike me down,
No evil settle in my soul.

Although a thousand men have fallen at my side,
I'll not be shaken with the Lord at hand.
His faithful love is all the armor that I need
To wage my battle with my foe.
