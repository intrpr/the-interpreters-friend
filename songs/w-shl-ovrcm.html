Translated into ASL by David Bar-Tzur

EXPLANATION OF THE GLOSSING SYSTEM
(to understand how I describe the signs in this translation).

1. ||: (3x) OPPRESSION WE OPPRESSION-OFF :|| SOME-DAY.

Refrain: DEEP INSIDE-SELF HEART ME D-O BELIEVE
OPPRESSION WE OPPRESSION-OFF SOME-DAY.

2. ||: (3x) WE WALK HAND-IN-HAND :|| SOME-DAY. Refrain

3. ||: (3x) WE LIVE HAVE PEACE :|| SOME-DAY. Refrain

4. ||: (3x) WE NOT FEEL AFRAID :|| SOME-DAY. Refrain

5. ||: (3x) WE STAND TOGETHER :|| SOME-DAY. Refrain

6. ||: (3x) TRUTH MAKE US FREE :|| SOME-DAY. Refrain

7. ||: (3x) THE-rt LORD HELP US PATIENT :|| SOME-DAY. Refrain

8. ||: (3x) WE BECOME LIKE HIM-rt :|| SOME-DAY. Refrain

9. ||: (3x) WHOLE WORLD BECOME HOMOGENEOUS. :|| SOME-DAY. Refrain

Notes: The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

OPPRESSION - DH [B], PO down, FO away, pushs down twice on [S], PO > DS, FO away.

OPPRESSION-OFF - Starts like PRESSURE, DH [B], PO down, FO away, push down slightly on [S], PO > DS, FO away, then the DH is brought upwards suddenly and the signer mouths "off".

SOME-DAY - WILL/FUTURE but hands twists slightly at wrist.

INSIDE-SELF - The FT of [C] are held against the chest and stuff the FT of [O^] into the [C] twice.

HAND-IN-HAND - (2h)[Bb] interlink as in WEDDING and swing to and fro.

THE - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

HOMOGENEOUS - (2h)[Y], POs down, FOs away, circle horizontally. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - standard.
