Notes: GO-AHEAD - (2h)[B^ dot], POs ><, FOs away, move forward in a jerk.

KNOW-THAT - Starts like KNOW but twists and descends, looking almost like DON'T-KNOW except the hand twists down at the end.

WAVES-PARTING - (2h)[5:], POs down, knuckles touching, hands wg while separating.

WAY-DOWN - Index finger points downwards and descends in a wavy path.

PRAY - (2h)[B] press palms together and circle vertically normal (perpendicular) to the torso.

ON-MY-KNEES - Press knuckles against upturned palm of NDH and moves both in vertical circles normal (perpendicular) to the chest.

WAY-UP - Index finger points upwards and ascends in a wavey path.

J-H - JEHOVAH.

mouth-FLAME - Hold dorsal side (back) of hand against mouth and move away with wg and mouth grimacing open like that of a dragon.

TRAVEL-sky - [V:], PO down, FO away, moves > NDS in a wavy pattern.
