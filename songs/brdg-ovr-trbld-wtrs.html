Notes: YOU - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

EXHAUSTED - TIRED + [V], PO up, FO > NDS, is drawn with effort across the palm of [B], PO up, FO away.

DEFLATED - [C], PO > NDS, FO up, is held at midchest and closes to [O^]. You can hold [B], PO down, FO > DS underneath it.

TEAR-UP - (2h)[1] point to eyes and then bend into (2h)[X].

WIPE-AWAY-your-TEARS - First wipe away the tears under your own eyes, moving hands to side rather than down (looks nicer), then mime wiping away the tears of someone standing in front of you.

alt.GO-AWAY - (2h)[5], POs > signer, FOs up, alt. move away, closing to [O^].

The signs between "||:" and ":||" are to be repeated.

IDEA-LIKE - A blending where IDEA has a [Y] instead of an [I] in anticipation of LIKE which is signed smoothly right afterwards.

CRASH-SHORE - (2h)[B], NDH PO down, FO > DS, is held still and DH smacks against it and moves up with wg to show waves crashing on the rocks.

NOSE-DIVE - NDH [B], PO > DS, FO diagonally up, DH [Bb] dives down while side-swiping NDH palm.

alt.HIT-CHIN - with fists.

COMFORT - Like COMFORTABLE but signed more slowly.

DARK"step-by-step" - Hands move slowly with three sudden stops.

SURROUND - [5], PO down, FO away, circles above NDH [1], PO > DS, FO up.

SAILBOAT/SAIL

ARRIVE"step-by-step" - Hands move slowly with three sudden stops.

VISION - VISUALIZE.

SHINY - (2h)[open 8], POs down, FOs away, twist rapidly at wrists while rising. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Shine.

GO-WITH - (2h)[A] press palms together and move forward.
