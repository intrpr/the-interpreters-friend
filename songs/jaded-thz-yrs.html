Notes: #DO-THINGS - (2h)[D], POs up, FOs away, hands circle

STAY-AWAKE - (2h)[C], POs >< FOs up, hands held at eyes.

SCAR - FT of [X] scratches forehead above DS eyebrow.

WRECKLESS - (2h)[1] point to ears, then hands move forward and open to (2h)[5], PO > back of signer, FOs up, with brr.

SPEND - (2h)[S], POs up, are held at pockets, flick out index fingers and move up.

REGRET - [Y] twists thumb against chin.

CRAZY-FOR - [5:], PO > singer, FO up, is shaken slightly from side to side.

LOOK-BACK - (2h)[H or V] are brought back over the shoulder, POs down, FOs back.

TIME-PERIOD - [T], PO > NDS, FO up, travels in a vertical plane parallel to the NDH and makes contact with NDH [B], PO > DS, FO away. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Period - Time.

TIME-IS-TICKING - Use index finger to show the minute hand of your wrist watch ticking away.
