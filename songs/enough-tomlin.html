YOU-lf SACRIFICE YOURSELF-lf.

YOU-lf MORE-THAN ENOUGH FOR ME.

Notes: ALL - (2h)[B^], POs up, FOs ><, DH circles around NDH and lands on its palm. For a QuickTime file of this sign, see ASL Pro: Whole.

MORE-THAN - (2h)[B^], touch FTs, then DH moves up in an arc towards the signer and away. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - more than.

ME - Underlined words are honorific indices ("indexes"), using the FT of the upturned palm to indicate who is being honored.

BREATH-OUT - FTs of (2h)[O^] are > nostrils. Hands move away and open to (2h)[5] as if breathing out.

AWESOME - (2h)[S], POs ><, FOs up are held at eyes with awed facial expression and the hands open and then close.

SACRIFICE - (2h)[B], POs up, lift up hands and move them towards God.

SO-MUCH - (2h)[5:], POs ><, FOs away, separate while "so much" is mouthed.
