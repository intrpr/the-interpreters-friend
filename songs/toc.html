I have translated over a thousand songs throughout my career and post them here as I continue to do so. I have added some explanation after each songs to help you understand what I am trying to express by my glossing. For further help, see Glossing system used for songs and ritual texts. One feature that comes up often in the glossing of these songs is the HONORIFIC-INDEX, that is when the upturned [B^] points the FT at the person or persons (also God) that is being referred to and descends slightly. I have indicated this by underlining the pronoun: ME, YOU, HE, etc., with (2h)YOU, being used for the plural form of "you". English lyrics have been included for most songs. If a title is followed by this icon: , there is a link to a video of that song in ASL on that page.
