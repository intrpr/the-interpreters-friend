Footnotes

  1. FOREFATHERS - (2h)[A], FOs up, DH - held against forehead, NDH close to head but lower, both hands open to (2h)[5] while moving upwards + (2h)[5], POs ><, FOs up, move in a vertical circle while moving back over shoulder.
  2. ABRAHAM - [A] strikes between the thumb and forefinger of [B], like BOTHER, but a single movement.
  3. ISAAC - [I] taps fingertip against throat.
  4. WORSHIP - NDH is clasped over DH [S] and hands circle perpendicular to the chest. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Worship.
  5. PASS-DOWN'bit by bit' - (2h)[O^], circle around each other from the shoulder and moving forward, but the action is stop and go.
  6. ADVANCED* - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, move upwards arcing towards the signer with an emphatic movement.
  7. INDEX-LIST-OF-3 - NDH [3], PO > signer, FO > DS, and DH points to thumb, then moves > DS and down, then points to the index finger, and similarly with the middle finger to say we are referring to all the previously listed items.
  8. INTERNALIZE - The FT of [C] are held against the chest and stuff the sign INVOLVED is generated at that location.
  9. MESSIAH - M-KING.
  10. MIRACLE - WONDERFUL* WORK.
  11. (2h)#ALL - (2h)[A], POs ><, FOs up, move away while opening to (2h)[L].
  12. CAUSE - (2h)[S] are held at the chest as if holding a rope and move forward while opening to (2h)[5]. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - cause.
  13. SHIELD - NDH [B], PO > signer, FO > DS, is held close to the body, and the DH is [5], PO away, FO up, and held further away. The DH circles CCW as seen by signer as if there is something there to be shielded from. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - shield.
  14. SOFT-HEARTED - HEART + (2h)[5^] descend slightly twice while closing to (2h)[O^].
  15. HAVE-MERCY-ON-us - (2h)[open 8], POs > signer, hands move in vertical circles normal (perpendicular) to the chest.
  16. (2h)COME-ON - (2h)[B^] move in circles pivoting at the wrists to beckon someone to do something.
  17. OPPRESSION - DH [B] presses down on [S], PO > signer, FO > DS, until NDH tips over and has PO up.
  18. OPPRESSION-OFF - Starts like PRESSURE, DH [B], PO down, FO away, push down slightly on [S], PO > DS, FO away, then the DH is brought upwards suddenly and the signer mouths "off".
  19. LOWERED-INTO-GRAVE - [B], PO > DS, FO away, acts as one wall of the grave and [U], PO down, FO away, moves down as if it is a body being lowered into a grave.
  20. HEAVENLY-FATHER - (2h)[A], FOs up, DH is held against forehead, NDH close to head but lower, both hands open to (2h)[5] while moving upwards.
  21. TAKE-OFF - (2h)[B], DHPO > DS, DHFO away, NDHFO diagonally upwards, shoots rapidly upwards while brushing against ND palm.
  22. FAR-AWAY-Heaven - [1] points to Heaven and twists rapidly as it moves in that direction.
  23. SCADS-OF - (2h)[5:], POs down, FOs away, move forward to show many beings.
  24. angels-SAME-AS-us - (2h)[Y], POs ><, FOs up, oscillate between the place where Heaven is spatialized and the signer.
  25. HOLY++-upward - H-CLEAN is repeated while moving upwards.
  26. BODY-BURN - (2h)[5wg] move up the chest, referring to the seraphim (angels), literally "the burning ones".
  27. REALLY-WANT - the sign WANT but shaken back and forth to show intensity.
  28. KING-DAVID - D-KING.
  29. ANOINTED - [A dot] circles head with DH thumb pointing down.
  30. UPRIGHT - elbow of [S], PO > NDS, FO up, - struck against the fingers of NDH [B] held like part of TABLE.
  31. LABEL - FT of [H], PO towards, are drawn horizontally across palm of [B], PO away, FO up.
  32. CROWN - (2h)[open 8] place crown on head with small double movement.
  33. PLACE-CROWN-ON-HEAD-lf - (2h)[open 8] place crown on head with large single movement.
  34. TABLET - Use [B] to show the traditional outline of the tablets.
  35. BRING-DOWN - (2h)[B], POs up, FOs away, carry the tables down from the mountain.
  36. SABBATH - The DH [F], PO down, descends between NDH [B], PO down, at the wrist area and the body, then moves away from the body and rises up while changing to [S], PO down.
  37. COVENANT - AGREE + RELATIONSHIP moves back and forth between where God is spatilized and the signer.
  38. STATUE - (2h)[A dot], POs away, FOs up, outline shape.
  39. CIRCUMCISE - (2h)[A dot], POs down, DHFT circles NDHFT as if cutting off foreskin and ends with DELETE.
  40. COMMANDMENTS - C-LAW+.
  41. PURE - like NICE but the DH moves backwards slightly on the palm, pauses, then moves forward rapidly to show intensity.
  42. FAVOR - [open 8] taps chin twice.
  43. GO-#BACK - (2h)[B] move towards the spatialized area while rapidly transitioning through [C] to [K].
  44. GO-AHEAD - (2h)[B^ dot], POs ><, FOs away, move forward in a jerk.
  45. KIND - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs away, NDH is held close to heart and DH circles around it.
  46. PASSOVER - There are many signs for "Passover"; I sign the old one: [S] taps thumbside against elbow (like CRACKER).
  47. SUKKOT - (2h)[A] is shaken like the sign TOGETHER, but slowly and with an exaggerated movement.
  48. alt.GIVE-FROM-HEART-TO-lf - alt. [O^] touches heart and opens as it moves outwards.
  49. WITNESS - [1] touches under eye while head moves down.
  50. FOUNDATION - NDH [S], PO > signer, FO > DS, - held out with arm parallel to the chest and DH [S], PO > signer, strikes hand from below and then strikes elbow from below.
  51. FROM-TIME-TO-TIME - [B^], PO > NDS, FO away, hops away from signer in upward arcs.
  52. SOFT-HEARTED - Produce the sign SOFT close to your heart.
  53. LOVING - LOVE++.
  54. MIRACLE - WONDERFUL(1x) WORK. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Miracle.
  55. COHEN - The hands are held with thumbs touching, POs face away from signer, and the fingers are held together but split between the middle and ring fingers, like Mr. Spock on Star Trek (Leonard Nemoy is a cohen and got the gesture from this one which is used to bless the people during holidays).
  56. LIGHT-MENORAH - (2h)[bX], DH strikes NDH like a match, and then DH moves up to the menorah and moves > DS in upward arcs as if lighting a series of candles.
  57. CHANNUKAH - (2h)[4], POs away (some use POs >) signer, FOs up, index fingers touch and then separate while sweeping up in an arc, like the arms of a candelabra.
  58. LONG-LIST - (2h)[O^], POs > signer, NDH - above and touches DH; both hands open to (2h)[5] while DH descends.
  59. TAKE-OFF - like DEVELOP but moves quicklt and abruptly with a "po".
  60. TRUE~BIZ - TRUE + (2h)[S], POs down, FOs away, DH brushes from side to side across NDH wrist.
  61. USED-TO-BE - [5], PO > NDS, FO up, circles over shoulder.
  62. AARON - (2h)[A], FTs touch area under the throat, separate while moving to the shoulders, then descend down the sides of the chest.
  63. 5-LOOK - [5], PO > signer, circles face.
  64. SHINE-DOWN-GRACE - [O^], held high and PO > Heaven, supinates and opens to [5] while being brought towards head while wg.
  65. INSIDE-SELF - The FT of [C] are held against the chest and stuff the FT of [O^] into the [C] twice.
