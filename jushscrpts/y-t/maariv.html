Footnotes

  1. All glosses marked with a footnote are described below. For an explanation of other glossing symbols used, see Explanation of glossing used. DARK'gradually' - (2h)[B], POs over shoulder, FOs up, slowly cross but with tiny stops and starts.
  2. SHUT-DOWN - (2h)[5], POs away, FOs up, are held side by side and both close to (2h)[O^].
  3. INDEX-LIST - index finger of DH points to thumb, then index finger, then middle finger of NDH [5], PO > signer, FO > DS.
  4. REALLY-WANT - the sign WANT but shaken back and forth to show intensity.
  5. KNOW-THAT - Starts like KNOW but twists and descends, looking almost like DON'T-KNOW except the hand twists down at the end.
  6. ONLY-ONE - [1], PO away, FO up, supinates and moves upwards slightly.
  7. MOSES - M-LAW.
  8. SCADS-OF - (2h)[5:], POs down, FOs away, move forward to show many beings.
  9. HAVE-NONE - (2h)[B], POs > signer move upwards past mouth and swing upwards and apart while mouth blows on hands as they pass.
  10. SEPARATE++-away - SEPARATE is signed repeatedly while the hands move away from the body to show the splitting of the Red Sea.
  11. JACOB - Could be fingerspelled or signed as a [J] across the chin.
  12. OPPRESSION-OFF - Starts like PRESSURE, DH [B], PO down, FO away, push down slightly on [S], PO > DS, FO away, then the DH - brought upwards suddenly and the signer mouths "off".
  13. TENT'stretch over head' - Perhaps this gloss is obvious, but the sign TENT is produced over the signer's head.
  14. SABBATH - The DH [F], PO down, descends between NDH [B], PO down, at the wrist area and the body, then moves away from the body and rises up while changing to [S], PO down.
  15. CONTINUALLY - the sign CONTINUE moves in a vertical circle orthogonal (perpendicular) to the chest.
  16. COVENANT - AGREE RELATIONSHIP-WITH-God.
  17. PUSH-ASIDE - (2h)[B], with DH higher, palms are pressed against imaginary wall that - pushed to one side.
  18. GET-OFF-MIND - (2h)[open 8], touch forehead, pronate and move away.
  19. FESTIVAL - (2h)[5], POs ><, tap thumbs on respective sides of chest.
  20. NAME~SHINY - Starts like the sign NAME, the NDH is left in place while the DH touches it with the [open 8] (as in the sign FEEL), then moves upwards while twisting the wrist.
  21. #ALL-OF-YOU - [A], PO up, FO > DS - held at DS then swings away in an arc and ends [L^], PO up, FO > NDS.
  22. ADVANCED - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, move upwards arcing towards the signer.
  23. (1-OF-3), (2-OF-3), (3-OF-3) - NDH [3], PO > signer, FO > DS, and DH taps thumb for "(1-OF-3)", taps index finger for "(2-OF-3)", and taps middle finger for "(3-OF-3). (1-OF-4), later in this text, has a similar structure.
  24. DO-UP - (2h)[5], POs down, FOs away, move in symmetric, vertical circles parallel to the chest while rising.
  25. FOREFATHERS - (2h)[A], FOs up, DH - held against forehead, NDH close to head but lower, both hands open to (2h)[5] while moving upwards + (2h)[5], POs ><, FOs up, move in a vertical circle while moving back over shoulder.
  26. ABRAHAM - [A] strikes between the thumb and forefinger of [B], like BOTHER, but a single movement.
  27. ISAAC - [I] taps fingertip against throat.
  28. JACOB - Could be fingerspelled or signed as a [J] across the chin.
  29. PASS-DOWN'bit by bit' - (2h)[O^], circle around each other from the shoulder and moving forward, but the action is stop and go.
  30. GIFT-TO - (2h)[X], POs ><, FOs up, hands move forward and FOs end pointing to the person that something is being given to freely.
  31. TRUE~BIZ - TRUE + (2h)[S], POs down, FOs away, DH brushes from side to side across NDH wrist.
  32. GET-OFF-MIND - (2h)[open 8] touch forehead, then pronate and move away.
  33. SET-ASIDE - (2h)[B] push topic aside.
  34. HOLIDAY - (2h)[5], POs >&l;, FOs up, alt. tap thumb against check on respective sides.
  35. PASSOVER - [S] taps thumbside against elbow (like CRACKER). For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - cracker.
  36. SHAVUOT - EIGHT-WEEKS.
  37. SUKKOT - (2h)[A] is shaken like the sign TOGETHER, but slowly and with an exaggerated movement.
  38. ASSEMBLE - (2h)[5] close to (2h)[O^] diagonally with DH high and NDH low and then diagonally with hands vice versa. Motion is the same as ADD-TOGETHER. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - assemble.
  39. LOOK-BACK - (2h)[N], POs > back, FOs up, move back over shoulder.
  40. (2h)#ALL - (2h)[A], POs ><, FOs up, are held at shoulder height and move away while opening to (2h)[L].
  41. WHITTLE - Thumb of [A dot] twists at back hand of [S], PO down, FO away, as if whittling a piece out.
  42. CHISEL - (2h)[S], DH mimes hammering and NDH holding a chisel.
  43. BEND-KNEES - [V] stands on palm of NDH, then bend DH FT while maintaining contact with palm.
  44. BOW-DOWN - (2h)[B], POs away, FOs up, are held like the final position of GIVE-UP, then bend these arms twice while bowing at waist once.
  45. TENT'stretch over head' - (2h)[horns] touch FT and move diagonally away and down, but this is done above head and encloses the signer's body.
  46. (2h)5:-CL'stars in the sky' - (2h)[5:], POs diagonally up, hands jab at space above while descending.
  47. MULTIPLE-COMPLEX - (2h)[4], POs ><, FOs up, hands pass each other while wg.
  48. BIG-QUESTION - like the sign QUESTION-MARK but both hands and all fingers (not the thumb) form a symmetric shape that - a question mark in the right hand and a reversed question mark in the left hand.
  49. TOP-PRIORITY - palm of DH [5] strikes index finger of [5], PO > signer, FO > DS.
  50. DEFINITE - (2h)[5], POs ><, FOs away are held in neutral space and descend slightly and abruptly while closing to (2h)[F].
  51. INSIDE-SELF - The FT of [C] are held against the chest and stuff the FT of [O^] into the [C] twice.
  52. FAR-FUTURE"lalala" - [B], PO > NDS, FO away, is held at shoulder height and moves forward like FUTURE, but it moves farther than usual and the tongue is shuttle from side to side.
