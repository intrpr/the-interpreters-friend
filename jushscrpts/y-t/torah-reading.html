BLESSING OF THE NEW MONTH

On the Sabbath preceding Rosh Chodesh, a special blessing for the new month is recited. The reader stands at the bimah and he or a congregant next to him holds the Torah Scroll.

(CONTINUE PRAY QUIET)...

Footnotes

  1. All glosses marked with a footnote are described below. For an explanation of other glossing symbols used, see Explanation of glossing used. MIRACLE - WONDERFUL* WORK.
  2. KINGDOM - KING + [5], PO down, circle horizontally.
  3. LONG-LONG-AGO - (2h)[5], POs ><, FOs up, move back over the shoulder while circling vertically.
  4. GIFT-TO - (2h)[X], POs ><, FOs up, hands nod down until FOs are away.
  5. HEAVENLY-FATHER - (2h)[A], FOs up, DH - held against forehead, NDH close to head but lower, both hands open to (2h)[5] while moving upwards.
  6. JERUSALEM - [B with thumbs flush with other fingers but not folded into palm], touches mouth and then pronates.
  7. ADVANCED - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, move upwards arcing towards the signer.
  8. RAISE-UP - (2h)[B], POs odnw, FOs away, lower slightly, supinate, and rise up.
  9. GROUP-WANDER - (2h)[C], POs ><, FOs away, are held as if to contain the group and the hands move away together in a random pattern as if wandering with th.
  10. (2h)F-CL'staves perpendicular to body' - (2h)[F], POs ><, FOs down, start at respective sides of the body and move ouward to show the handles.
  11. (2h)1-CL'@ each corner' (2h)S-CL'bear on shoulder' CARRY'forward' - (2h)[1], POs away, FOs up, show the two people on the left side of the ark (front and back) and then the two on the right + (2h)[S] are positioned as if holding a stave over the shoulder + (2h)[1], POs up, show something being carried forward at shoulder level.
  12. GROUP-MOVE-FORWARD - (2h)[C], POs ><, FOs away, hands are cupped together and moved forward to show action as a group.
  13. MOSES - M-LAW.
  14. TORAH - (2h)[S], POs up, twist twice while separating, as if unrolling a scroll.
  15. BLOW-TOP - [5], PO down, FO away, slaps top of [S], PO > DS, FO away, and then moves upwards while DH swivels at the wrist.
  16. OFF-POINT-TO-lf-AND-rt - DH [S], PO > NDS, flicks finger off to the left and then to the right.
  17. (CONTINUE PRAY QUIET) - This is meant to be signed, but it is really a comment from the interpreter to describe to the Deaf people what is happening in the service.
  18. TRUE~BIZ - TRUE + (2h)[S], POs down, FOs away, DH brushes from side to side across NDH wrist.
  19. GLORY - (2h)[5], POs><, FOs away from signer, DH which - on top wiggles fingers while ascending. For an animated gif of this sign, see Animated dictionary of religious signs - Deaf Missions: Glory.
  20. (2h)#ALL - (2h)[A], POs ><, FOs up, move away while opening to (2h)[L].
  21. ATTEND-TO-me - PAY-ATTENTION.
  22. KNOW-THAT - Starts like KNOW but twists FT away and descends, looking almost like DON'T-KNOW except the hand twists down at the end.
  23. ONLY-ONE - [1], PO away, FO up, twists so that PO > signer, and the hand moves up slightly.
  24. NAME~SHINY - Starts like the sign NAME, the NDH is left in place while the DH touches it with the [open 8] (as in the sign FEEL), then moves upwards while twisting the wrist.
  25. #ALL-FIVE-ON-LIST - [A] is held higher than NDH [5], PO > DS, FO away, and DH descends while closing to an [L]. Similarly for #ALL-THREE-ON-LIST which occurs later on.
  26. FOREFATHERS - GREAT-GREAT-GRANDFATHER, that is, [5] touches thumb to temple and moves away inthree upward arcs + (2h)LONG-LONG-AGO, that is, (2h)[5], POs ><, FOs up, move back over the shoulder while circling vertically. Personally, I like the Reform way of saying "forefathers and foremothers" so a way you could do this it to use your nondominant hand to sign GREAT-GREAT-GRANDMOTHER simultaneously with GREAT-GREAT-GRANDFATHER and add (2h)LONG-LONG-AGO.
  27. SUDDEN-WRONG - [Y], PO > NDS, FO up, twist at wrist and strikes chin.
  28. #ALL-OF-YOU - [A], PO up, FO > DS - held at DS then swings away in an arc and ends [L^], PO up, FO > NDS.
  29. COHEN - The hands are held with thumbs touching, POs face away from signer, and the fingers are held together but split between the middle and ring fingers, like Mr. Spock on Star Trek (Leonard Nemoy is a cohen and got the gesture from this one which is used to bless the people during holidays).
  30. COME-ON - (2h)[B] move in circles pivoting at the wrists to beckon someone to do something.
  31. IDEA~LIKE - a blending where IDEA has a [Y] instead of an [I] in anticipation of LIKE which - signed smoothly right afterwards.
  32. alt.C-CL'pick fruit from rt and eat' - alt. [C] grabs fruit, brings it to mouth and takes a bite.
  33. BLOSSOM - (2h)[A] are held together but the hands are twisted like the beginning of CHANGE. Hands twist back while opening to (2h)[5:] with the hands still held together.
  34. REALLY-WANT - the sign WANT but shaken back and forth to show intensity.
  35. MORE-AND-MORE - MORE is signed twice but with the NDH kept in place.
  36. (1-OF-4), (2-OF-4), (3-OF-4), and so on - NDH [4], PO > signer, FO > DS, and DH taps index finger for "(1-OF-4)", taps middle finger for "(2-OF-4)", and similarly with the ring finger and little finger.
  37. FOUNDATION - NDH [S], PO > signer, FO > DS, - held out with arm parallel to the chest and DH [S], PO > signer, strikes hand from below and then strikes elbow from below.
  38. BEFORE - [B^], is held over shoulders, FO > back, and nods.
  39. ELIJAH - [E], PO away, FO up, moves under [B], PO down, FO > DS, and past it.
  40. FORETELL - [V], PO > back, FO up, moves under [B], PO down, FO > DS, and past it. There are other signs for this.
  41. ANOINTED - [A dot] circles head with DH thumb pointing down.
  42. KING-DAVID - D-KING.
  43. THRONE - SIT + (2h)[B:], POs down, FOs away, behave as if they were on the arms of a throne.
  44. SWEAR - (2h)[B], NDH held on imaginary Bible and DH held up in oath. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - inauguration.
  45. PASSOVER - [S] taps thumbside against elbow (like CRACKER). For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - cracker.
  46. SHAVUOT - 7-WEEKS.
  47. SUKKOT - (2h)[A] is shaken like the sign TOGETHER, but slowly and with an exaggerated movement.
  48. ASSEMBLE - (2h)[S], FO > signer, move > signer while opening to (2h)[5wg].
  49. SYNAGOGUE - [S], PO down, FO away, strikes [S], PO down, FO > DS, then [Y] strikes NDH.
  50. OPPRESSION-OFF - Starts like PRESSURE, DH [B], PO down, FO away, push down slightly on [S], PO > DS, FO away, then the DH - brought upwards suddenly and the signer mouths "off".
  51. #ALL-OF-US - [A], PO > NDS, FO up touches DS shoulder then [L^], PO > signer touches NDS shoulder.
  52. FRIEND'circles horizontally' - (2h)[X] interlinked moves in a horizontal circle.
  53. PSALM - P-SONG.
  54. CRY-OUT - [5:] is held in front of mouth and moves up and away.
  55. CRUSH - (2h)[5], palm of DH strikes NDH palm like the beginning of SCHOOL but twists slowly after striking.
  56. (2h)SPEAKwg - (2h)[4wg], FOs up, are held with DH against chin and the NDH against the DH.
  57. alt.TREE-FALL"pow" - [5], PO away, FO up, places its elbow against the top of NDH [5], PO down, FO > DS, and "falls" forward while still rooted to the NDH while mouthing "pow".
  58. LEBANON - DH [S], PO up, is held next to [B], PO > DS, FO away from signer. DH opens to [5] 2x.
  59. SYRIA - [W], PO > signer, touches FTs to palm of [B], PO > DS, FO away from signer.
  60. (2h)5-CL'rages forward' - (2h)[5], POs > signer, FOs up, hands move forward quickly.
  61. BARREN - (2h)[open 8], POs down, FOs away, move away while closing to (2h)[A], while mouth "thp".
  62. SHAKE - (2h)[5], POs down, FOs away, move side to side antisymmetrically.
  63. STRIP-OFF-BARK - [5:] runs its FT down the side of [5], PO > DS, FO up, and closes to an [A] as if stripping bark.
  64. PUT-DOWN - (2h)[B], POs up, FOs away, move downwards as if puting something down.
  65. #BACK-TOGETHER-God - (2h)[B], POs ><, FOs up, are held with NDH close to the body and the DH high and close to where God is spatialized. The hands transition to a C and then a K while rushing towards each other and contacting.
