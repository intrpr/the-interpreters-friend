When the circumcision is complete, the baby is given to one of the prominent guests to hold, while the following prayers (including the giving of the name) are recited. Footnotes

  1. All glosses marked with a footnote are described below. For an explanation of other glossing symbols used, see Explanation of glossing used. (2h)#ALL - (2h)[A], POs ><, FOs up, move away while opening to (2h)[L].
  2. PUT-UP-WITH - Same signs as PATIENT.
  3. CIRCUMCISE - There are many signs for this. My preferred is (2h)[A dot], POs down, DHFT circles NDHFT as if cutting off foreskin + flick thumb against index finger and move hand upwards as if flicking something away.
  4. MOHEL - This is the "rabbi" who is performing the circumcision. Use whichever sign you want for "circumcise" or see above and add AGENT.
  5. INDEX-'Elijah's chair' - Point to the chair where the person who is holding the baby on a pillow (sandek) will sit.
  6. V:-CL 'spirit descends from heaven, sits in chair' - With the CL to show Elijah descending from Heaven and landing in Elijah's chair.
  7. WITNESS - [1] touches under eye while head moves down.
  8. REALLY-WANT - the sign WANT but shaken back and forth to show intensity.
  9. IDEA~LIKE - a blending where IDEA has a [Y] instead of an [I] in anticipation of LIKE which - signed smoothly right afterwards.
  10. TORAH - (2h)[S], POs up, twist twice while separating, as if unrolling a scroll.
  11. INSIDE-SELF - The FT of [C] are held against the chest and stuff the FT of [O^] into the [C] twice.
  12. JERUSALEM - [B with thumbs flush with other fingers but not folded into palm], touches mouth and then pronates.
  13. KNOW-THAT - Starts like KNOW but twists and descends, looking almost like DON'T-KNOW except the hand twists down at the end.
  14. KNOW-THAT - Starts like KNOW but twists and descends, looking almost like DON'T-KNOW except the hand twists down at the end.
  15. ABRAHAM - [A] strikes between the thumb and forefinger of [B], like BOTHER, but a single movement.
  16. ABRAHAM - [A] strikes between the thumb and forefinger of [B], like BOTHER, but a single movement.
  17. COVENANT - AGREE RELATIONSHIP-WITH-God.
  18. INSIDE-SELF - The FT of [C] are held against the chest and stuff the FT of [O^] into the [C] twice.
  19. GO-AHEAD - (2h)[B^ dot], POs ><, FOs away, move forward in a jerk.
  20. JUMP-UP-AND-DOWN - FT of [V] bend and jump away from NDH [B], PO up, FO away.
  21. BLOOM - (2h)[A] are held together but the hands are twisted like the beginning of CHANGE. Hands twist back while opening to (2h)[5:] with the hands still held together.
  22. NEXT-IN-LIST - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, both descend with an inward arc.
  23. ISAAC - [I] taps fingertip against throat.
  24. JACOB - Could be fingerspelled or signed as a [J] across the chin.
  25. #ALL-OF-YOU - [A], PO up, FO > DS - held at DS then swings away in an arc and ends [L^], PO up, FO > NDS.
  26. THRONE - SIT + (2h)[B:], POs down, FOs away, behave as if they were on the arms of a throne.
  27. SHINY - (2h)[open 8], POs down, FOs away, twist rapidly at wrists while rising.
  28. WHITTLE - Thumb of [A dot] twists at back hand of [S], PO down, FO away, as if whittling a piece out.
  29. CHISELCHISEL - (2h)[S], DH mimes hammering and NDH holding a chisel.
  30. BEND-KNEES - [V] stands on palm of NDH, then bend DH FT while maintaining contact with palm.
  31. BOW-DOWN - (2h)[B], POs away, FOs up, are held like the final position of GIVE-UP, then bend these arms twice while bowing at waist once.
  32. TENT'stretch over head' - (2h)[horns] touch FT and move diagonally away and down, but this is done above head and encloses the signer's body.
  33. (2h)5:-CL'stars in the sky' - (2h)[5:], POs diagonally up, hands jab at space above while descending.
  34. NAME~SHINY - starts like the sign NAME, the NDH - left in place while the DH touches it with the [open 8] (as in the sign FEEL), then moves upwards while twisting the wrist.
  35. NOTHING-TO-IT - (2h)[F], POs away, FOs up, hands move slightly and symetrically from side to side.
  36. SUPREME - (2h)[A], FOs away, DH starts below NDH and arcs around it until it is on top.
  37. COMPLETE* - (2h)[B^], POs up, FOs ><, DH circles around NDH and lands on its palm emphatically.
  38. ADVANCED - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, move upwards arcing towards the signer.
  39. INDEX-LIST-OF-3 - NDH [3], PO > signer, FO > DS, and DH points to thumb, then moves > DS and down, then points to the index finger, and similarly with the middle finger to say we are referring to all the previously listed items.
  40. HEAVENLY-FATHER - (2h)[A], FOs up, DH - held against forehead, NDH close to head but lower, both hands open to (2h)[5] while moving upwards.
