Footnotes

  1. All glosses marked with a footnote are described below. For an explanation of other glossing symbols used, see Explanation of glossing used. JUST - (2h)[B^], FOs up, tap FTs together while circling horizontally.
  2. POSS."each" - [B], PO away, points to a series of imaginary people before the signer to indicate what they're like.
  3. OPPRESSION - DH [B], PO down, FO away, push down twice on [S], PO > DS, FO away.
  4. OPPRESSION-OFF - Starts like PRESSURE, DH [B], PO down, FO away, push down slightly on [S], PO > DS, FO away, then the DH is brought upwards suddenly and the signer mouths "off".
  5. LOWERED-INTO-GRAVE - NDH [B], PO > DS, FO away, represents side of grave and DH [U], PO down, FO away, is lowered next to NDH.
  6. IDEA~LIKE - a blending where IDEA has a [Y] instead of an [I] in anticipation of LIKE which - signed smoothly right afterwards.
  7. FOUNDATION - NDH [S], PO > signer, FO > DS, - held out with arm parallel to the chest and DH [S], PO > signer, strikes hand from below and then strikes elbow from below.
  8. UPRIGHT - elbow of [S], PO > NDS, FO up, - struck against the fingers of NDH [B] held like part of TABLE.
  9. BLEND-TOGETHER - same sign as MAINSTREAM.
  10. NERVE - closed FTs of [F], FOs >NDS, strike chin emphatically.
  11. ISAAC - [I] taps fingertip against throat.
  12. BOUND - cross wrists behind back.
  13. OVERFLOW - [5], PO down, FO away, covers top of [S], PO > DS, FO away, and FT wiggle as the hand moves away, like a cup overflowing.
  14. KNOW-THAT - Starts like KNOW but twists and descends, looking almost like DON'T-KNOW except the hand twists down at the end.
  15. PURE - like NICE but the DH moves backwards slightly on the palm, pauses, then moves forward rapidly to show intensity.
  16. FAIR - same sign as EQUAL but mouth "fair".
  17. LONG-LIST - (2h)[O^], POs > signer, NDH - above and touches DH; both hands open to (2h)[5] while DH descends.
  18. STRAY-LEFT-AND-RIGHT - like the sign OFF-THE-POINT but the DH flicks index finger to one side and then to the other side; it's another way to sign the concept "sin".
  19. NAME~SHINY - starts like the sign NAME, the NDH - left in place while the DH touches it with the [open 8] (as in the sign FEEL), then moves upwards while twisting the wrist.
  20. #ALL-OF-YOU - [A], PO up, FO > DS - held at DS then swings away in an arc and ends [L^], PO up, FO > NDS.
  21. ADVANCED - (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, move upwards arcing towards the signer.
  22. (1-OF-3), (2-OF-3), (3-OF-3) - NDH [3], PO > signer, FO > DS, and DH taps thumb for "(1-OF-3)", DH [1] taps index finger for "(2-OF-3)", and taps middle finger for "(3-OF-3).
  23. INDEX-LIST-OF-3 - NDH [3], PO > signer, FO > DS, and DH points to thumb, then moves > DS and down, then points to the index finger, and similarly with the middle finger to say we are referring to all the previously listed items.
  24. FAR-AWAY - [1], twists while moving away with tongue shuttling from side to side.
