Footnotes

  1. All glosses marked with a footnote are described below. For an explanation of other glossing symbols used, see Explanation of glossing used. TRUE~BIZ - TRUE + S-BUSINESS.
  2. BLOSSOM - (2h)[A] starts like CHANGE but FT open to (2h)[5:].
  3. INDEX-LIST - index finger of DH points to thumb, then index finger, then middle finger of NDH [5], PO > signer, FO > DS.
  4. LONG-LIST - (2h)[O^], POs > signer, NDH is above and touches DH; both hands open to (2h)[5] while DH descends.
  5. FOREFATHERS - (2h)[A], FOs up, DH is held against forehead, NDH close to head but lower, both hands open to (2h)[5] while moving upwards + (2h)[5], POs ><, FOs up, move in a vertical circle while moving back over shoulder.
  6. PRIEST - (2h)[L], FTs touch the chest just below the neck, separate while still touching and then descend down the sides of the chest.
  7. TORAH - (2h)[S], POs up, twist twice while separating, as if unrolling a scroll.
  8. PURE - like NICE but the DH moves backwards slightly on the palm, pauses, then moves forward rapidly to show intensity.
  9. MORDECHAI - M-JEW, looks like SWEET.
  10. ESTHER - E-QUEEN.
  11. SHUSHAN - STONE + (2h)B-CL'surrounding wall', that is, B-GROUP.
  12. HAMAN - (2h)[H] touch under nose and roll against the thumbs as they descend, as if Haman had a curly moustache like the villains in the silent movies.
  13. COVENANT - AGREE + RELATED which moves back and forth between Heaven and the signer.
  14. (2h)YOU - the honorific index for you plural.
  15. HEAVENLY-FATHER - (2h)[A], FOs up, DH - held against forehead, NDH close to head but lower, both hands open to (2h)[5] while moving upwards.
  16. PAY-FOR - [O^], PO > person one is paying for, moves towards person while closing to an [A].
  17. GO-AHEAD - (2h)[B^ dot], POs ><, FOs away, move forward in a jerk.
  18. alt.GIVE-FROM-HEART-TO-lf - the hands alternately move from the heart to the left opening from [O^] to [5^].
  19. NAME~SHINY starts like the sign NAME, the NDH - left in place while the DH touches it with the [open 8] (as in the sign FEEL), then moves upwards while twisting the wrist.
  20. RENOVATE ADVANCE'step-by-step' - the sign IMPROVE but with a circular motion as in "remodel" + (2h)[B^], POs ><, FOs up, move upward in hops.
  21. OFF-OPPRESSION starts like PRESSURE, DH [B], PO down, FO away, push down slightly on [S], PO > DS, FO away, then the DH is brought upwards suddenly and the signer mouths "off".
  22. MANY-LOOK-TO-God - (2h)[4] move FT towards Heaven as if many eyes were looking to God.
  23. #ALL-OF-US - thumb of [A] touches DS collarbone, hands changes to [L] and index finger touches NDS collarbone.
  24. ABRAHAM - [A] strikes between the thumb and forefinger of [B], like BOTHER, but a single movement.
  25. ISAAC - [I] taps fingertip against throat.
  26. JACOB - [I] traces jawline but under lower lip.
  27. LIST-OF-THREE"each" - DH [1] points to the three FT of NDH [3], PO > singer, FO > DS.
  28. COME-ON - (2h)[B] makes hands circle towards chest like summoning someone.
  29. UPRIGHT - elbow of [S], PO > NDS, FO up, is struck against NDH [S] held in position like TABLE.
