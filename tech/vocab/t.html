truncate
~ (intentionally)
(1) [V] closes like scissors cutting once at [Bb], PO down, FO > DS. (2) [V] closes like scissors cutting once at [Bb], PO down, FO > DS + SUMMARRIZE. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see ASL browser - truncate.

~ (unintentionally)
