Declaration of Unity - Alcoholics Anonymous

David Bar-Tzur

Created 28 December 2007, links updated monthly with the help of LinkAlarm.

(Explanation of the glossing system)

WE RESPONSIBLE HELP A-A FUTURE:

TOP-PRIORITY, WE SUCCEED TOGETHER.

SOCIALIZE MEAN WE UNITED.

WHY? OUR LIFE DEPEND A-A UNITED.

WE PASS-DOWN MUST.

Notes: TOP-PRIORITY - palm of DH [5] strikes index finger of [5], PO > signer, FO > DS.

Text:

This we owe to A.A.'s future:

To place our common welfare first;

To keep our fellowship united.

For on A.A. unity depend our lives,

And the lives of those to come.
