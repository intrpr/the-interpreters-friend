Explanation of glossing system used
(to understand how I describe the signs in this dictionary)

Addis Ababa ()
(1) [A], PO down, moves down then moves in arc > DS.1 (< the abbreviation for Addis Ababa (A.A.).) (2) [A dot], PO < NDS, moves down then moves in arc > DS.1 (< the initial letter of the name in the Geeze alphabet.)
