Explanation of glossing system used
(to understand how I describe the signs in this dictionary)

Athens (Αθηνα)
DH [A dot] touches thumbtip to [B], PO up, FO away from signer and then is drawn back slightly.1 For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see Woordenboek Nederlands - Vlaamse Gebarentaal: Athene.

Crete (Κρητη)
Note: I found this sign on a Belgian website, so it may not be indigenous, but most of the signs in this site are quite accurate, so given that, it's reasonable to use it until proven otherwise. [1] twists at DS of neck. For a QuickTime movie of this sign, see Woordenboek Nederlands - Vlaamse Gebarentaal: Kreta.
