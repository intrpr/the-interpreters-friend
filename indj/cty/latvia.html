Explanation of glossing system used
(to understand how I describe the signs in this dictionary)

Daugavpils
[3], PO > NDS, FO up, moves past chin by swiveling at the elbow and then the had returns to place. To see an animation of this sign, go to Latviesu Zimju Valodas Vardnica: Daugavpils.

Jelgava
FT of NDH [1] is held between the middle and index fingers of [3], PO > NDS, FO diagonally away. NDH is held still and DH twists twice while remaining in contact with NDH. To see an animation of this sign, go to Latviesu Zimju Valodas Vardnica: Daugavpils.

Kurzeme
[B], PO down, FO away, hand moves slightly side to side twice, then FO is changed to > NDS, then handshape changes to [B], PO down, FO > NDS, and moves in a horizontal circle. To see an animation of this sign, go to Latviesu Zimju Valodas Vardnica: Jelgava.

Latgale
[A], PO > signer, FO > NDS, moves up and down twice slightly, bending at the elbow, then handshape changes to [B], PO down, and moves in a horizontal circle. To see an animation of this sign, go to Latviesu Zimju Valodas Vardnica: Latgale.

Riga
[1], PO away, FO > NDS, twists to [L], PO > signer, FO > NDS.1 To see an animation of this sign, go to Latviesu Zimju Valodas Vardnica: Riga.

Vacija
[F], PO away, FO up, moves slightly to DS twice. To see an animation of this sign, go to Latviesu Zimju Valodas Vardnica: Vacija.

Vidzeme
[A], PO > NDS, FO away, is placed in the palm of [B], PO up, FO away, then DH handshape changes to [B], PO down, FO > NDS, and moves in a horizontal circle. To see an animation of this sign, go to Latviesu Zimju Valodas Vardnica: Vidzeme.

Zemgale
[B], PO down, FO > NDS, moves down, bending at the elbow, then moves in a horizontal circle. To see an animation of this sign, go to Latviesu Zimju Valodas Vardnica: Zemgale.
