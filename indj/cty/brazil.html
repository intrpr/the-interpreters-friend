Explanation of glossing system used
(to understand how I describe the signs in this dictionary)

Alagoas (state)
Start with [bO] at corner of mouth and rub index finger against thumb. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "A" and then "ALAGOAS".

Amapá (state)
[A], PO > NDS, FO away, touches NDS corner of mouth and then DS corner. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "A" and then "AMAPÁ.

Amazonas (state)
[5], PO down, FO > NDS, thumb is held against NDS temple and runs along forehead > DS while each finger closes into a fist, until the handshape is an [A] by the time it arrives at the DS temple. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "A" and then "AMAZONAS".

Aracajú
(2h)[bC], POs ><, FOs up, DH is above NDH and taps its thumb against the top (index finger) of NDH. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "A" and then "ARACAJÚ".

Bahia (state)
[5:] taps FT against chest. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "B" and then "BAHIA".

Belo Horizonte
[B], PO away, FO up, twists to [K], PO > NDS, FO up To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "B" and then "BELO HORIZONTE".

Brasília
(2h)[L], POs ><, FOs up, FTs touch, separate and close to (2h)[bO] twice. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "B" and then "BRASÍLIA".

Curitiba
Tap top of head with palm of [B], then form a [C], PO > NDS, FO up. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "C" and then "CURITIBA".

Florianópolis
(2h)[V dot], FT touch and hands separate with a wg. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "F" and then "FLORIANÓPOLIS ".

Fortaleza
[P] touches cheek with middle finger and that finger only strokes cheek. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "F" and then "FORTALEZA".

Goiânia
[1], PO away, FO up, twists at wrist. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "G" and then "GOIÂNIA".

Goiás (state)
[1], PO away, FO up, twists at wrist. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "G" and then "GOIÁS".

João Pessoa
"J" + [P], PO down, touches cheek. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "J" and then "JOÃO PESSOA".

Maceió
[[bO], touches DS corner of mouth and index finger rubs against thumb so that the handshape alternates between [bO] and [A]. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "M" and then "MACEIÓ ".

Manaus
[5] touches thumb to NDS of forhead and then slide thumb along foreahd to DS with a wg until it ends up as [A]. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "M" and then "MANAUS".

Maranhão (state)
[M], PO > NDS, FO up, twists at wrist. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "M" and then "MARANHÃO".

Mato Grosso (state)
[B^] taps the radial [thumb side] of the hand behind the ear. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "M" and then "MATO GROSSO".

Mato Grosso do Sul (state)
[B^] taps the radial [thumb side] of the hand behind the ear + S-U-L. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "M" and then "MATO GROSSO DO SUL".

Minas Gerais (state)
Index finger strokes neck from DS to center of neck. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "M" and then "MINAS GERAIS".

Natal
[N: dot], PO down, FO away, strokes sie of cheek. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "N" and then "NATAL2".

Palmas
Clap hands, FOs up, and DH lower than NDH. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "P" and then "PALMAS".

Pará
[P] touches temple with middle finger and twists away so that PO is away from signer. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "P" and then "PARÁ".

Paraíba (state)
[P] rubs palmar side against the cheek in a circle. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "P" and then "PARAÍBA ".

Paraná
Tap head from above. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "P" and then "PARANÁ ".

Pernambuco
(2h)[5:] tap FTs while hinged at the wrists like a clam opening and closing. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "P" and then "PERNAMBUCO".

Piauí (state)
[P], PO down, circles atnNDH forearm. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "P" and then "PIAUÍ".

Porto Alegre
[O], is held high close to temple and twists at wrist. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "P" and then "PORTO ALEGRE".

Porto Velho
[P], PO down taps radial [thumb] side against cheek + [S], PO > NDS, touches chin from below. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "P" and then "PORTO VELHO ".

Recife
(2h)[5:] tap FTs while hinged at the wrists like a clam opening and closing. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "R" and then "RECIFE".

Rio de Janeiro
[A dot], PO > back, twists abd runs thumbtip down arm. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "R" and then "RIO DE JANEIRO".

Rio Grande do Norte (state)
R-N. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "R" and then "RIO GRANDE DO NORTE".

Rio Grande do Sul
R-S. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "R" and then "RIO GRANDE DO SUL".

Rondônia (state)
ND [B] arm is stretched out horizontally, FO > DS, and [R], FO up, touches the underside of the ND arm near the elbow and then near the wrist. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "R" and then "RONDÔNIA".

Roraima (state)
[R] touches FT to DS corner of mouth and then NDS. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "R" and then "RORAIMA ".

Santa Catarina (state)
S-C. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "S" and then "SANTA CATARINA".

São Paulo
[P] taps middle finger against DS temple. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "S" and then "SÃO PAULO".

Sergipe
[S] is held high near temple and twists at the wrist. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "S" and then "SERGIPE".

Suíça
[G] taps FT aginst center of cheek. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "S" and then "SUÍÇA ".

Vitória.
[V], PO > signer, taps on face so that the FTs are on either side of the nose and mouth. To see a video of this sign, go to LIBRAS - Dicionário da Língua Brasileira de Sinais, click on "V" and then "VITÓRIA".
