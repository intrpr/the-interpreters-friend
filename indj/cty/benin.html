Abomey

Cotonou
[5:] touches FTs to DS shoulder, like BOSS.1

Kingtown
[K] touches NDS shoulder, then DS waist + (2h)[B], FOs up, tap FTs together 2x.1(< the signs KING + CITY.)

Porto Novo
[5:] taps palm against NDH [1], FO up.1
