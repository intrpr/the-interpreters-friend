Explanation of glossing system used
(to understand how I describe the signs in this dictionary)

Chamdo
[1^] circles head CCW as seen from above + [C] PO > signer runs FT across chest from NDS shoulder to DS waist. To see a video of this sign go to tibet_prefectures at time 0:32.

Dagste (county)
Both hands have the shape of [ILY], but the middle and ring fingers are perependicular to the palms and are touching with FT up and then move away from each other. + (2h)[B], NDH points > DS (as in TABLE), DH points upwards, and then the DH curves slightly at the wrist and bows forward (as in AFTERNOON) + Index touches where a ring would be on [S]. The last sign means "county".B> To see a video of this sign go to Tibet Deaf Lhasa Counties at 0:50.

Dulong (county)
[W], PO away, FO up + (2h)[B], POs down, overlap hands and descend + Index touches where a ring would be on [S]. The last sign means "county". To see a video of this sign go to Tibet Deaf Lhasa Counties at 0:28.

Lhasa or Lasa
(2h)[B], DH PO > NDS, FO > away, NDH PO up, FO away. DH fingers rub against NDH palm while DH closes to [A] twice with arms staying in place + press together palms of (2h)[B], FOs up. To see a video of this sign go to tibet_prefectures at time 0:22.

Lhokha
[V], PO > signer, runs FT across top of chest from NDS to DS, then down DS. In the video, the FT seem to go further back the waist as well. To see a video of this sign go to tibet_prefectures at time 0:41.

Medrogonkha (county)
NDH [C], PO > DS, FO away, stays in place while DH [5^] is held higher, and moves > NDS while closing repeatedly to [O^] + Index touches where a ring would be on [S]. The last sign means "county".B> To see a video of this sign go to Tibet Deaf Lhasa Counties at 1:01.

Nakchu or Nagqu
[O^] surrounds the nose. In the video the signer bows her head slightly. To see a video of this sign go to tibet_prefectures at time 1:03.

Ngari
[5] touches palm to top of head with fingers pointing straight down forehead + [B], PO > singer, FO > NDS is held at base of throat. To see a video of this sign go to tibet_prefectures at time 0:51.

Nyingchi or Nyingtri
(2h)[1] cross like an X over the ear. To see a video of this sign go to tibet_prefectures at time 1:12.

Qusshui (county)
[B] moves forward in a wavey pattern like a fish + Index touches where a ring would be on [S]. The last sign means "county".B> To see a video of this sign go to Tibet Deaf Lhasa Counties at 0:39.

Shigatse or Shikatse
(2h)[1] touch above head and trace a partial ellipse that ends at the sides of the head. To see a video of this sign go to tibet_prefectures at time 1:21.
