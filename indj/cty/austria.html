Explanation of glossing system used
(to understand how I describe the signs in this dictionary)

Salzburg
[F], PO down, rubs index and thumb together while moving > DS.1 [< Salz ("salt").]

Vienna (Wien)
(1) (2h)[V], PO > signer, cross at wrists and rapidly change to (2h)[V:] 4x in unison.1 (< eagle on flag plus initialization.) (2) [2], PO away from signer, rapidly changes PO to down by bending at wrist.1
