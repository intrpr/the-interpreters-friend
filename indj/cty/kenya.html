Explanation of glossing system used
(to understand how I describe the signs in this dictionary)


Mumbasa
[G], PO away, closes repeatedly to [bO^], like PRINT, next to eye.1
Narobi
(1) [1], PO > signer, spirals upward.1 (2) (2h)[H], PO down, alt. overlap while rising, like BUILD.1
