Explanation of glossing system used
(to understand how I describe the signs in this dictionary)

Sana (صنعاء)
[A dot], PO away, touches thumb to dorsum (backhand) of [B], PO down, FO > DS. For a film of this sign, click on Sana - صنعاء.
