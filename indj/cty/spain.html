Explanation of glossing system used
(to understand how I describe the signs in this dictionary)

Alacant
[U dot] touches thumb to corner of mouth and wg. To see a video of this sign, go to Sematos - LSE - Alacant.

Álava Araba
[A dot] twists at wrist while outlining jaw from ear to chin twice. To see a video of this sign, go to Sematos - LSE - Álava Araba.

Albacete
(2h)[A dot], POs > signer, FO s ><, touch thumbs and twist so that FOs are down. To see a video of this sign, go to Sematos - LSE - Albacete.

Alicante
[U dot] touches thumb to corner of mouth and wg. To see a video of this sign, go to Sematos - LSE - Alicante.

Almansa
[L] touches thumb to temple and closes to a [O^] twice without twisting the wrist. To see a video of this sign, go to Sematos - LSE - Almansa.

Almería
[1] touches cheek and bends at knuckle without curling, stroking cheek without moving the rest of the hand. To see a video of this sign, go to Sematos - LSE - Almería.

Alzira
[C], PO > NDS, FO up, closes to a [O^] and returns to a [C]. To see a video of this sign, go to Sematos - LSE - Alzira.

Asturias (region)
(2h)[L], POs away, touch thumb to thumb and index to index and hands separate while closing to (2h)[bO]. To see a video of this sign, go to Sematos - LSE - Asturias.

Ávila
[L], PO down, touches thumb and index to forehead and moves away while closing to a [bO] twice. To see a video of this sign, go to Sematos - LSE - Ávila.

Badajoz (province)
Index finger touches NDS of neck and then DS. To see a video of this sign, go to Sematos - LSE - Badajoz.

Badalona
(2h)[5:] touch at top of chest and at bottom. To see a video of this sign, go to Sematos - LSE - Badalona.

Barcelona
DH [4] brushes FT down palm of ND[B], PO > DS, FO away. To see a video of this sign, go to Sematos - LSE - Barcelona.

Bilbao, Bilbo
(2h)[1], NDH touches chin and stay, DH PO > NDS, FO up, wags at wrist twice. To see a video of this sign, go to Sematos - LSE - Bilbao.

Bizkaia
[F but thumb is crossed over the index finger], FO up, twist twice at wrist. To see a video of this sign, go to Sematos - LSE - Bizkaia.

Blanes
Cross is traced with index finger by moving down chest and then from NDS to DS. To see a video of this sign, go to Sematos - LSE - Blanes.

Burgos
(2h)[5^], POs down, move upwards while closing to (2h)[O^]. To see a video of this sign, go to Sematos - LSE - Burgos.

Cabrera (island)
[horns], PO down, FO > NDS, touches forhead and twists away twice. To see a video of this sign, go to Sematos - LSE - Cabrera.

Cáceres
[C], PO > NDS, FO up, moves away slightly at NDS, and then at DS. To see a video of this sign, go to Sematos - LSE - Cáceres.

Cadiz
(2h)[5:], POs away, FOs up, twist at wrists so that POs are down. To see a video of this sign, go to Sematos - LSE - Cadiz.

Cantabria (region)
[V], PO > chest, FO > NDS, hands moves in a wavy pattern from NDS to DS. To see a video of this sign, go to Sematos - LSE - Cantabria.

Castelló, Castellón
[B||], PO > NDS, FO up, moves from mouth and twists down and to the side as if tracing a moustache. To see a video of this sign, go to Sematos - LSE - Castelló.

Ceuta
[A dot], touches jaw close to ear and then chin twice. To see a video of this sign, go to Sematos - LSE - Ceuta.

Ciudad Real
[bX], PO > NDS, FO up, places elbow on dorsal (back) side of NDH, PO down, extended > DS and both hands descend together twice. To see a video of this sign, go to Sematos - LSE - Ciudad Real.

Cordova (Córdoba)
(2h)[bO], POS ><, FOs up, are held at temples and move around forhead until FTs touch. To see a video of this sign, go to Sematos - LSE - Córdoba.

Corruna (A Coruña, La Coruña, province)
[5:], PO > temple, is held above head and circles. To see a video of this sign, go to Sematos - LSE - La Coruña.

Cuenca
[C], PO > NDS, FO up, taps FT against palm of [B], PO > DS, FO up. To see a video of this sign, go to Sematos - LSE - Cuenca.

Donostia
[F but thumb is crossed over the index finger], PO > NDS, FO up, moves away at NDS and then at DS. To see a video of this sign, go to Sematos - LSE - Donostia.

Eivissa, Ibiza

El Ferrol
[O^] taps FT against cheek twice. To see a video of this sign, go to Sematos - LSE - El Ferrol.

El Hierro

Elche

Formentera (island)

Fuerteventura (part of the Canary Islands)
[O^] rests elbow against dorsal [back] side of [B], PO down, FO > DS, and extended > DS. DH moves like the head of duck that is swimming forward. To see a video of this sign, go to Sematos - LSE - Fuerteventura.

Gasteiz
