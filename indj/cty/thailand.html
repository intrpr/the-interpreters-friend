Explanation of glossing system used
(to understand how I describe the signs in this dictionary)

Bangkok
DH [O], PO > NDS, FO up, knocks at base of [B], PO > DS, FO up.1

Chain Gana
DH [S], PO away from signer, brushes up [B], PO > signer, FO up, and when it passes DHFT, extends pinky and bends it.1
